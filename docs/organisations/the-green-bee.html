Organisation Overview

Founded by sisters Brandy Nichols and Ashley Anglemyer, The Green Bee opened around January 2023 as northern Indiana's first zero-waste store, offering refillable household products alongside locally-made, repurposed and recycled crafts, gifts and home decor.

Business Model

B2C refillery and craft retail: customers bring containers to refill laundry soap, dish soap, self-care products, lotions and shampoo; also sells locally-made handmade goods incorporating repurposed and recycled materials.

Circular Economy Activities

Refillable household and personal-care products; locally-made repurposed/recycled crafts and decor.
