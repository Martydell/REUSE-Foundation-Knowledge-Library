Organisation Overview

Pimpant is a French brand founded in 2020 by Karline and Baptiste, manufacturing refillable cleaning and personal-care products made in Normandy. Its signature format is a powder stick that customers dilute with water in a reusable bottle to make products such as laundry detergent, shower gel, dish soap, and toothpaste.

Business Model

Pimpant sells concentrated powder refills (sticks/sachets) designed to be diluted at home in a reusable bottle, drastically cutting the water and plastic normally shipped in liquid products. Sales run direct-to-consumer online and through partner retailers (e.g., La Fourche, Nouvojour) offering bio and COSMOS Natural-certified formulations.

Pimpant's dilute-at-home powder format is a distinctive, low-logistics-footprint approach to refill that avoids shipping water, differentiating it from typical liquid refill models. Still a small, France-focused brand with no published impact metrics. ★★★ Medium
