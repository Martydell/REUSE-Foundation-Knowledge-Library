Organisation Overview

Bout' à Bout' is a French non-profit association founded in 2016 in Nantes to rebuild a regional glass-bottle reuse (consigne) system in western France. The organisation operates what is reported to be France's largest glass-container washing facility, a 2,500 m² plant in Carquefou powered by renewable electricity supplied by Enercoop, with capacity to wash around 60 million bottles and jars per year (roughly 10,000 per hour). Bout' à Bout' works with close to 100 beverage and food producers and over 200 distributors (wine shops, grocers, retailers) across more than 300 collection points, supporting reuse of over 500 distinct container references.

Business Model

Bout' à Bout' operates a business-to-business reuse-infrastructure model: it provides diagnostic and consulting services to help producers adopt reusable glass packaging, manages collection logistics from a network of 300+ drop-off points, industrially washes returned bottles and jars at its Carquefou facility, and resells or returns cleaned containers to producers for refilling. Revenue is generated through washing-service fees and logistics/consulting fees charged to participating producers and retailers, alongside grant/institutional support typical of French association-status environmental organisations. The organisation's stated growth target was to reach roughly 800 producers and 1,000 collection points across the Grand Ouest region by 2025.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Reverse Logistics (collection network of 300+ drop-off points across western France)
  • Deposit Return (consigne-based glass bottle and jar reuse system)
  • Reusable Packaging (bottles/jars reported reusable up to roughly 30 times)
  • Industrial Reuse (large-scale industrial washing facility, ~60 million containers/year capacity)
  • Consumer Education (public-facing consigne collection points and awareness of glass reuse)

No rental, repair, or packaging-as-a-service digital-tracking system beyond the washing/logistics network was identified.

Products & Services

Industrial glass bottle and jar washing; collection-point and reverse-logistics management; diagnostic and consulting services on reusable-packaging adoption for food and beverage producers; buying/selling of reusable glass containers.

Customers

Nearly 100 food and beverage producers and over 200 distributors (wine shops, grocery stores, retailers) in the Grand Ouest (western France) region, alongside individual consumers who return bottles at collection points.

Measurable Impact

  • Washing facility capacity: approximately 60 million bottles/jars per year (~10,000/hour) at the Carquefou plant (company/press reporting, ADEME Infos, 2023).
  • Network scale: 300+ collection points, ~100 producer partners, 200+ distributor partners, 500+ reusable container references (company website/press, 2023–2024).
  • Stated 2025 target: 800 producers and 1,000 collection points in the Grand Ouest (company communications; forward-looking target, not a confirmed outcome).
  • Bottles reported reusable up to approximately 30 times (industry/press reporting) — not independently audited.

Technology

Industrial bottle-washing line at the Carquefou facility; no consumer-facing digital deposit or tracking app was identified as part of the core public offering.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Bout' à Bout' is a well-documented, operating example of regional-scale industrial glass reuse infrastructure in France, with substantial third-party press and institutional coverage (ADEME, regional business press) corroborating its washing capacity, network size, and institutional backers (ADEME, BPI France, Citéo). Its association (non-profit) legal status and multi-year track record since 2016, combined with a genuinely large industrial facility, make it a strong reference case for B2B glass reuse/consigne logistics — directly matching the seed data's description.
