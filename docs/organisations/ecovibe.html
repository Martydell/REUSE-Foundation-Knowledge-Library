Organisation Overview

EcoVibe is a UK-based e-commerce retailer, founded in 2018 and headquartered in London, selling plastic-free and refillable cleaning and personal-care products as an alternative to conventional single-use plastic packaging. Its core offering is concentrated cleaning-product sachets — made from plant-derived, sustainably sourced ingredients and manufactured in the UK — that dissolve in water and are used to refill reusable spray bottles, reducing packaging weight and the carbon footprint of transporting mostly-water cleaning products. The company frames its work around ten stated sustainability principles, including reducing single-use plastic, minimising waste, and eliminating toxins, alongside a consumer-education and community-building focus.

Business Model

EcoVibe operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce model selling plastic-free cleaning and personal-care ranges, with its flagship product line being dissolvable cleaning-product sachets designed to be used with a reusable spray bottle: customers buy a durable bottle once, then purchase low-weight, low-packaging sachet refills repeatedly rather than buying pre-mixed liquid cleaner in a new plastic bottle each time. The company states its UK-manufactured sachets reduce transport-related carbon footprint by approximately 94% compared to shipping bottled liquid cleaner, since only concentrate (not water) is shipped. Alongside cleaning refills, EcoVibe sells a broader range of plastic-free household and personal-care products via its own website and third-party retail channels (e.g., Amazon storefronts). Revenue is generated through direct product sales rather than a subscription or in-store refill-station model.

Products & Services

Plastic-free cleaning-product refill sachets (multi-surface, antibacterial and other formulations) for use with reusable spray bottles; reusable bottle/kit starter sets; broader range of plastic-free household and personal-care products.

Customers

UK retail consumers purchasing via EcoVibe's own e-commerce site and third-party marketplaces (e.g., Amazon UK); zero-waste/eco-conscious shoppers.

Not publicly available (specific named commercial or retail partners were not confirmed in this research pass beyond marketplace listings such as Amazon UK).

Measurable Impact

Company materials state that UK-manufactured cleaning-refill sachets reduce transport-related carbon footprint by approximately 94% compared to shipping equivalent pre-mixed bottled liquid cleaner, by eliminating the water weight from transported product. This is a company-stated comparative figure rather than an independently audited or dated cumulative impact metric (e.g., total bottles or plastic avoided to date), so should be treated as a product-level claim rather than a verified company-wide impact statistic.

Technology

Standard e-commerce platform; dissolvable sachet/concentrate refill format rather than an in-store dispensing or digital tracking system.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

EcoVibe is a modestly sized but well-established (2018) UK direct-to-consumer refill brand with a clear, genuine reuse mechanic — reusable spray bottles refilled via low-packaging dissolvable sachets — and a specific, plausible sustainability claim (94% transport-carbon reduction from eliminating shipped water weight). It is comparable in model to brands like 900.care but in the household-cleaning rather than personal-care category. Its relevance to the Foundation is solid as a refill-systems example, though independently verified, dated company-wide impact data was not found and would strengthen the profile.
