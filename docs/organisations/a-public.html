Organisation Overview

PUBLIC (marketed as "A Public" / apublicshop.com) is an independent brick-and-mortar and online refill shop based in West Seattle, Washington, founded by Kyleigh Turk-Polifko. The store has operated a refill service for home and personal-care products since 2018, alongside sales of curated reusable and refillable household goods. It has received regional media coverage (Sunset Magazine, Seattle Magazine, The Seattle Times, KING 5 News) as a Seattle-area zero-waste retail destination.

Business Model

PUBLIC operates a hybrid retail model combining (1) a physical refill station in West Seattle where customers bring their own containers or purchase them in-store, have containers weighed (tared), and fill them with bulk household cleaning, laundry, and personal-care products; and (2) e-commerce sales of curated reusable and refillable goods (housekeeping, bath and body, on-the-go items, food-storage/food-waste-reduction products, and kids' items) shipped across the US with free shipping on orders over $65. The shop also runs a refill drop-off/pick-up service — customers leave empty containers and collect refilled ones before closing, or the shop texts when an order is ready. A "Beauty Collection Program," run with Routine and Pact, collects hard-to-recycle beauty packaging (4oz and under) for zero-waste-to-landfill diversion rather than landfill disposal.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

PUBLIC is a genuine, operating refill-retail business with a multi-year track record (since 2018) combining in-person bulk refilling, curated reusable-goods retail, and a hard-to-recycle packaging take-back partnership — placing it squarely within the REUSE Foundation's core scope of refill systems and reusable packaging. It is a small, single-location independent business without published growth, volume, or environmental-impact metrics, limiting the ability to assess its measurable contribution. It is a useful example of a US neighbourhood refill-shop model but is not a large-scale or systemic actor.

Priority Rating: ★★★ Medium (verified refill-retail operator with a take-back partnership; core reuse scope, but small scale and no published impact data)
