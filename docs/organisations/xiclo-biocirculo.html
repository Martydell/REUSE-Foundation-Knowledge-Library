Organisation Overview

Biocírculo is a Bogotá-based mechanical recycling company that processes post-consumer PET, HDPE, PP and PS plastic waste into pelletised recycled raw material for packaging and plastic-product manufacturers, partially or wholly displacing virgin plastic. It is B Corp-certified (since 2022) and supplies recycled resin used in packaging, including by refill/reuse-oriented brands.

Business Model

Biocírculo collects and mechanically recycles plastic waste (PET, HDPE, PP, PS) at its Bogotá facility, converting it into pelletised recycled raw material sold B2B to packaging and plastics manufacturers as a substitute for virgin resin, alongside related products such as posts, strips and pallets made from recovered caps and labels. Revenue comes from sale of recycled material rather than consumer-facing services.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Biocírculo plays a supporting infrastructure role in Colombia's circular plastics economy by supplying recycled resin to packaging manufacturers, which can enable but is one step removed from direct reuse/refill activity. Relevance is moderate: it is a material-recovery enabler rather than a consumer-facing reuse or refill operator.

Verification Notes

IMPORTANT: The seed entry conflated two distinct Colombian companies. "Xiclo" (xiclo.app) is a separate reusable-packaging platform for food delivery, founded in 2021 by Ana María Villegas and her brother — it does not supply recycled HDPE packaging and is unrelated to Biocírculo beyond both operating in Colombia's circular-economy sector. The seed website (biocirculo.com) and description ("recycled HDPE packaging supplier for refill brands") match Biocírculo, not Xiclo, so this profile covers Biocírculo; Xiclo should be researched and profiled separately if required.
