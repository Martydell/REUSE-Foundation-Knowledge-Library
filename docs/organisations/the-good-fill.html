Organisation Overview

Founded in 2022 in Nashville, The Good Fill offers bulk refills of all-natural home and personal-care products both online and in-store, using a reusable aluminium case system plus mail-in refill pouches with prepaid return envelopes.

Business Model

B2C refill retailer with a hybrid shipping model: customers order bulk-size refills online shipped in pouches (returned via prepaid envelope for reuse) or purchase in a reusable aluminium case; product range spans dish soap, clay masks, silicone food huggers and other household/personal-care items.
