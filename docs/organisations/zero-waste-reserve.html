Organisation Overview

Zero Waste Reserve is a physical and online retailer, opened in August 2022 by Kelsey Pisel, selling natural, non-toxic and sustainable products for the body, home and planet. Located at The Plex in Bettendorf, Iowa, it sells reusable household items alongside other zero-waste consumer goods, shipping within the continental United States.

Business Model

Zero Waste Reserve operates as a direct-to-consumer retail business, combining a physical storefront with an online shop. It sells reusable and low-impact alternatives to disposable products (e.g. reusable sponges), runs a customer loyalty/rewards points programme, and typically ships online orders within a few days of purchase.

Circular Economy Activities

Products & Services

Reusable and eco-friendly household, body and personal-care products (e.g. "UnSponge" reusable kitchen sponge), sold in-store and online with a loyalty rewards programme.

Customers

Individual consumers, primarily in the continental United States (in-store customers local to Bettendorf, Iowa).

Partnerships

Local artisan suppliers (e.g. Countryside Accents, maker of the UnSponge product).
