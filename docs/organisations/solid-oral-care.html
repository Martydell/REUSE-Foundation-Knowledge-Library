Solid (Solid Oral Care)

New Zealand toothpaste maker with a glass-jar return/reuse program (jars reused up to 30 times).

Organisation Overview

Founded by dental professional Laura Nixon, Solid makes plastic-free toothpaste, tablets and powders packaged in glass jars designed to be sterilised and reused up to 30 times. The company runs a jar-return program and encourages in-store retailer refills.

Business Model

B2C oral-care manufacturer with a returns-based reuse loop: customers collect used glass jars and mail them back via a prepaid courier ticket system for cleaning and reuse (12 jars returned earns a free product); also promotes retailer in-store refill of containers.
