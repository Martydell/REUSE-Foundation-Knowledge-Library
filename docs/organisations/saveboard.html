Organisation Overview

saveBOARD manufactures CodeMark-certified wall and ceiling boards from up to 100% recycled packaging waste — beverage cartons, soft plastics and composite packaging — shredded and heat-pressed. Founder Paul Charteris acquired the underlying IP from the US and opened the Te Rapa factory in November 2021.

Business Model

B2B/B2C construction materials manufacturer: sources hard-to-recycle packaging waste (Tetra Pak-style cartons, flexible plastics) as feedstock, processes it into building boards sold as a lower-carbon Gib-board alternative to the construction industry.
