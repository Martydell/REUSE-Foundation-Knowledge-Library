Organisation Overview

Evy Café is a small, independent café located at Rue de l'Hôpital 31B (also listed under Av. des Quatre Marronniers 28), 1400 Yverdon-les-Bains, Switzerland. Public listings (local.ch, Uber Eats) describe it as a "bar à café / petite restauration" serving coffee, waffles, milkshakes and ice cream. This research could not independently verify the seed dataset's classification of the business as focused on "reusable products" — no evidence of a reusable-cup deposit scheme, refill system, or other circular-economy initiative specific to this business was found in any public source.
