Organisation Overview

Lại Đây ("Laiday") Refill Station, founded in 2018 by Quyen Nguyen, is described as Vietnam's first bulk store dedicated to refillable household and personal-care products. Customers bring their own non-plastic containers to refill items such as detergents, shampoos and cleaning liquids stored in large dispensing jugs. The business has expanded beyond refills into a broader sustainable-lifestyle retailer, stocking over 400 eco-friendly and reusable products, with locations across Ho Chi Minh City in District 2, District 7 and Binh Thanh District.

Business Model

Laiday operates a "3Ps" circular business model — Profit, Planet, People — combining refill retail with sale of reusable goods. Around 30 refillable household and personal-care items are sourced domestically, while roughly 30% of its reusable product range (e.g. bamboo, cloth, and metal alternatives to single-use plastics) is imported because equivalent products are not yet manufactured in Vietnam. Revenue is generated through direct retail sales at physical refill stations, where customers pay by weight/volume for products dispensed from bulk containers rather than pre-packaged units, plus sale of reusable lifestyle products (bags, straws, cutlery, personal-care items) for daily use, work, school and travel. The model targets urban, environmentally conscious consumers in Vietnam's largest city and has been recognised internationally as an example of grassroots circular retail in Southeast Asia, appearing in regional sustainability platform case studies (RKC-MPD, ERIA) and circular-economy networks (CirculAgronomie).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Laiday Refill Station is a meaningful early-mover in Southeast Asian refill retail, recognised regionally as Vietnam's first dedicated refill station and cited in circular-economy case-study networks. Its multi-location presence in Ho Chi Minh City and combined refill-plus-reusables model make it a relevant reference point for emerging-market reuse retail, though it lacks published quantitative impact data and has not disclosed formal commercial partnerships.
