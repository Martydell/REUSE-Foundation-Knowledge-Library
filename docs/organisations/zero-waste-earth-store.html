Organisation Overview

Zero Waste Earth Store (ZWES) is a bulk-goods refill store based in Shah Alam, Malaysia, operating under a "Zero Waste To Landfill" mission. Customers bring their own containers to purchase groceries, cleaning products and personal-care items in bulk, reducing single-use packaging waste at the point of purchase.

Business Model

ZWES runs a physical retail store plus online ordering with local pickup and shipping across Peninsular Malaysia. Customers bring their own containers (BYOC) to refill bulk products such as washing soda, cleaning essentials and groceries; the store also runs a "Zero Waste Heroes" community programme through which donated bottles/containers are redistributed to customers who lack their own. Revenue comes from direct retail sales of bulk and eco-friendly products.
