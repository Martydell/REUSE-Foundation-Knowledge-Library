Organisation Overview

CleanCup is a French reuse initiative operating what is described as the first automated, eco-friendly water fountain system to distribute and wash reusable glasses, targeting campuses, businesses, and public institutions. It positions itself as a solution to eliminate the more than four billion disposable cups reportedly used annually in France, replacing single-use cups with an automated reusable-glass distribution and washing loop.

Business Model

CleanCup's model centres on an automated dispensing unit that both provides a clean reusable glass/cup and washes returned ones on-site, intended to eliminate the need for disposable cups entirely at institutional locations (offices, campuses, public buildings) rather than relying on users to bring or manage their own reusable items. This is a B2B/institutional deployment model — the automated units are installed at client sites rather than sold as consumer products. Specific commercial terms, ownership structure, and deployment scale were not confirmed in sources accessed during this research pass.
