Organisation Overview

Jolly Eco is a Singapore homegrown eco-friendly retailer with a physical store at Parkway Parade (80 Marine Parade Road) selling reusable, non-toxic, ethically sourced kitchen and bathroom products, as well as reusable period-care items (menstrual cups, period underwear). Its stated aim is to promote sustainable living and reduce single-use "disposal culture" through everyday reusable alternatives.

Business Model

Jolly Eco operates a physical retail store selling curated reusable consumer products — collapsible silicone cups, bamboo cutlery, biodegradable sponges, stainless steel containers, menstrual cups, and period underwear — targeting Singapore consumers seeking to replace single-use household and personal-care items. It appears to function as a single-location specialty retailer rather than a multi-store chain or online-first business, though it may also sell online via its website.
