Migros

Turkey's major listed supermarket chain, running pilot in-store refill dispensers for OMO liquid detergent in partnership with Unilever.

Organisation Overview

Migros Ticaret A.Ş. was established in Istanbul in 1954 through a joint initiative of the Swiss Migros Cooperative Federation and the Istanbul Municipality, modeled on the Swiss Migros retail concept. It has grown into one of Turkey's largest supermarket chains and has been publicly traded on Borsa Istanbul since 1991 (ticker MGROS). As part of its sustainability program, Migros partnered with Unilever's OMO detergent brand to pilot in-store refill units, letting customers refill empty liquid detergent bottles rather than buying new plastic packaging.

Business Model

Migros operates as a conventional large-format supermarket/hypermarket retail chain across Turkey, with grocery, household, and personal-care sales as its core revenue base. Within this, it has piloted a narrow refill initiative: an OMO Liquid Detergent refill unit installed at its Ataşehir "MMM Migros" store in 2022, later expanded to additional stores in Ankara and İzmir by 2024. Customers scan a barcode on their empty bottle, select from three refill volumes (910ml, 1690ml, 1950ml), and the machine dispenses product and generates a new barcode for checkout; customers without a bottle can buy a small starter bottle at the unit. This refill program sits within Migros's broader "Business Plastics Initiative" commitment, under which the company targeted roughly 500 tonnes of plastic reduction by 2023 (achieving 247 tonnes in the initiative's first year per company disclosure).

Products & Services

Full supermarket/hypermarket retail (grocery, fresh food, household, personal care); in three stores (Ataşehir, Ankara, İzmir as of 2024), OMO liquid detergent refill dispensing units offered in partnership with Unilever.

Customers

General Turkish retail grocery shoppers nationwide; refill-unit customers specifically at the Ataşehir (Istanbul), Ankara, and İzmir pilot store locations.

Partnerships

Unilever (OMO brand) — refill dispenser partnership, piloted 2022 and expanded 2024.

Measurable Impact

Over 8,000 OMO refills completed in 2024 across the refill units (company/press-reported figure). Migros's broader Business Plastics Initiative reported 247 tonnes of plastic reduction in its first year against a ~500-tonne target by 2023 (company sustainability disclosure).

Technology

Barcode-based refill dispensing units that recognize returned-bottle size, dispense the corresponding product volume, and generate a new checkout barcode for payment.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Migros is a large, financially established public retailer whose OMO refill dispenser pilot demonstrates how in-store refill technology can be layered onto conventional supermarket operations at meaningful (if still small, three-store) scale. The verified refill volume (8,000+ refills in 2024) and named brand partnership (Unilever/OMO) give this profile stronger evidentiary grounding than many single-product refill pilots, though the initiative remains a minor share of Migros's overall retail footprint. Priority: ★★★ Medium.
