Organisation Overview

Village General Store & Refillery is a Roseville, California retail store framed as "a return to the community general store in a modern and sustainable way," located at 500 Vernon St. It sells everyday pantry staples, home-cleaning products, DIY ingredients, lifestyle/home goods, vintage items, and personal-care products, with refill stations for cleaning and personal-care items.

Business Model

The store combines a physical refillery — where customers bring or purchase containers to refill household and personal-care products — with retail sale of pantry staples, vintage, and lifestyle goods, plus online refill order fulfillment per the seed data. Revenue is generated through in-store and online retail sales to local and regional customers.
