Organisation Overview

Ekolo is a Finnish company based in Jyväskylä, founded in 2004 with the stated purpose of promoting sustainable consumption and production. It operates both a physical store (Kauppakatu 18, Jyväskylä) and an online shop selling household, food, beauty and lifestyle products aligned with zero-waste and sustainability principles, including refillable and bulk options. The seed-provided directory URL (mediterrolio.com listing) was not the company's own site; the verified official domain is ekolo.fi.

Business Model

Ekolo operates as a combined bricks-and-mortar and e-commerce retailer, selling curated sustainable-lifestyle products — described by the company as a "smallholder-owned" business — through its Jyväskylä storefront and online shop. Revenue is generated through direct retail sales of everyday consumer goods positioned as environmentally preferable alternatives, spanning food, beauty and household categories, with an emphasis on reducing packaging waste through bulk/refill formats where offered. As a long-running (20+ year) regional retailer rather than a franchised chain, its business model centres on curation and customer education around sustainable substitution products rather than large-scale reuse infrastructure.
