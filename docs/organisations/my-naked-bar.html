Organisation Overview

My Naked Bar is a Singaporean indie brand producing handcrafted, cold-process vegan soap bars, marketed for sensitive skin (eczema/psoriasis-friendly) and sold in upcycled milk cartons to reduce packaging waste. It also runs private soap-making workshops.

Business Model

My Naked Bar sells small-batch, cold-process soap bars direct-to-consumer via its website and retail partners (e.g., Naiise, TANGS), and through Changi Airport gift shops via a Singapore-themed souvenir line. It also generates revenue from paid soap-making workshops where participants craft their own bars.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

My Naked Bar's core reuse contribution is packaging in upcycled milk cartons rather than a formal reuse/refill system — it is a solid-bar (package-reduction) product, adjacent to but distinct from reusable-packaging or refill infrastructure. Modest direct relevance.
