Organisation Overview

Uni is a single bodycare/haircare brand offering 100% recyclable, refillable packaging with reef-safe, upcycled ingredients (including olive-oil-industry byproducts). It sells directly via weareuni.com and through authorized retailers — Space NK (UK), Ulta Beauty, MECCA, Credo Beauty, Amazon, and Goop (US/international).

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer e-commerce ("Uni Refill System" with a subscription/recurring-order tool) combined with wholesale distribution through premium beauty retailers. Products use refillable cartons/inserts so customers replace only the product insert rather than the outer packaging, reducing plastic waste per use cycle.

Products & Services

Refillable body serums, body oils, hand wash, shower products, and fragrances, sold as durable outer packaging plus replaceable product refills.

Customers

US and UK/international beauty consumers purchasing direct online or through premium retailers (Space NK, Ulta, MECCA, Credo, Amazon, Goop).

Partnerships

Space NK (UK retail channel and listed authorized retailer), plus Ulta Beauty, MECCA, Credo Beauty, Amazon, and Goop as authorized retailers in other markets.
