Mobile IoT-enabled refill delivery for home-care products across Indian cities.

Organisation Overview

Refillable is a Mumbai-founded startup providing home-care product refills (detergents, cleaners, sanitisers) via retrofitted delivery vehicles fitted with IoT-enabled refill stations, letting customers reuse the same containers repeatedly in a closed loop; it has expanded to Pune, Chennai, and Lucknow.

Business Model

Customers order via app/online, and a retrofitted delivery vehicle (originally a Bajaj truck fitted with an IoT dispensing system) delivers, refills, or exchanges 5-litre cans of cleaning liquids, collecting empties for cleaning and reuse. Refillable sources raw materials from partner manufacturers (e.g., Rossari, Herbal Strategi, Ecosys) rather than manufacturing itself, and charges per refill with cash or online payment; customers may also purchase durable steel containers for repeated use.

Products & Services

Home-care liquid refills (detergents, floor cleaners, sanitisers, handwash) delivered via mobile IoT refill vehicles; reusable container sales.

Customers

Households and communities in Mumbai, Pune, Chennai, and Lucknow.

Partnerships

Raw-material/ingredient partners including Rossari, Herbal Strategi, and Ecosys.

Measurable Impact

Reported over 10,000 bottles of home-care product refilled (per press coverage, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Refillable's mobile IoT delivery model tackles last-mile refill logistics in dense Indian cities and has demonstrated multi-city expansion, making it a strong example of scalable reverse-logistics refill infrastructure in an emerging market.
