Organisation Overview

Refilled is a Sydney-based startup that installs "The Refiller," a smart drink dispenser pouring over 100 varieties of flavoured still or sparkling drinks on demand into reusable bottles or glasses, targeting offices, co-working spaces, gyms, and cafeterias to eliminate single-use plastic bottle waste.

Business Model

Refilled sells or leases Refiller dispenser units to corporate clients (offices, co-working spaces), who offer staff on-demand flavoured drinks poured directly into reusable containers instead of stocking single-use bottles or cans. The company has raised AUD $1.3 million in seed funding and reports lower operating costs than vending machines due to the large drink variety stocked per unit.

The Refiller — a smart, app/on-demand drink dispenser offering 100+ flavoured still/sparkling drink options for offices and shared workspaces.

Customers

Corporate offices and workplaces, including reported installations at Meta, Uber, Docusign, Google, and Canva.

Partnerships

Installations reported at Meta, Uber, Docusign, Google, and Canva offices.

Measurable Impact

Company reports having prevented more than 440,000 single-use plastic bottles from landfill, with a stated goal of saving 100 million bottles by 2030 (per company/press sources, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Refilled offers a scalable, investor-backed hardware solution directly displacing single-use bottles in corporate settings, with credible major-brand adoption and a clearly stated impact target — a strong workplace-reuse case study.
