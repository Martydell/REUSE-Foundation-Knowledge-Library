Organisation Overview

Fuwa3e (FUWA Biotech Co., Ltd) is a Vietnamese company that ferments discarded pineapple peels into "eco-enzyme," a plant-based cleaning agent used across a range of household and industrial cleaning products. Headquartered in Thanh Hoa, the company was co-founded by Do Xuan Tien, Le Duy Hoang, and CEO Bui Thi Bich Ngoc, growing out of eco-enzyme research that began around 2016 before formal incorporation in 2019. Since 2020, Fuwa3e has pioneered a network of refill stations across Vietnam, letting customers bring their own containers to buy cleaning products at a discount instead of purchasing new plastic bottles, positioning the company at the intersection of agricultural-waste upcycling and single-use-plastic reduction.

Business Model

Fuwa3e's core business is manufacturing and selling eco-enzyme-based cleaning products (multi-purpose cleaners, dishwashing liquid, laundry products) made by fermenting pineapple peel waste sourced from agricultural/food processing byproducts, avoiding synthetic chemicals. Revenue comes from both bottled retail product sales (through retail and online channels) and its refill station network, where customers refill personal containers at a lower price point than buying new packaging, which builds repeat-purchase loyalty while cutting plastic packaging costs for the company. Fuwa3e has scaled its refill infrastructure to over 200 refill points across more than a dozen provinces/cities (with company materials citing distribution reach across dozens of provinces nationwide, including Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City and Da Nang), and has received recognition through social-enterprise and business-scaling programs (e.g., HEC Montreal/social business creation initiatives, UNDP feature coverage). The company frames itself as a "social impact business" combining environmental upcycling (converting food-industry waste into usable product) with a refill/reuse distribution model rather than a purely product-focused packaged-goods company.

Products & Services

Eco-enzyme-based multi-purpose cleaners, dishwashing liquid, and laundry cleaning products; a refill station network for bring-your-own-container purchasing at discounted prices; retail bottled products through standard distribution channels.

Customers

Individual consumers across Vietnam (retail and refill-station customers), reached through partner retail outlets and refill points concentrated in major cities including Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, and Da Nang, plus smaller provincial cities.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Fuwa3e is a strong, verifiable example of a refill-systems business paired with agricultural-waste upcycling, with a genuinely large operating footprint (200+ refill points) for a company of its size and credible third-party press/program coverage (UNDP, social-business-creation programs). Its dual environmental angle — converting food-industry waste into product inputs while displacing single-use plastic bottles through refill — aligns well with REUSE Foundation priorities.
