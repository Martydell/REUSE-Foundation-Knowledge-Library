Touch the Toes

Singapore's first sustainable yoga apparel and equipment store.

Organisation Overview

Founded in 2011 by Eliza, Kelly and Wuen, Touch The Toes was Singapore's (and reportedly Asia's) first sustainably-produced yoga gear retailer, selling eco-friendly mats, props and apparel made from organic cotton, recycled and upcycled materials, and bamboo blends; it added a community studio/workshop space in 2017.

Business Model

B2C specialty retail: curates and sells sustainably-made yoga apparel, mats and props through retail outlets (e.g. The Green Collective @Funan, Isetan Scotts) and online, supplemented by studio workshops and green-living community events.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Consumer Education
  • Other (sustainable/recycled-material apparel, not reuse infrastructure)

Products & Services

Eco-friendly yoga mats, cork props, bags, water bottles, and apparel made from organic/recycled/upcycled materials; yoga and sustainability workshops.

Customers

Singapore-based yoga practitioners and eco-conscious consumers.

Partnerships

Retail presence within The Green Collective (Funan) and Isetan Scotts.

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A long-running sustainable apparel retailer with genuine material-consciousness credentials, but its core activity is materials substitution and retail rather than reuse/refill systems — limited direct relevance to the REUSE Foundation's focus. ★★ Low
