Organisation Overview

Paraguay Sin Basura is an environmental foundation working to reduce consumer waste polluting Paraguay's waterways and natural spaces, sharing reduction/recycling knowledge and connecting citizens, companies and government with circular-economy initiatives.

Business Model

As a non-profit foundation, Paraguay Sin Basura is grant- and donor-funded (e.g., support from the Levine Family Foundation), running public awareness campaigns such as a localized "Julio Sin Plástico" (Plastic Free July) and providing recycling guidance and resources to consumers, companies and policymakers.

Products & Services

Public awareness campaigns (e.g., Plastic Free July), recycling/reduction educational resources, and links to local circular-economy initiatives.
