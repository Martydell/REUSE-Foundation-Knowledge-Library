Organisation Overview

Netpak Ambalaj, incorporated in 1998 and headquartered in Istanbul, is a leading Turkish manufacturer of nonwoven bags, woven bags, ultrasonic nonwoven bags and laminated eco-friendly bags. It holds a leading domestic market position and exports to roughly 40 countries across five continents.

Business Model

Netpak manufactures reusable shopping and carrier bags (nonwoven, woven, laminated) at industrial scale for retail and export markets, serving thousands of stores domestically and internationally as a B2B supplier of reusable-bag alternatives to single-use plastic bags.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Netpak is a scaled, export-oriented manufacturer of reusable bags, giving it clear reuse-relevance as an upstream supplier of reusable packaging alternatives, though it lacks published environmental impact metrics. Seed data's specific claim of "organic cotton" bags could not be confirmed — the company's documented product range is nonwoven/woven/laminated bags.
