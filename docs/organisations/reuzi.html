Organisation Overview

Reuzi is an Irish e-commerce and education business selling sustainable, plastic-free goods across kitchen, bathroom, health/beauty, children's and office categories, alongside a bulk refill bar ("reuzi Pantry"). Founded by Pat Kane in 2018, it also runs corporate workshops, school programs, and eco-events promoting reduced single-use consumption.

Business Model

Reuzi combines direct-to-consumer e-commerce (800+ curated sustainable products) with a physical click-and-collect refill point and paid educational services — corporate sustainability workshops, school programs, "eco parties," and consulting/storytelling engagements for businesses. Revenue comes from product sales, refill purchases, and service fees for training and events, positioning reuzi as both a retailer and a sustainability consultancy.
