Organisation Overview

Moreloop is a Thai online marketplace, co-founded 2018, matching garment factories' surplus/deadstock fabric with buyers and upcycling scraps into new products — founder Thamonwan noticed leftover fabric piling up at her family's garment factory.

Business Model

Online B2B/B2C marketplace connecting garment factories with surplus fabric to buyers; also upcycles fabric scraps into new products. Won a prestigious Seed Award (beating 1,000+ applicants).
