Organisation Overview

A woman-owned, package-free bulk grocery store in Poughkeepsie, New York, offering 250+ non-perishable food items plus household essentials, personal care products, local dairy and a self-serve craft-beer tap system, all sold by weight into reusable containers.

Business Model

Single-location bricks-and-mortar bulk retailer generating revenue from weight-based sales of dry goods, household and personal-care items and local dairy; customers bring their own reusable containers or use in-store compostable bags, positioning the store within the Hudson Valley regional package-free retail scene.
