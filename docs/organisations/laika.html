Organisation Overview

Laika.bg positions itself as Bulgaria's first shop for Bulgarian-made products of verified natural origin, combining an e-commerce platform with a physical store at Sopharma Business Towers in Sofia. It curates natural cosmetics, foods (including gluten-free items, chocolates, tahini, oils and spices), supplements, superfoods, beverages, handmade gifts and books, stating that products are personally researched and tested before being listed for sale.

Business Model

Laika.bg operates a curated multi-category retail model, both online (with delivery via Econt Express, free above €59) and through a single physical storefront in Sofia. Its differentiator is a vetting process in which the company reviews production methods before adding a product to its catalogue, positioning itself as a trust-based marketplace for Bulgarian natural and organic goods rather than a mass-market retailer. Revenue is generated through direct product sales across cosmetics, food, supplements and gift categories. The seed data describes Laika as a shop with "refillable products," but no public evidence — on the official site, in press coverage, or on social media — confirms a bulk-dispensing or container-refill programme; its core proposition instead centres on sourcing verification and support for Bulgarian producers rather than a reuse/refill infrastructure.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Laika.bg is primarily a curated natural-products retailer and does not have verifiable evidence of a refill, bulk-dispensing, or packaging-reuse system despite the seed description. Its relevance to a reuse/circular-economy knowledge library is therefore limited to general "buy natural, support local producers" consumer education rather than a demonstrable circular business model. It should be treated with caution as a reuse case study pending direct confirmation from the company of any refill offering.
