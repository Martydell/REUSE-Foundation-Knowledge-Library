Organisation Overview

Oasis Packaging Industries is a Lahore-based flexible packaging manufacturer founded in 2012, producing films, poly bags and custom pouches for industrial and consumer clients. Among its pouch formats it offers reusable, zip-lock-closure pouches with hanger holes and loop handles alongside conventional single-use packaging lines.

Business Model

B2B contract manufacturer selling flexible packaging (laminated films, shrink films, poly bags, custom pouches) to industrial and consumer-goods clients, with up to 6-colour flexographic printing. Reusable zip-lock pouches are one product option within a broader flexible-packaging catalogue rather than the company's core focus.
