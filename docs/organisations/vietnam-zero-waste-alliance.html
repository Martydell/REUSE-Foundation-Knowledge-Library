Organisation Overview

The Vietnam Zero Waste Alliance is a coalition of Vietnamese civil-society organisations and individuals — including GreenHub, Mekong Community Development, Vietnam Sustainable Green, iDE Global, Green Việt, Enda Vietnam, and Culao Champa — supported internationally by Pacific Environment, the Global Alliance for Incinerator Alternatives, and Break Free From Plastic. It promotes "conservation of all resources by means of responsible production, consumption, reuse, and recovery... without burning."

Business Model

As a non-profit alliance rather than a commercial enterprise, VZWA operates through member-organisation collaboration, conducting waste and brand audits (2018–2020), community cleanup campaigns, and composting/reduction advocacy, funded via its members and international environmental-NGO partners rather than product sales.

Waste and brand audits conducted 2018–2020; states that over 70% of waste can be composted rather than incinerated. No further quantified figures found.
