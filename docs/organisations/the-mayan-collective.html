Organisation Overview

A San Luis Obispo, California candle-bar retailer selling 100% soy-wax candles in reusable glass jars, with a dedicated Refill Station in Arroyo Grande where customers refill candle vessels and bottles of soap, detergent, dish soap, shampoo and conditioner. Donates 20% of proceeds to causes in Guatemala.

Business Model

Retail candle-bar concept offering a DIY pour-your-own-candle experience plus a separate refill-station location; sells soy wax candles in reusable glass jars intended for reuse and refills bottles for household/personal-care liquids. Revenue comes from candle sales, DIY workshop experiences, and refill top-ups. Small-scale specialty retailer with a charitable-giving component (20% of proceeds to Guatemala).

Circular Economy Activities

  • Reusable Packaging (reusable candle jars)
  • Refill Systems (refill station for candles, soap, detergent, dish soap, shampoo, conditioner)
  • Consumer Education (DIY candle bar experience)

Products & Services

Soy wax candles in reusable jars, DIY candle-making bar, and a refill station for candle vessels and household/personal-care liquids (soap, detergent, shampoo, conditioner).

Customers

Local consumers in San Luis Obispo and Arroyo Grande, California; candle-bar visitors and gift shoppers.
