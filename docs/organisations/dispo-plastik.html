Organisation Overview

Dispo Plastik is a Turkish thermoform-plastics manufacturer based near Istanbul, producing plates, cups, cutlery, food containers, fruit-packaging trays, nursery/seedling trays and related disposable tableware products at reported production volumes exceeding 1,000 tons per month. It supplies business customers through B2B trade channels and international marketplace/directory listings such as Europages and Netetrade. Available public sources — including its own marketplace listings — describe Dispo Plastik's core product range as disposable/single-use plastic tableware and packaging, in contrast to the seed data's description of it as a "reusable plastic plates/microwave-safe containers" manufacturer; no verified public source confirms a dedicated reusable-product line at meaningful commercial scale.

Business Model

Dispo Plastik operates as a high-volume industrial thermoform manufacturer selling disposable plastic tableware and food-packaging products (plates, cups, cutlery, containers, trays) to business customers — food-service operators, distributors and retailers — largely through B2B trade channels, international B2B marketplaces (Europages, Alibaba, Netetrade) and direct wholesale relationships. Its business model is standard contract/wholesale manufacturing: high monthly production volume (reported over 1,000 tons/month) supplying commoditised single-use plastic tableware products across Turkey and export markets. No public evidence was found of a subscription, deposit-return, or reuse-as-a-service model; the company's positioning in available sources is squarely within the conventional disposables manufacturing sector rather than the reuse/circular-economy sector.
