The Green Market (Bali)

Bali's first zero-waste bulk food store, with self-weigh refills across multiple locations.

Organisation Overview

Billed as the first zero-waste bulk food store in Bali, The Green Market sells organic and non-organic groceries via a self-weigh refill system, with locations in Kerobokan and Seseh, plus WhatsApp ordering and delivery/pickup.

Business Model

B2C bulk grocery retail: customers buy glass jars/bottles in-store or bring their own, using a self-weighing system to refill groceries, cleaning and lifestyle products; supplemented by online pre-order and wholesale services.
