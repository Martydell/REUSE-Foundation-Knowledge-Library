Organisation Overview

Grums Aarhus is a Danish skincare company, founded in Aarhus in 2018 by Rasmus and Simon, that collects used espresso coffee grounds from local cafés and upcycles them — extracting fibers, oils and a water-based extract in its own in-house lab — into body scrubs and related skincare products. Products are packaged in tubes described as roughly 86% plant-based, made from sugarcane rather than conventional petroleum plastic, reducing associated CO2 emissions. The brand sells direct-to-consumer and through a European retailer and beauty-clinic network.

Business Model

Grums runs a direct-to-consumer e-commerce operation alongside B2B retail and professional distribution, with the company stating its products are used clinically in over 100 beauty clinics across Europe. Its core sustainability proposition rests on two upcycling/material-substitution mechanisms: diverting coffee-ground waste from local Aarhus cafés into a proprietary in-house extraction process rather than sending it to landfill or incineration, and using plant-based (sugarcane-derived) plastic tubing for its scrub packaging instead of standard fossil-fuel plastic. The company documents product carbon footprint information on its packaging and markets formulation transparency (A-Kolbe certification referenced for clean formulations). Revenue comes from product sales rather than a service or take-back model — this is a materials-substitution and waste-upcycling business rather than a reusable-packaging or refill system.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Grums is a genuine waste-upcycling and materials-substitution business rather than a reuse/refill system in the REUSE Foundation's core sense (no packaging return loop, deposit, or refill mechanism). Its relevance lies in diverting a specific waste stream (coffee grounds) and substituting fossil plastic with plant-based packaging, both of which support circular-economy goals but are adjacent rather than central to reuse-system infrastructure. Impact is unverified at present. Priority rating: ★★★ Medium.

Verification Notes

IMPORTANT CORRECTION: The seed website (gruum.com / "grüum") was investigated and found to belong to an entirely different, unrelated UK-based company selling zero-plastic dental care and personal-care products (toothpaste tablets, razors) with no coffee-grounds or Denmark connection. The organisation actually matching the seed's description (Denmark, coffee-grounds skincare, sugarcane packaging) is "Grums Aarhus" (grumsaarhus.com), founded 2018 in Aarhus, Denmark. This profile documents Grums Aarhus, not grüum/gruum.com. Users should not conflate the two brands despite the near-identical name.
