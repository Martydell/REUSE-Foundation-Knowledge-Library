Organisation Overview

Care by Nature is a Danish company, operating under the legal entity Nordic Beauty Supply ApS, based in Nørager, that sells natural laundry and household cleaning products centred on soap berries (sæbebær) — dried fruit shells containing natural saponins used as a chemical-free alternative to conventional liquid detergent. According to the company's own account, it was founded by a mother (named Camilla on the company's website) after finding soap berries resolved skin irritation caused by conventional detergents. The company's products, sold in minimal/plastic-reduced packaging, are now distributed through most major Danish supermarket chains as well as specialty shops and its own webshop.

Business Model

Care by Nature sells soap berries and soap-berry-derived products (liquid detergent, laundry fragrances, stain removers, delicates care) plus adjacent natural cleaning staples (sodium bicarbonate, household vinegar) via direct-to-consumer e-commerce, B2B wholesale to retailers (e.g., Løvegården, a Danish retailer, is a named stockist), and physical distribution through Danish supermarket chains and specialty shops. Its core sustainability pitch is packaging-volume reduction: the company states that a household doing seven laundry loads per week would otherwise use roughly 25 plastic detergent bottles a year, versus one to two soap-berry pouches. This is a material-substitution and packaging-reduction model (fewer, smaller packages of a reusable-per-wash natural material) rather than a refill or reuse-container system — soap berries themselves are consumed/discarded after a number of washes rather than being part of a return/reuse loop.

Products & Services

Soap berries (dried, for laundry use); liquid detergent with soap-berry extract; laundry fragrances; natural stain removers; delicate-fabric (wool, silk, cashmere) care products; general natural cleaning staples (bicarbonate of soda, household vinegar).

Customers

B2C direct-to-consumer via e-commerce, and B2B wholesale to Danish retailers; products stocked in most major Danish supermarket chains and specialty/organic shops. Named retail partner: Løvegården.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Care by Nature is a genuine, Denmark-based natural-products company with a credible plastic-reduction narrative built around soap berries as a multi-wash, minimal-packaging alternative to bottled liquid detergent, and has achieved real retail distribution (major Danish supermarket chains). However, its model is more accurately characterised as waste/packaging reduction through material substitution than a formal reuse, refill, or deposit-return system as defined by REUSE Foundation's core vocabulary — a correction to the seed data's "zero-waste everyday items and kitchen supplies" framing, which is broader than what the company actually sells (it is focused specifically on laundry/cleaning, not general kitchen supplies). Public information on founding year and audited impact is limited.
