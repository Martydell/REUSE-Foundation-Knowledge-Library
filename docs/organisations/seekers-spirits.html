Organisation Overview

Seekers Spirits is a Phnom Penh craft distillery that lets customers bring empty spirit bottles to designated "refill hubs" (including its own distillery and partner pop-ups) to refill with its core spirits, reducing new glass/packaging demand while also running a community glass-recycling initiative.

Business Model

B2C refill-hub model: customers return empty bottles to refill with spirits at the distillery or partner locations (e.g. Be Eco! pop-up); the distillery also accepts glass for reuse/recycling through the "Pay it Forward Glass Recycling" initiative, alongside closed-circuit water reuse in production.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems
  • Reusable Packaging

  • Consumer Education

Products & Services

Spirits refills at designated hubs; glass collection/recycling drop-off point.

Customers

Phnom Penh consumers and hospitality venues.

Partnerships

Be Eco! (pop-up refill hub host), GAEA (recycling initiative partner).

Measurable Impact

Closed-circuit water system reduces up to 95% of water consumed in distillation.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A small but concrete refill-hub and glass-reuse model in a market with limited formal recycling infrastructure — modest but genuine reuse contribution. ★★★ Medium
