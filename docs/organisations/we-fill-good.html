Organisation Overview

We Fill Good is a local circular-economy retail store based in Kittery, Maine (Seacoast region), operating both a physical storefront and an online shop selling refillable and reusable low-waste household products. It offers free local delivery within 15 miles (on orders $45+) and nationwide shipping ($70+, excluding liquids).

Business Model

The store generates revenue through retail sale of refillable and reusable household, kitchen, bath, and laundry products, plus low-waste "starter kits" for bathroom, kitchen, laundry, and compost use, supplemented by a recycling service and gift cards. It serves both walk-in local customers and remote customers via delivery/shipping.

Products & Services

Refillable and reusable kitchen items (olive-wood utensils), bath/body products (soap bars, dry oils, combs, soap-saver bags), low-waste starter kits, recycling services, and gift cards.
