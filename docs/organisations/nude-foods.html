Organisation Overview

Nude Foods is a Cape Town plastic-free grocery chain founded by Paul Rubin in 2017, offering bulk/refill wholefoods, organic local produce, and zero-waste home and body-care products. It has expanded to multiple locations (Zonnebloem, Newlands) plus an in-store café and apothecary.

Business Model

Bricks-and-mortar retail chain selling bulk staples on tap (oils, nut butters, dry goods) that customers refill into their own or reusable containers, supplemented by a café and wellness apothecary. Sources only organic produce from within 10km of Cape Town, supporting local growers alongside its zero-waste retail model.

Bulk/refill dry goods, oils, organic fruit and vegetables, zero-waste home-care and body-care products, café beverages, and an apothecary of natural wellness remedies.

A well-established, multi-location zero-waste grocery model with a clear refill-first retail format, useful as an African case study of scaled bulk-refill retail. Priority: ★★★★ High
