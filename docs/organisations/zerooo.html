Zerooo

Deposit-return reusable packaging system for cosmetics and drugstore products in Germany and Austria.

Organisation Overview

Zerooo is a Hamburg-based start-up operating a deposit-return system for PET and glass containers used by cosmetics and drugstore brands. Containers are returned via deposit machines or checkout points and reused in closed loops, with some glass containers circulating up to 30 times.

Business Model

Zerooo provides brands (e.g. Sodasan, i+m Naturkosmetik, Speick, Denttabs, Aveo) with standardised reusable containers and the reverse-logistics infrastructure to collect, clean, and redistribute them. Consumers pay a small deposit (€0.50) per container, refunded on return at one of over 800 German and 100 Austrian collection points. Retailers and brands pay to participate in the shared system, reducing single-use packaging costs long-term.

Products & Services

Standardised reusable PET and glass bottles/containers for cosmetics, skincare, and detergent brands, plus deposit-machine and collection-point infrastructure.

Customers

Cosmetics and drugstore brands (e.g. Sodasan, i+m Naturkosmetik, Speick) and their end consumers in Germany and Austria.

Partnerships

Partnered with ALPLA to co-develop a reusable PET bottle for personal care products; participating brands include Sodasan, i+m Naturkosmetik, Speick, Denttabs, and Aveo (Müller).

Measurable Impact

Over 800 collection/return points in Germany and 100 in Austria; some glass containers reused up to 30 times (company-reported, via ESM Magazine/Packaging Europe).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A concrete, scaling deposit-return infrastructure play in a sector (cosmetics/personal care) with historically low reuse penetration; the ALPLA partnership signals serious manufacturing backing. Relevant model for the knowledge library.
