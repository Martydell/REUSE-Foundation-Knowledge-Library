Organisation Overview

Founded in 2021 by Ana María Villegas and her brother, Xiclo emerged from Villegas's circular-economy studies at UC Berkeley and pandemic-era observations of food-delivery packaging waste. Its app connects restaurants and diners around returnable containers reusable 1,000+ times, with Xiclo handling collection, washing and sterilisation.

Business Model

B2B2C packaging-as-a-service: restaurants order meals packed in Xiclo's durable containers; customers request pickup of used containers via the app, and Xiclo collects, washes and redistributes them — replacing single-use food-delivery packaging with a tracked circular loop.
