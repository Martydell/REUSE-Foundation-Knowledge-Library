Organisation Overview

RefillMyBottle is a community-driven global map/app launched in Indonesia in 2015, helping travellers and locals find nearby free or low-cost water refill stations to reduce single-use plastic bottle consumption; it has since expanded to Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, and other countries via local ambassadors.

Business Model

RefillMyBottle operates a free public web map and mobile app listing refill stations, crowdsourced and maintained with support from local "Refill Ambassadors" in each country/region. It is funded through partnerships, sponsorships, and grants rather than user fees, with businesses and public fountains added to the map at no cost to encourage broader adoption of refill habits.

Products & Services

Free web-based map and mobile app of global water refill stations; local ambassador program for expanding station listings by country.

Customers

Travellers and local residents seeking refill stations; general public in Indonesia and expanded markets (Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia).

Partnerships

Local "Refill Ambassadors" network; technology partner Incentro (map/platform development).

Measurable Impact

Reports more than 360 refill stations listed across Indonesia alone, part of a larger multi-country total (per company/press sources, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

RefillMyBottle is a long-running (since 2015), multi-country refill-mapping initiative with a proven ambassador-expansion model, useful as a reference for scaling community refill networks across Southeast Asia and beyond.
