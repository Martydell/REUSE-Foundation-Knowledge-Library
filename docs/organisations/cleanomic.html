Organisation Overview

Cleanomic is a US household-cleaning brand selling concentrated cleaning tablets that customers dissolve in water inside a reusable glass spray bottle or soap bottle, rather than repeatedly buying pre-mixed liquid cleaner in single-use plastic containers. Its product range spans multi-surface, glass/mirror, wood/flooring, fruit-and-vegetable-wash, and bottle-wash cleaning tablets, sold direct-to-consumer and via Amazon.

Business Model

Cleanomic's core mechanism is a "buy the reusable bottle once, then buy refill tablets" model: customers purchase a reusable glass spray or soap bottle, then reorder low-volume concentrated cleaning tablets which are dropped into the bottle and mixed with tap water to create the final cleaning product — avoiding repeated purchase and shipping of pre-diluted liquid in plastic bottles. Products are sold through the company's own e-commerce site and through Amazon listings, targeting environmentally-conscious household consumers in the US.
