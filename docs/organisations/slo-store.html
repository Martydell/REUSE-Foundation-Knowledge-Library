Organisation Overview

Slo Store is a Filipino zero-waste retailer with a physical refill station, selling face, body and hair care, menstrual products, pet and wellness items, and storage products under the philosophy of slow, mindful, intentional living to reduce environmental impact.

Business Model

Online store combined with a physical refill station where customers bring containers to purchase personal-care and household products by weight/volume, reducing single-use packaging. Revenue from direct product and refill sales to environmentally conscious consumers.
