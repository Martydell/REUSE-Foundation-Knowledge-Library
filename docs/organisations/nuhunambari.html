Organisation Overview

Nuhun Ambarı describes itself as Turkey's first organic products store, bringing together organic producers nationwide (under its Orgagen Ambarı brand). It emphasises cloth bags, reusing packaging/boxes, and minimising waste throughout its supply chain rather than operating as a dedicated "zero-waste shop" per seed data.

Business Model

Retail and online marketplace for certified organic food and natural products, sourced directly from Turkish producers to support local seeds and farming. Revenue comes from product sales; sustainability is embedded via reused packaging/boxes and cloth-bag promotion rather than a formal bulk-refill system.

Circular Economy Activities

Products & Services

Certified organic groceries, dried fruit, tea, body-care and pantry products sold via physical store and online marketplace.

Customers
