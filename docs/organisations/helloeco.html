Organisation Overview

HelloEco describes itself as "the first and only zero-waste concept store in Kazakhstan," based in Almaty's Samal district. It sells eco-friendly beauty, home, and grocery products both pre-packaged and in bulk/refill format, encouraging customers to bring their own containers. The store's stated philosophy centers on waste reduction and reuse, and it maintains both a small transactional website (helloeco.kz) and an active Instagram presence (@helloecokz) that appears to be its primary customer-facing channel.

Business Model

HelloEco operates as a physical zero-waste retail shop (with limited online ordering) in Almaty, selling natural cosmetics, household cleaners, groceries, and reusable "eco-lifestyle" goods (eco-bags, reusable kitchen items, sustainable clothing). Its refill model is the core differentiator: customers who bring their own bottles/containers receive a discount (reported as 500 tenge off) on liquid products such as cleaners and cosmetics, and the store offers a small cashback (reported as 100 tenge) for returning glass jars for reuse. Revenue is generated through in-store retail sales of bulk and packaged goods plus refill transactions. As a small independent retailer rather than a franchise or multinational chain, HelloEco's scale is limited to a single city/region, and it relies heavily on social media (Instagram, WhatsApp) for customer engagement and ordering rather than a full e-commerce platform.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems (in-store bulk refill of cosmetics, cleaners, and food products)
  • Reusable Packaging (glass jar return/reuse for cashback)
  • Waste Reduction (zero-waste retail concept, customer-supplied containers)
  • Consumer Education (positioning itself as an eco-lifestyle education point in a market with limited zero-waste retail options)

Products & Services

Bulk/refillable natural cosmetics, skincare, household cleaners, perfumes; packaged groceries and snacks; reusable eco-lifestyle goods including eco-bags, reusable kitchen items, eco-stationery, and sustainable-material clothing.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

HelloEco is a small, independent zero-waste retail pioneer in a market (Kazakhstan) with very few refill-focused businesses, giving it outsized regional significance as a proof-of-concept for refill retail. However, its public documentation is thin — no impact reporting, no verifiable founding date, and no formal partnership disclosures — and its scale is limited to a single store. It is a legitimate example of grassroots refill-system retail worth tracking for regional/emerging-market coverage, but current public evidence supports only a modest confidence/priority level pending further primary-source verification.
