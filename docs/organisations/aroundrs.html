Organisation Overview

Aroundrs is an Italian startup, headquartered in Florence, that provides a reusable-packaging system for food takeaway and delivery. Conceived in 2020 during COVID-19 lockdowns when co-founder Giulia Zanatta and colleagues observed a surge in single-use takeout waste, the company was formally established in 2021 as a Società Benefit (an Italian benefit-corporation legal form combining commercial and social/environmental purpose). Aroundrs supplies durable, reusable containers — tracked through a mobile app — to restaurants, supermarkets, corporate cafeterias, and event caterers as an alternative to single-use packaging.

Business Model

Aroundrs operates a business-to-business-to-consumer model described on its own site as "Reusable Packaging-as-a-Service." Partner businesses (restaurants, supermarkets such as CRAI chain locations in Turin, corporate cafeterias, caterers, and event/social canteens) pay Aroundrs for customised reusable-container packages; end consumers use the service free of charge via a mobile app, providing a credit card as guarantee against non-return, with a stated €10 charge applied if a container is not returned. Containers (lunch boxes, pizza boxes, deli boxes, cups, and other formats) are designed to be microwave- and dishwasher-safe and are stated to be usable roughly 200–300 times before being recycled. The company has received Tuscan regional/EU co-funding (referenced on its site as "POR FESR Toscana 2014-2020") and lists collaboration with Plastiz and local Tuscan institutions.

Products & Services

Reusable, dishwasher- and microwave-safe food containers (lunch boxes, pizza boxes, deli boxes, cups) rented via a mobile-app-based tracking and return system; customised B2B reusable-packaging packages for restaurants, supermarkets, corporate cafeterias, caterers, and event organisers.

Customers

B2B partner businesses — restaurants, supermarkets (including CRAI chain stores in Turin), corporate cafeterias, catering services, and event/social canteens in Italy — with free end-consumer use via the Aroundrs app.

Partnerships

Plastiz; unspecified local Tuscan institutions; CRAI supermarket chain (Turin locations); Tuscany regional/EU co-funding programme referenced as "POR FESR Toscana 2014-2020." (Per company website; independent third-party confirmation of these specific partnerships was not obtained.)

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available as independently verified figures — the company website states it tracks CO2 reduction via lifecycle assessment and monitors container movements in real time via its app, but no specific published figures (e.g. tons of CO2 saved, number of containers in circulation, number of active partner locations) were found during this research pass.

Technology

Mobile app for container tracking, return management, and (per company claims) real-time monitoring of container movements and per-transaction environmental impact estimation; credit-card-based deposit/guarantee mechanism to incentivise container return.

Aroundrs is a clear, well-defined example of a reusable-packaging-as-a-service startup with a credible origin story, a formal benefit-corporation structure, and named regional/institutional backing, making it a relevant small-scale European case study for the knowledge library. However, it is an early-stage startup with no independently published impact metrics, funding figures, or scale data, limiting confidence in its current reach or maturity.
