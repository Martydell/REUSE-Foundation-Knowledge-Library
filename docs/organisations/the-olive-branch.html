Organisation Overview

A health food shop in Clonakilty, Co. Cork, founded in 2004 by Olive Finn and Andy Beattie. Its zero-waste refill offering — bulk groceries and household/personal-care refills — is run through a related "little sister" store/brand called Twig Refill Station.

Business Model

Independent, owner-operated retail health-food shop generating revenue from health foods and community-oriented retail; the refill/zero-waste side of the business (bulk dry goods, household cleaning and personal-care refills) operates under the separately branded Twig Refill Station, encouraging customers to bring reusable containers or buy in-store cotton bags/glass jars.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems (bulk dry goods, household cleaning, shampoo, skincare refills via Twig)
  • Reusable Packaging (reusable cotton bags, glass jars)
  • Consumer Education
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Health foods and bulk/refill dry goods (grains, legumes, herbs, spices, dried fruit, nuts, oils), refillable household cleaning products, shampoo and skincare, reusable coffee cups and canteens (via Twig Refill Station).

Customers

Local residents and visitors in Clonakilty, West Cork, Ireland.
