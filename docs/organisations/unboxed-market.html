Organisation Overview

Unboxed Market is a Toronto grocery store offering package-free fresh produce, bulk dry goods, butcher, cheese and deli counters, plus refill stations for home and personal care products. It aims to be a full one-stop shop for low-waste grocery shopping, in-store and online.

Business Model

Retail grocery model where nearly all products are sold without single-use packaging: customers bring their own containers or rent reusable ones in-store, buy exact quantities by weight, and refill household items (shampoo, detergent, olive oil, milk) at dedicated stations. Also operates online ordering with delivery and pickup, sourcing from local Ontario suppliers.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A solid example of a full-format package-free grocery retailer combining refill infrastructure with local sourcing. Useful as a case study for grocery-scale refill/reuse models, though impact metrics are not published. Priority: ★★★ Medium
