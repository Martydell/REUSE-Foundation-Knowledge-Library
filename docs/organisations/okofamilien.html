Organisation Overview

Økofamilien is a small Danish family business running a webshop for organic, plastic-free and chemical-free household, personal-care and lifestyle products. It ships customer orders using RE-ZIP, a circulating reusable shipping-packaging system, rather than single-use boxes.

Business Model

E-commerce retailer curating organic and reusable third-party product brands (e.g., Organic Beauty Supply), generating revenue from product sales. Sustainability is embedded operationally through its choice of RE-ZIP reusable shipping packaging and cornstarch-based compostable void fill instead of styrofoam.

Products & Services

Organic and chemical-free household, personal-care and food-storage products, shipped in reusable RE-ZIP packaging with compostable cornstarch fill.
