Organisation Overview

MOSSUP (Moroccan Supermarkets Tackling Single Use Plastic) is a pilot run by Dutch company Searious Business with retailers Marjane and Carrefour in Morocco. It combines a PET bottle-to-bottle collection/reward scheme with a reusable deli-container deposit system, funded by the Netherlands' SBIR programme.

Business Model

Two-pronged retail pilot: (1) PET bottle return points rewarding customers with vouchers/loyalty points; (2) reusable deli-counter containers requiring a small refundable deposit, returned and reused rather than discarded after single use.

Products & Services

In-store PET bottle collection points; deposit-based reusable deli food containers.

Customers

Marjane and Carrefour supermarket shoppers in Morocco.

Partnerships

Marjane, Carrefour, Netherlands Ministry of Foreign Affairs (SBIR funding).

Measurable Impact

93% of surveyed customers reported wanting a plastic collection system; program expanded after "incredibly positive" customer feedback.
