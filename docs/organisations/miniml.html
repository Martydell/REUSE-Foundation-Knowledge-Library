Organisation Overview

Miniml is a Yorkshire, UK refill brand founded by Emma, who started the company after experiencing sensitive skin and questioning why everyday cleaning and personal-care products often contain ingredients she wouldn't otherwise choose. Described as "built in Yorkshire and grown from a garage start-up," the company manufactures its own natural (99%+), vegan, cruelty-free products in-house at its UK factory, sold in "forever-bottles" designed to be refilled repeatedly, made from 100% post-consumer recycled plastic. Miniml reports serving over 1,000,000 customers and having saved 500+ tonnes of plastic.

Business Model

Miniml sells cleaning, laundry, and hair/body-care products (94 total SKUs across the three categories) via a refill-by-post model: customers order refill pouches/products online (including a "Subscribe & Save" recurring option) to top up durable "forever-bottles" made from 100% post-consumer recycled plastic, rather than buying new packaging each time. Products are also available through retail stockists. The company has received consumer-press recognition (Independent Magazine, Good Housekeeping, Ideal Home) and operates a rewards/referral programme alongside its subscription model.
