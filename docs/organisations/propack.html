Organisation Overview

Propack, operating as MITOP SA, is a Uruguayan manufacturer converting post-consumer PET and polypropylene into food-grade recycled packaging, including trays and clamshell containers. It processes collected bottles and containers through a proprietary system to achieve food-contact quality, partnering with regional recycler Ecopet/Cristalpet.

Business Model

Propack sources post-consumer PET and PP waste, reprocesses it into food-grade resin, and manufactures packaging products (trays, clamshells, cardboard cases) sold to food producers and packagers. Some product lines use up to 100% post-consumer recycled content while remaining recyclable and, in the case of certain polypropylene trays, reusable and microwave-safe.

Products & Services

Food-grade trays and clamshell containers made from recycled PET/PP, plus cardboard packaging, for the food industry.

Customers

Food producers and packaging companies in Uruguay and the region.

Partnerships

Strategic partnership with Ecopet/Cristalpet (Uruguayan subsidiary of Grupo Envases de México).

Measurable Impact

Achieved up to 100% post-consumer recycled resin content in some final packaging products (self-reported, described as exceeding regional/global standards).
