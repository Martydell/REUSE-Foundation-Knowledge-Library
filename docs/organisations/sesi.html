Organisation Overview

Founded in 2006 in Oxfordshire, SESI supplies sustainable, vegan, cruelty-free detergents and toiletries to independent shops, farmers markets, cooperatives and charities across the UK in returnable 5L/20L drums, pioneering a closed-loop bulk refill model nearly two decades ago.

Business Model

B2B wholesale supply of eco cleaning products in reusable drums: SESI delivers products monthly by van, collects empty 5L/20L drums, cleans and returns them to circulation — free of charge across England and Wales — eliminating single-use plastic bottles at the retail level.
