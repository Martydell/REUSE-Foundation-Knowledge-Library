Organisation Overview

Fill Refill Co is a small, independent, family-run UK manufacturer based at its own factory in Finedon, Northamptonshire. It produces refillable household cleaning, laundry, hand/body and hair-care products, sold in closed-loop returnable glass bottles and bulk returnable containers under its "Washtainer" system. The company was incorporated in November 2018 (Companies House no. 11683937) and launched its closed-loop rinse-and-return model in 2020. It holds B Corp certification, is a 1% for the Planet member, and is Leaping Bunny (cruelty-free) certified.

Business Model

Fill Refill Co manufactures its own product range in-house and sells via direct-to-consumer channels alongside independent refill shops and hospitality/food-service accounts (e.g. Silo London). Its core circular mechanic is a rinse-and-return glass bottle system: customers return used glass bottles, which the company washes and refills (the "Washtainer" system) rather than using single-use packaging, alongside bulk returnable containers for B2B customers. It also partners with Milk & More for bottle-return and wash logistics, leveraging existing UK doorstep-delivery infrastructure rather than building fully separate reverse-logistics capacity from scratch. Revenue comes from product sales (DTC and B2B/bulk), with the closed-loop system positioned as a brand differentiator and cost-saver over time versus one-way packaging.

Products & Services

Household cleaning, laundry, hand/body and hair-care products; returnable glass bottles; bulk returnable "Washtainer" containers for B2B/hospitality customers.

Customers

Direct consumers (DTC); independent retailers and refill stores; hospitality/food-service accounts (e.g. Silo London); B2B bulk container customers.

Partnerships

Milk & More (bottle return and wash logistics); collaborations with Silo London and OMNES; charity partner City to Sea. Certifications: B Corp, 1% for the Planet, Leaping Bunny.

Measurable Impact

Company reports (via LinkedIn) its Washtainer system passed its 100,001st returned glass bottle; donates 1% of turnover to environmental causes via 1% for the Planet. No independently audited impact figures located — these are company self-reported.

Technology

No digital tracking/app platform identified; the reuse system relies on physical returnable glass bottles/containers and logistics partnerships (e.g. with Milk & More) rather than software.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Fill Refill Co is a strong, verifiable small-scale UK example of a genuine closed-loop refill and returnable-packaging system, backed by Companies House registration data, named logistics/retail partnerships, and relevant certifications (B Corp, 1% for the Planet, Leaping Bunny). Its scale is modest (independent, family-run, own-factory operation) but its model is a clean fit for Refill Systems and Reverse Logistics categories.
