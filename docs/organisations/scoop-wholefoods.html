Organisation Overview

Founded in 2013 by Jeremy & Bettina Kirk in Australia, Scoop Wholefoods sells bulk organic and natural wholefoods, sourcing most grains, nuts, teas and oils direct from Australian farmers. Its Singapore operation, a joint venture with retailer Gill Capital, is billed as Asia's largest organic wholefoods store.

Business Model

B2C bulk-bin retail: over 2,000 products (~80% certified Australian organic) sold loose by weight, letting customers bring reusable containers and buy exact quantities, reducing packaging waste versus pre-packaged groceries; operates multiple physical stores plus online ordering.
