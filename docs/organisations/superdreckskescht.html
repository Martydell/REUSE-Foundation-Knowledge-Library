Organisation Overview

SuperDrecksKëscht (SDK) is a four-decade-old Luxembourg state initiative for hazardous-waste management, evolved into a key circular-economy player. Supported by the Ministry of Environment, Chamber of Commerce and Chamber of Trades, it advises over 6,000 companies (60%+ of the active workforce) on sustainable material management.

Business Model

Government-backed advisory and certification body: provides waste-management and circular-economy advisory services to businesses and households, operates an SDK quality label (3,600+ certified companies), and develops "resource potential" concepts supporting reuse, repair and refill systems nationally.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Consumer Education
  • Waste Reduction
  • Material Recovery
  • Repair

Products & Services

Waste-management advisory services, SDK sustainability certification/label, household and business circular-economy programmes.

Customers

Luxembourg businesses (6,000+ advised) and households nationwide.
