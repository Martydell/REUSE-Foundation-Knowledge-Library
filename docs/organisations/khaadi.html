Organisation Overview

Khaadi is a major Pakistani fashion and lifestyle retail brand known for handcrafted textiles promoting Pakistani culture and heritage, with retail presence in Pakistan, the UK, and (recently) the US. Its sustainability initiatives include "The Green Hub" in Mardan — described as Pakistan's first net-zero-energy retail space — where the company has shifted to e-receipts and uses recycled shopping bags in place of plastic packaging.

Business Model

Khaadi's core business is conventional fashion retail — clothing, textiles, and lifestyle products sold through physical stores and e-commerce across Pakistan and international markets. Its packaging-reuse-adjacent activity is limited to recycled (not necessarily reusable in a formal loop sense) shopping bags and digital receipts at select locations, particularly its flagship Green Hub store. The company plans to expand the Green Hub concept nationally, with each location individually designed to suit its local environment rather than a standardised rollout.
