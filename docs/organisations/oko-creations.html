Organisation Overview

Öko Créations is a women-led, family-run Quebec business manufacturing reusable menstrual pads, period underwear, and hemp-fleece makeup-removal pads, all produced domestically at its Boisbriand workshop rather than imported.

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer and retail sales of washable textile personal-care products designed to replace disposables — a single makeup-removal pad is reusable over 2,000 times. Products (hemp/organic cotton blend, 55%/45%) are manufactured entirely in-house in Quebec, supporting a "buy once, reuse for years" model.

Products & Services

Reusable hemp-fleece makeup-removal pads, washable menstrual cloth pads, and period underwear.

Customers

Canadian consumers (direct and via retailers such as Birdie's Room, Lil Monkey Cheeks) seeking reusable personal-care alternatives.

Measurable Impact

Each reusable makeup-removal pad can be used more than 2,000 times (per official product description) — a durability claim rather than an aggregate impact figure.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A credible small-scale domestic manufacturer of long-life reusable textile personal-care products, useful as a North American case study in reusable consumer goods, though company founding year and broader impact data remain unverified. Priority: ★★★ Medium
