Organisation Overview

Teko.ph leads the Philippine chapter of "Refill Not Landfill" (RNL), a Southeast Asia-wide campaign to eliminate single-use plastic water bottles. It distributes free (customer covers ₱95 shipping) double-wall insulated stainless-steel bottles and partners with local businesses to supply free refill water.

Business Model

Non-profit distribution and awareness campaign: gives away reusable bottles (value 500+ PHP) at a nominal shipping fee, recruits partner businesses to host free water-refill points, and maps refill stations, funded as part of the broader regional RNL initiative rather than product sales.
