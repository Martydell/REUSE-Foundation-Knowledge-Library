Organisation Overview

Albo Drinks is a family-owned Belgian beverage retailer and wholesaler founded in 1963 by Kathleen "Bomma" and "Bompa" Van Looveren, originally delivering drinks from a garage in Mortsel. It has grown into a multi-thousand-product beverage specialist (beer, wine, spirits, soft drinks) serving both private consumers and hospitality businesses (cafés, restaurants, hotels) from retail locations in Wilrijk and Aartselaar. It is now run by the second and third generations of the founding family. No independently verifiable evidence was found that reusable packaging, glass-bottle deposit-return systems, or circular-economy activity form part of its business model.

Business Model

Albo Drinks operates as a conventional drinks retailer/wholesaler: a large-format retail shop (2,000+ m²) and webshop selling beer, wine, spirits (whisky, gin, sake), and non-alcoholic beverages, alongside gift baskets, curated wine selections, tastings, masterclasses, and event/hospitality rental services. It also supplies the Horeca (hotel/restaurant/café) sector with wine consulting. The company reports roughly 11–50 employees. No information was found on its official website, LinkedIn page, or in general web search about a proprietary reusable-bottle or deposit-return system operated by the company itself; any bottle deposits encountered by its customers would be attributable to Belgium's general, industry-wide voluntary deposit system for reusable glass (e.g., certain beer bottles), not to an Albo-specific reuse programme.
