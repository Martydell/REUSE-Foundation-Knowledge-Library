Organisation Overview

Chełpliwe Pudeła is a small, independently run vegan meal-delivery catering business based in Gdynia, Poland. It delivers three meals a day to subscribers in reusable containers and bags rather than disposable packaging, with customers responsible for washing and returning the containers for reuse. The seed name spelling in our internal tracker ("Chelpliwe Pudela") was slightly incorrect — the verified name uses Polish diacritics: Chełpliwe Pudeła.

Business Model

The business operates a subscription-style meal-delivery model: customers receive vegan meals prepared fresh and delivered in reusable containers and bags, reducing single-use packaging compared to conventional food-delivery services. Unlike some reuse systems that include collection logistics, this model places the return/washing burden on the customer — containers are reused by the same household rather than collected and redistributed by the company itself. Revenue comes from direct meal-plan sales to individual consumers in the Gdynia/Tricity area.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Chełpliwe Pudeła is a small, grassroots example of packaging reuse embedded directly into a food-delivery business model, relevant as a micro-scale case study of customer-side reuse (versus company-operated collection loops). Its value as a benchmark case is limited by its small scale and near-complete absence of published operational or impact data.
