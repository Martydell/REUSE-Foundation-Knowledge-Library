Organisation Overview

Atelier Rebul traces its history to 1895, when French pharmacist Jean César Reboul opened the Grande Pharmacie Parisienne on Istanbul's Istiklal Avenue, later run by his Turkish partner Kemal Müderrisoğlu and family. The pharmacy's signature cologne, Rebul Lavanda (1938), became a symbol of Istanbul heritage. The modern "Atelier Rebul" retail and lifestyle brand — soaps, colognes, home fragrance and skincare — launched in 2013, built on this 125+ year history, and now operates in roughly 21 countries, including a dedicated Belgium/Benelux retail operation (atelierrebul.be) that the seed tracker's domain reference points to.

Business Model

Atelier Rebul is a vertically integrated cosmetics and personal-care manufacturer and retailer operating under Rebul Holding, a Turkish family-owned company now in its fourth generation of family leadership. It manufactures liquid soaps, colognes, home fragrance and skincare products at its own facility (reported at 45,750 m², with a monthly capacity of 4.5 million bottles) and sells through owned retail stores, e-commerce (including country-specific storefronts such as the Benelux site), and international distribution partners, including a 2019 partnership with Japan's Rohto Pharmaceutical that brought the brand into the Japanese market in 2021. Alongside standard bottled products, Atelier Rebul sells 500ml refill packs for its liquid hand soaps (e.g. Istanbul, Verbena & Ginger, Mandarin scents), which customers pour into existing bottles rather than buying new packaging each purchase; the company states this reduces plastic use versus buying a new standard bottle. The brand positions itself on heritage, craftsmanship and pharmaceutical-grade formulation, and holds B Corp certification.

Not identified from public sources: deposit-return schemes, packaging-as-a-service, or reverse-logistics take-back of empty bottles.

Products & Services

Liquid hand soaps, bar soaps, colognes (including the heritage Rebul Lavanda), skincare, and home fragrance products, sold in original packaging and as standalone liquid-soap refill packs.

Customers

Individual retail consumers, purchasing via Atelier Rebul's own stores (Turkey and other markets), e-commerce sites (including the Benelux storefront), and through international retail partners such as Rohto Pharmaceutical's distribution network in Japan.

Partnerships

Rohto Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. (Japan) — strategic partnership since 2019, enabling Atelier Rebul's entry into the Japanese market in 2021.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Atelier Rebul is a long-established, credible cosmetics manufacturer with genuine heritage (1895) and a modern refill-pack offering for its liquid soaps, giving it a real but narrow reuse footprint within a much larger conventional retail business. Its relevance to the REUSE Foundation library is moderate: the refill packs are a concrete, verifiable circular-packaging practice, but they cover only one product line within a large multi-country cosmetics business, and no independent impact data (volumes, plastic avoided) could be verified. The seed data's country tag ("Turkey") was correct; the note flagging the .be domain as a possible red herring was well-founded and is resolved here — Atelier Rebul is Turkish-headquartered, and the Belgian site is a regional retail storefront.
