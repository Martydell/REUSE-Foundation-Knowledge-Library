Organisation Overview

Founded in 2005 by Marie-Eve Lejour and Sébastien Boismenu, this Quebec brand handmakes natural soaps and body-care products with mostly organic ingredients. Its "zéro déchet" (zero waste) line includes body butter, solid dish soap, face soap and serums sold without plastic packaging.

Business Model

B2C artisanal manufacturing and wholesale distribution: solid, package-free personal-care products sold direct and through ~220 points of sale across Quebec, targeting customers seeking natural, minimal-packaging alternatives to liquid/bottled products.
