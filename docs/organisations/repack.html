Organisation Overview

RePack, founded in Helsinki in 2011 by Jonne Hellgren, Juha Mäkelä, and Petri Piirainen, provides reusable, foldable delivery packaging for e-commerce and re-commerce brands. Inspired by Finland's bottle deposit system, it launched its return service in 2013 and won Finland's Fennia Prize design award in 2014.

Business Model

RePack rents durable, foldable delivery bags (made from ~70% recycled polypropylene) to e-commerce retailers as a packaging-as-a-service subscription; customers return used packaging postage-free for cleaning and redistribution. Each RePack bag can be reused at least 20 times, cutting the production footprint versus single-use packaging. The company's current site also markets "ReSend" (reverse-logistics software for trade-ins/repairs) and "ReMarketing" services alongside its core reusable packaging, reflecting an apparent expansion toward refurbished-tech reverse logistics.
