Organisation Overview

Founded in 2019 by Mahira in London, Spruce makes natural, non-toxic, zero-waste cleaning and laundry products. Customers buy a reusable spray bottle once and refill endlessly with plastic-free refills, and the brand is sold in both UK and US markets.

Business Model

B2C refillable cleaning-products brand: reusable aluminium/spray bottles purchased once, refilled with concentrated plastic-free formula refills sent by post or bought in-store, reducing repeat plastic bottle purchases; Certified B Corp status underpins broader sustainability positioning.
