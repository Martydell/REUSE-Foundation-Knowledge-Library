Organisation Overview

Eco Refill Station serves the Mandurah region of Western Australia (Shoalwater, Safety Bay, Mundijong and surrounds), offering natural household cleaning and body-care refills with a BYOC model; the business was previously run by a founder named Kate and has since changed to new ownership continuing the same model.

Business Model

B2C refill delivery/service: customers refill household cleaner and body-care containers via a local refill network across Western Australian suburbs, rather than a single fixed storefront location.
