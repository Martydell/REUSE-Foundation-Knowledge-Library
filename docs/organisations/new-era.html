Organisation Overview

New ERA is the leading EU trade association representing businesses across the reuse value chain — manufacturing, logistics, washing, digital tracking and service provision. Founded in 2022 by nine pioneering members, it now represents over 80 businesses and associations promoting refillable and reusable packaging in the circular economy.

Business Model

As a non-profit membership association, New ERA is funded by member dues from businesses spanning HORECA, FMCG, e-commerce and transport packaging sectors. It does not sell products; its value proposition is policy advocacy, technical expertise, and coordination to help the EU establish infrastructure, incentives and rules that accelerate the shift from single-use to reusable packaging systems.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

As the principal EU-level trade body for reuse packaging, New ERA is a key convening and policy actor shaping the regulatory environment for reuse across Europe, though it does not itself operate reuse systems. Priority: ★★★★ High
