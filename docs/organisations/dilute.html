Organisation Overview

Dilute is a Nordic cleaning-products brand that sells concentrated cleaning solutions in paper-based, water-soluble sachets designed to be dissolved into a reusable spray bottle rather than shipped as a pre-mixed liquid in a new plastic bottle each time. The brand is operated by EkoEkoShop Trading Oy, based in Nokia, Finland, and offers three core product lines — Kitchen Cleaner, All-Purpose Cleaner and Bathroom Cleaner — sold as starter kits (bottle plus refill sachets) or standalone refill packs. Products are marketed as 100% biodegradable and suitable for a range of surfaces. Note: verified sources found no connection between this brand and Humdakin (a separate, unrelated Danish cleaning-products company); the seed data's country and brand-affiliation claims for "Dilute" could not be confirmed and appear to be incorrect.

Business Model

Dilute operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce model via diluteshop.com, selling reusable spray bottles paired with refill sachets containing concentrated cleaning powder/liquid that the customer dissolves in water at home. This "dilute-at-home" model shifts the shipped product from mostly water (as in a pre-mixed spray bottle) to a lightweight, low-volume paper sachet, reducing shipping weight/emissions and eliminating the need to buy a new plastic bottle with every purchase — the same bottle is reused indefinitely and only the sachet refill is repurchased. Revenue comes from initial starter-kit sales (bottle + refills) followed by repeat refill-pack purchases, a classic refill/subscription-adjacent consumer model. The company emphasises European manufacturing and biodegradable ingredients as part of its sustainability positioning.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems — paper-sachet concentrate refills designed to be added to a reusable spray bottle
  • Reusable Packaging — single reusable bottle intended for repeated long-term use
  • Waste Reduction — eliminates repeat single-use plastic bottle purchases and reduces shipping volume/weight
  • Consumer Education — on-site messaging about reducing plastic and water waste in cleaning products

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Dilute is a small but clearly reuse-aligned consumer brand: its core product design (reusable bottle + lightweight refill sachet) directly reduces single-use plastic packaging and shipping-related waste, matching the REUSE Foundation's refill-systems focus. However, it is an early-stage, single-brand e-commerce operation with no independently published impact data and limited public information on scale, founding date or reach beyond its own website.
