Global hard-to-recycle waste specialist behind the Loop reusable-packaging platform.

Organisation Overview

Founded in 2001 by Tom Szaky, TerraCycle turns hard-to-recycle pre- and post-consumer waste into raw materials. In 2019 it launched Loop, a global circular shopping platform delivering products in durable containers collected, cleaned and reused (sometimes 100+ times), partnering with P&G, Coca-Cola, Carrefour, Tesco, Nestlé and others.

Business Model

Dual model: (1) TerraCycle's core recycling programmes accept hard-to-recycle waste streams from consumers/brands for material recovery; (2) Loop is a separate circular e-commerce platform where major consumer-goods brands package products in durable, reusable containers that Loop's logistics collect, clean and refill.

Measurable Impact

Loop containers designed for 100+ reuse cycles; global multi-brand, multi-retailer rollout since 2019.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A foundational global player whose Loop platform is one of the most prominent large-scale reusable-packaging systems, backed by major FMCG brands and retailers — essential reuse infrastructure. ★★★★★ Essential
