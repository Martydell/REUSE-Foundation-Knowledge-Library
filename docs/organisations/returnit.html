Organisation Overview

RETURNiT is a Seoul-based platform operated by itgreen Co., Ltd. that lets customers order food through major Korean delivery apps (e.g., Coupang Eats, Naver Order) delivered in reusable stainless-steel containers instead of single-use packaging, with containers collected, sanitized, and recirculated.

Business Model

RETURNiT partners with restaurants and merchants to adopt reusable stainless-steel containers, integrating with major food delivery platforms. The company operates cleaning centers that perform a seven-stage sanitization process on returned containers before redistribution, and also offers B2B corporate delivery solutions. Revenue is generated through merchant partnerships, platform integration, and container logistics/cleaning services.

Products & Services

Reusable stainless-steel food delivery containers; container collection, seven-stage sanitization, and redistribution; delivery-platform integration; B2B corporate delivery solutions.

Customers

Restaurants and food merchants on delivery platforms (Coupang Eats, Naver Order, etc.); corporate clients; end consumers ordering delivery food in Seoul.

Partnerships

Integrates with major Korean delivery platforms including Coupang Eats and Naver Order.

Measurable Impact

Company states one reusable container replaces over 300 single-use containers.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A strong example of reusable packaging integrated directly into mainstream food-delivery infrastructure at platform scale, with a credible reuse-multiple claim. Founding date and independently verified impact data are not yet available.
