Organisation Overview

Multi Pack, established 2007, is described as Pakistan's leading manufacturer/exporter of industrial plastic products — crates, pallets, buckets, garbage bins, drums/jerry cans — serving food, agri, dairy, textile, and auto-parts manufacturers.

Business Model

Manufactures durable, stackable industrial plastic crates/pallets/buckets on high-precision injection molding machines, sold to B2B industrial clients across multiple sectors; also does PP (polypropylene) recycling.

Plastic pallets, crates/baskets (stackable/non-stackable), buckets, garbage bins, drums, jerry cans, flexible packaging.
