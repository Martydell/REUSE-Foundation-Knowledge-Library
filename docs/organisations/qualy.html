Organisation Overview

Qualy is a Thai product-design brand launched in 2004 by the Suppametheekulwat family, whose original business was an OEM plastics manufacturer (Saint Louis Plas-Mold, est. 1977). Qualy designs household goods intended to reduce waste and encourage sorting/reuse behaviour, sold globally.

Business Model

Qualy designs and manufactures plastic homeware (storage, organisation, waste-sorting bins) under its own brand, sold via its e-commerce site and international distributors/retailers. Revenue comes from direct-to-consumer and wholesale product sales rather than a service model; sustainability is embedded in product design (stackability, recycled material use, longevity) rather than a reuse-logistics system.

Circular Economy Activities

Products & Services

Stackable storage containers, food-storage sets, and modular recycling/waste-sorting bins (e.g. Block 20/Block 30 series) designed for home organisation and waste separation.

Customers

Households and retailers worldwide via international distribution and Qualy's own online shop.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A design-led consumer goods brand with genuine eco-design intent, but it sells single-purchase plastic products rather than operating a reuse/refill/deposit system — its circularity contribution is indirect (durability, recycled content, waste-sorting encouragement). Priority: ★★ Low
