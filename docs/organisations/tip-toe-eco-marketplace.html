Organisation Overview

TipToe Eco Marketplace is a community-hub storefront in Kennebunk, Maine, hosting over 40 local Maine makers and consignors selling plastic-free gifts, art, home and body-care products, alongside a full home-and-body refill bar and a "ReLove" section for previously-owned goods.

Business Model

Consignment-marketplace model: local artisans and eco-brands consign goods for sale in a shared retail space, supplemented by an in-house refill bar for body and home-care products and a secondhand ("ReLove") resale section — generating revenue via consignment fees/commission plus direct refill sales.
