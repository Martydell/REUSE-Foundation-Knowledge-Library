Organisation Overview

BAK Packaging (Pvt) Ltd. is a flexible packaging manufacturer based in Lahore, Pakistan, founded in 2019. The company provides printing, lamination and bag-making services, producing center-seal, 3-side-seal, 4-side-seal and flat-bottom box pouches for business customers, using in-house rotogravure printing and solvent-based/solvent-less lamination equipment. It positions itself as a full-service flexible-packaging supplier offering custom design and production for client brands.

Business Model

BAK Packaging operates a B2B manufacturing model, producing custom flexible packaging — primarily pouches and bags in various seal configurations, including resealable formats — for other businesses' branded products, rather than selling to end consumers directly. The company controls the full production chain in-house: design and development according to customer specification, rotogravure printing, solvent-based and solvent-less lamination, and bag-making on its own equipment, supported by an in-house testing laboratory for quality control. The seed data describes BAK's focus as "reusable/resealable pouches"; the company's own website confirms resealable pouch formats among its product range but frames its core business more broadly as flexible packaging manufacturing generally (covering food, and by extension other, packaging needs) rather than marketing itself specifically as a reuse-oriented or sustainability-branded packaging company. No public information was found describing take-back, refill or closed-loop reuse programmes tied to BAK's pouches; the "reusable" framing in the seed data appears to refer to resealable (multi-use-per-pack) closures rather than a formal reuse/return system.
