Organisation Overview

Schoeller Allibert formed in 2012–2013 through the merger of LINPAC Allibert and Schoeller Arca Systems, tracing its reusable-crate lineage back to 1960. It is the world's largest manufacturer of reusable plastic containers, crates and pallets for materials handling, merging into "IPL Schoeller" in 2025.

Business Model

B2B manufacturer and lessor of reusable plastic transport packaging (foldable large containers, crates, pallets, battery shipping boxes) sold and rented globally to retail, food & beverage, automotive and industrial supply chains as a durable alternative to single-use packaging.
