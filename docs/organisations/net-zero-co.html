Organisation Overview

Net Zero Co. is a US brand selling reusable, non-toxic household products, founded after its creators were prompted by concern over plastic straw waste. In 2023, Tru Earth acquired Net Zero Co., combining it with Basic Goods Trading Co. to form the Eco-Collective online marketplace.

Business Model

Net Zero Co. sells reusable household, kitchen and self-care products (e.g., stainless-steel straws, grocery bags) direct-to-consumer online, packaged in recyclable materials the company encourages customers to reuse, compost or recycle, with plastic-free shipping. It also runs a wholesale/retailer program for other businesses to stock its products.
