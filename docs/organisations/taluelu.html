Organisation Overview

Taluelu is an Estonian farm-based producer of handmade zero-waste household and personal-care items, referenced within Estonia's low-waste-living resource networks alongside organisations like Zero Waste Estonia SA.

Business Model

B2C artisanal manufacturer: produces reusable household and personal-care items on a working farm, sold direct or through Estonian sustainable-living retail networks, positioned within the country's broader zero-waste movement rather than operating a dedicated refill/return system.
