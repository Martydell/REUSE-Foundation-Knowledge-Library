Organisation Overview

Zero Store is a UK zero-waste retailer founded by "Charlie," who began the project in autumn 2018 and opened a shop in Leamington Spa in November 2019, followed by a second store in Stratford-upon-Avon in June 2022. It sells package-free dried goods, eco-cleaning refills, and plastic-free toiletries and household goods both in-store and online.

Business Model

Zero Store operates physical retail shops plus an online store and click-and-collect/concession points across Warwickshire, selling package-free bulk dried goods, refillable cleaning products, and plastic-free toiletries. Around a third of its roughly 80+ products are locally sourced, supporting other small ethical businesses; pricing is positioned to be comparable to supermarkets to widen accessibility of zero-waste shopping.

Package-free dried/bulk foods, refillable eco-cleaning products, and plastic-free toiletries and household goods, available in-store, via click-and-collect, and online delivery.

Measurable Impact

Claims to have "saved hundreds of thousands of plastics from bins" since 2018 (unquantified, self-reported).
