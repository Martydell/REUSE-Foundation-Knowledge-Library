Organisation Overview

Enviu is a Rotterdam-headquartered non-profit venture-building studio founded in 2004. Rather than operating a single product or service, Enviu designs, launches and scales new companies ("ventures") intended to solve environmental and social problems through commercially viable, circular business models. It operates across food, textiles, plastics/zero-waste, and mobility domains, with additional offices in Nairobi (Kenya), Bangalore (India) and Jakarta (Indonesia) supporting ventures in East Africa, South Asia and Southeast Asia. Enviu is the parent organisation behind a portfolio of circular-economy ventures researched elsewhere (e.g. Cupkita, Alner/Koinpack).

Business Model

Enviu's model is venture-building rather than direct commerce: it identifies systemic environmental/social problems, prototypes new business concepts in-house, and spins them out as independent (sometimes majority Enviu-owned at inception) companies designed to become financially self-sustaining. Funding for this incubation work comes from philanthropic and impact-investment partners — named sources include the IKEA Foundation, H&M Foundation, and the Adessium Foundation — rather than from selling a product directly to consumers. Enviu's portfolio has grown to over 20 ventures since founding, spanning regenerative agriculture (East Africa), circular textiles, zero-waste/plastic reduction, and clean mobility. As the ventures mature, Enviu typically retains an advisory or minority-ownership relationship while the venture operates independently.

Products & Services

Venture design and incubation services; portfolio ventures spanning circular textiles, zero-waste/reuse retail and packaging, regenerative agriculture, and mobility. (Individual ventures such as Cupkita and Alner/Koinpack are researched as separate profiles.)

Customers

Not a direct-to-consumer organisation; Enviu's "customers" are effectively its funding partners (foundations, impact investors) and the venture teams/entrepreneurs it incubates.

Partnerships

IKEA Foundation, H&M Foundation, Adessium Foundation (funding partners); World Economic Forum (listed member organisation). Additional venture-level partnerships not verified at the parent-organisation level.

Measurable Impact

Company-reported (undated in sources found): 60 green jobs created and 200+ tonnes of textile waste diverted from landfill via its textile-waste venture work; portfolio of 20+ ventures since 2004. No independently audited, dated impact report located in this research.

Technology

Not applicable directly — Enviu is an incubator; individual ventures may deploy their own technology (documented in their separate profiles).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Enviu is relevant to the REUSE Foundation primarily as the originating organisation behind multiple circular-economy/reuse ventures rather than as an operating reuse business itself. Its long track record (since 2004), non-profit steward-owned structure, and credible foundation funding partners make it a useful "parent" reference point when cataloguing ventures it has spun out, but it should not be scored using product-level reuse metrics.
