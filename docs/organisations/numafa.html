Organisation Overview

Numafa is a top-three global manufacturer of industrial cleaning and handling systems for reusable product carriers — crate washers, pallet washers, container washers and layer-pad washers — serving food and non-food industries worldwide from its Netherlands base.

Business Model

B2B equipment manufacturer selling and servicing high-throughput washing machinery (up to 6,000 crates/hour) to industrial and food-sector clients that operate reusable packaging fleets (crates, pallets, bins). Revenue comes from equipment sales, servicing, and full-service support contracts, with offices in the Netherlands and the Americas.

Circular Economy Activities

Products & Services

Crate washers, pallet washers, container washers, parts washers and layer-pad washers enabling hygienic reuse of industrial packaging carriers.

Customers

Food and non-food industrial companies operating reusable crate, pallet, bin and container fleets globally.

Partnerships

Anders Invest (investment partner) and WCA Group, per company news releases; listed on the Reusable Packaging Association's marketplace.

Measurable Impact

Crate washer systems process up to 6,000 crates per hour (per official site) — a throughput specification rather than an aggregate environmental impact figure.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A critical enabling-technology provider for industrial reusable packaging — without hygienic washing infrastructure like Numafa's, closed-loop crate/pallet reuse at scale is not viable, making it a high-relevance infrastructure player. Priority: ★★★★ High
