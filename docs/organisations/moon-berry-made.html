Organisation Overview

The website moonberrymade.com now operates as "Moonberry Mercantile and Trading," a physical natural-living general store based in Tarpon Springs, Florida, rather than the generic "eco-friendly consumer products" company described in the seed data. The shop sells raw dairy, locally-sourced meat, organic sourdough, apothecary items, and craft-kitchen goods, and includes a refillery offering for non-toxic household cleaning products. Public information about the business's founding date, founder, and detailed history is very limited.

Business Model

Moonberry Mercantile and Trading operates as a physical specialty-retail storefront (a "mercantile") in Tarpon Springs, Florida, curating natural and low-toxicity everyday goods: raw/unprocessed dairy, local meat, organic sourdough bread, apothecary/herbal items, and craft-kitchen products. Alongside its curated grocery and household goods, the store operates a refillery section allowing customers to purchase non-toxic household cleaning products by refilling their own containers rather than buying new single-use packaging each time. The business appears oriented toward a "traditional/low-tox lifestyle" community market rather than e-commerce, functioning primarily as a local bricks-and-mortar general store with a sustainability-oriented refill offering as one part of its overall product mix rather than its sole focus.
