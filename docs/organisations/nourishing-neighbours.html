Organisation Overview

Nourishing Neighbours is a 100% volunteer-run charity founded in 2020 serving Moreland (Melbourne) and surrounding areas, providing culturally safe emergency food relief, toiletries and period products to people experiencing food insecurity, via referral-only access.

Business Model

Registered charity with DGR status funded by donations and charity partners, distributing over 1 tonne of food relief weekly through a once-weekly service. Clients access the service exclusively via referral from a support agency, ensuring coordinated wraparound support rather than open retail access.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Weekly food relief parcels (pantry items, fresh produce, bakery, frozen meals), toiletries, period products and baby formula for referred clients.

Customers

Individuals and families experiencing food insecurity in Moreland and surrounding Melbourne suburbs, accessed via support-agency referral.

Partnerships

Charity and food-donation partners (listed at nourishingneighbours.org.au/our-partners/); specific partner names not itemised in available sources.

Measurable Impact

Over 1 tonne of emergency food relief distributed weekly (per official site).
