Organisation Overview

Launched 17 April 2023 by Valorlux, Spin is a free app-based system letting consumers borrow and return reusable 0.3L take-away cups at "SpinPoints" in Luxembourg City's hospitality sector — a pilot ahead of Luxembourg's 1 January 2025 single-use packaging ban.

Business Model

App-based reuse-as-a-service: consumers borrow a reusable cup at a SpinPoint, use it, and drop it into a collection container at any SpinPoint; items are transported to a washing centre in Sanem, quality-checked, and redistributed via a logistics partner — free to use for consumers.

Measurable Impact

Piloted at 15 points of sale in its first phase; expanded ahead of Luxembourg's 2025 single-use ban.
