Organisation Overview

Zero Posro is a Goa-based social enterprise selling seasonal, locally sourced produce and pantry staples loose from dispensers, requiring customers to bring their own containers or reusable bags. It aims to reduce plastic waste in the food retail sector.

Business Model

Zero Posro operates a retail store in Panaji plus a display point at Saukhyam, Porvorim, and products at Taj Vivanta, Panjim, selling grains, millets, spices, cold-pressed oils, cashew butter, coconut vinegar, and vegetables/fruit in bulk (BYOC model). It also runs a subscription service for weekly doorstep delivery of vegetable baskets, generating recurring revenue alongside one-off in-store bulk sales.
