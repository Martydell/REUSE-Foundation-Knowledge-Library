Organisation Overview

Muuse is a Singapore-based reuse-as-a-service company founded in 2018 after its co-founders witnessed takeaway packaging waste while surfing in Bali. It provides QR-tagged reusable cups and containers to F&B businesses as an alternative to single-use packaging, tracking each item through its lifecycle.

Business Model

Muuse operates a B2B model, charging F&B outlets a flat monthly service fee covering the reusable packaging, logistics, washing and platform/tracking software. Borrowing and returning is free for end consumers, removing the need for a deposit since each item carries a unique QR code for tracking and inventory management. Revenue comes from client subscriptions rather than consumer fees.

Products & Services

Reusable cups and food containers tagged with QR codes, provided to cafes, hawker centres and F&B chains under a rental/subscription model.

Customers

F&B businesses and hawker centres in Singapore (and other markets), rather than individual consumers directly.

Partnerships

Operates within Singapore hawker centres and with F&B chains; referenced by sustainability platforms such as Unravel Carbon and the Partnership for Sustainability Leadership in Business (PSLB) HKU. Not publicly available in further detail.

Measurable Impact

Reports a 98% return rate for its reusable packaging system.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Muuse is a strong, purpose-built reusable packaging operator with a clear B2B reuse-as-a-service model and demonstrated return-rate data. Highly relevant to the REUSE Foundation's mission.
