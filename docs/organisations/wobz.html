Organisation Overview

Wobz is a French reusable-packaging company based in the Lyon area, providing intelligent reusable containers (cups and food packaging) managed through its proprietary "MagicLoop®" digital deposit system, along with washing and container-management services. It targets events, municipalities, universities, and office/tertiary-sector clients seeking to replace single-use packaging.

Business Model

Wobz rents out shared, mutualised reusable containers (cups, deposit mats, connected terminals) tracked via its MagicLoop® digital deposit system, and provides the high-performance washing infrastructure needed to run a closed reuse loop, along with customisation and R&D services for scaling reuse deployments. Clients pay for container rental/service rather than owning the reusable assets themselves, aligning with a packaging-as-a-service model. It is a member of the Vrac & Réemploi Network and participates in the "New ERA" and CEC circular-economy movements.
