Organisation Overview

Planet Organic opened its first store in 1995 in Westbourne Grove, London, becoming the UK's first certified organic supermarket. It grew into the largest chain of Soil Association-certified organic shops in the UK, with around eight to ten stores in and around London, and offers an "Unpackaged" range of packaging-free bulk refills.

Business Model

Planet Organic operates bricks-and-mortar organic supermarkets stocking over 8,000 product lines, generating revenue through grocery, health food, and bulk retail. Its Unpackaged in-store range lets customers refill containers with staple foods, reducing packaging waste. The company entered administration in April 2023 and was subsequently repurchased by founder Renée Elliott, saving ten of its fourteen stores.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

As the UK's original organic supermarket with a long-running bulk refill offering, Planet Organic is a useful established retail case study, though its 2023 financial distress and reduced store count temper its current scale relevance. ★★★ Medium
