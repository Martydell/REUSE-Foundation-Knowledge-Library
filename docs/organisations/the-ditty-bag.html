Organisation Overview

Founded by Jason Hine, The Ditty Bag Zero-Waste Market and Café opened in 2021 in Mystic, Connecticut, helping customers reduce plastic use through bulk dry goods, refill stations for cleaning products, and package-free grocery items.

Business Model

B2C bulk grocery and refill retail: customers BYOC (bring your own container) or use compostable paper bags to fill up on grains, pastas, beans, nuts, teas and spices, plus refill stations for hand soaps, laundry detergent, dishwashing soaps and all-purpose cleaners.
