Organisation Overview

Shuangti is a platform company providing reusable takeaway food container (RTFC) services to restaurants and consumers, launching China's first campus pilot in June 2020 at a Guangdong university, letting diners rent containers and return them to recycling cabinets for cleaning and reuse.

Business Model

B2B2C container-as-a-service: campus restaurants rent RTFCs from Shuangti; consumers return used containers to on-campus recycling cabinets within 2-3 days; Shuangti collects, cleans and redistributes clean containers back to canteens, closing the loop on single-use takeaway packaging.
