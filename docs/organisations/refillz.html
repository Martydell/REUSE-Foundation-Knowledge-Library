Organisation Overview

Refillz is an independent zero-waste retailer operating physical refill stores in Naas and Portarlington, Ireland, plus an online shop. Customers bring or buy reusable containers to fill with pantry staples, cleaning products, and personal care items, avoiding single-use plastic packaging entirely.

Business Model

Refillz sells dry goods (grains, pasta, nuts, seeds, tea, coffee), liquids, and eco-friendly cleaning and personal care products (including brands like Sonett and Lilly's Eco Clean) by weight or volume into customer-supplied or purchased reusable containers. Revenue comes from in-store and online retail sales, plus gift vouchers and custom gift boxes. A second outlet operates inside the SOLAS Eco Garden Centre in Portarlington.
