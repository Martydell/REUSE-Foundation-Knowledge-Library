Organisation Overview

Goa-based personal-care brand making gender-neutral, organic, cruelty-free deodorant balms and shampoo bars sold in reusable tins, with standalone refill bars available so customers reuse the same tin. The company reports having tested and abandoned aluminium packaging due to quality and humidity-related oxidation problems in its Goa warehouse.

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer personal-care brand selling deodorant balms and shampoo bars via its own site and Amazon.in, packaged in reusable/recyclable tins; sells standalone refill bars/balms so customers can reuse the same tin rather than buying new packaging each time. Revenue comes from product sales rather than a dedicated refill-infrastructure or franchise model.
