Otrium

Amsterdam-based off-price fashion marketplace that has expanded into garment refurbishment and repair for damaged returns.

Organisation Overview

Otrium is an online marketplace connecting shoppers with end-of-season and excess fashion stock from 400+ brands, founded in 2016 by Milan Daniels and Max Klijnstra after experiencing unsold-inventory problems firsthand. It now serves over 4 million registered members.

Business Model

Otrium operates a dynamic-pricing off-price marketplace where fashion brands list unsold stock; the platform later added a garment refurbishment and repair programme (in partnership with Bleckmann's Renewal Workshop) to restore damaged returns for resale rather than disposal, extending product life within its core retail model.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Otrium is primarily an off-price resale marketplace; its refurbishment/repair initiative is a genuine but secondary circularity feature layered onto a conventional retail model. Moderate relevance as a repair/extended-life case study rather than a core reuse system. ★★★ Medium
