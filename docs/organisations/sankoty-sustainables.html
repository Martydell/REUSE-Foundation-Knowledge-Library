Organisation Overview

Sankoty Sustainables sells handmade and manufactured low-waste household and personal-care goods around Peoria, Illinois, with clean-ingredient, minimal-packaging products. The company also operates a zero-waste refill station inside the Take Two Resale Boutique in nearby Pekin, Illinois.

Business Model

B2C retail of sustainable consumer goods (detergent, bamboo articles, etc.) plus an in-store refill station model that lets local customers refill containers with bulk products rather than buying new packaging each time.
