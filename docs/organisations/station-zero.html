Organisation Overview

Station Zero is described as Bulgaria's first zero-waste refill shop, created by founders Ani and Stoicho and based on Yuriy Venelin street in Sofia. It offers bulk cleaning and personal-care product refills to reduce single-use packaging in the local market.

Business Model

B2C refillery: customers bring their own containers to refill cleaning and personal-care products by weight/volume, avoiding new single-use packaging; the shop operates as an independent local retail outlet rather than a franchise or chain.
