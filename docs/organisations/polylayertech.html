Organisation Overview

PolyLayerTech is an Italian company based near Venice specializing in extruded corrugated/alveolar polypropylene (PP) sheets used in reusable packaging for automotive, pharmaceutical, and food-sector applications. It also designs and manufactures machinery for PP sheet production and welding.

Business Model

PolyLayerTech manufactures both polypropylene sheet materials and the machinery used to produce and weld them, serving B2B customers such as packaging converters and manufacturers in automotive, pharma, and food industries. Its polypropylene products are marketed as reusable (up to six use cycles) and 100% recyclable, positioned as an alternative to single-use plastic and cardboard packaging.
