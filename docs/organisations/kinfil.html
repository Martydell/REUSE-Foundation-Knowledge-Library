Organisation Overview

Kinfill is a Dutch cleaning-products brand founded in 2020 in Rotterdam by Reda Jouahri, following roughly four years of development after an idea sparked in conversation with friends. The company manufactures environmentally-friendly cleaning concentrates — biodegradable, free of chemicals, palm oil, glue, chlorides, and dyes — sold in reusable, refillable glass bottles made from high-quality Italian glass with a minimalist design, reducing plastic household-cleaning waste.

Business Model

Kinfill sells a durable glass bottle once, then customers purchase concentrated refill products (glass and mirror cleaner, and other household cleaning formulas) to mix or pour into the same bottle repeatedly, rather than buying new plastic bottles of pre-diluted cleaner each time. Products are manufactured in the Netherlands from biodegradable ingredients. The brand is distributed through design-focused retail channels (Finnish Design Shop, Smallable, Niche Beauty, Botā), positioning itself partly as a home-design product alongside its sustainability angle.

Kinfill is a well-documented, design-forward refill brand for household cleaning, distinguishing itself through premium Italian-glass bottle design and biodegradable formulations, with real multi-retailer distribution across European design-focused channels.
