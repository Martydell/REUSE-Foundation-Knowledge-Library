Organisation Overview

Refillables Hoi An is the first refillable concept store in Central Vietnam, located in Hội An, offering pay-by-weight refills of cleaning and personal-care liquids plus dried foods and spices for customers who bring their own containers (BYOC).

Business Model

Customers bring any container (or purchase one) and fill it with dish soap, floor cleaner, hand soap, or dried foods/spices/grains, paying only by weight for what they take — allowing flexible budgeting and eliminating single-use packaging. Revenue comes from direct retail sales at the physical store in Hội An.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems

Products & Services

Pay-by-weight refills of dish soap, floor cleaner, and hand soap; dried food and spice refills (rice, nuts, seeds, herbs); BYOC model.

Customers

Local residents and tourists in Hội An, Quảng Nam Province, Vietnam.

Partnerships

Not publicly available.

Measurable Impact

Reported more than 23,000 containers refilled, diverting them from landfill in Quảng Nam province (per company/press sources, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Refillables Hoi An is a well-documented pioneering BYOC refill store in a major Vietnamese tourist destination, with a reported meaningful container-diversion track record, useful as a Southeast Asian small-business refill case study.
