Organisation Overview

Waterpod is a self-service, in-store drinking-water refill system developed by I-Drop Water, installed inside grocery stores with no upfront capital cost to the retailer. The company describes itself as "born in Africa," using multi-stage filtration and UV sterilisation to deliver affordable drinking water as a lower-cost, lower-plastic alternative to bottled water.

Business Model

I-Drop Water installs Waterpod units in grocery stores under a revenue-share arrangement, tracking sales remotely via its "Connected Network Technology" and invoicing hosting retailers monthly rather than charging them upfront — the retailer hosts the machine and shares in refill revenue, while shoppers pay per refill using their own reusable containers/bottles.
