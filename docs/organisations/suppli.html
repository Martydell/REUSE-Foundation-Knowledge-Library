Organisation Overview

Suppli is a circular supply-chain platform connecting restaurants, food-delivery apps, caterers and consumers around reusable food containers. Its software manages container accounting and return reminders, and Uber Eats piloted it (alongside Reusables.com) in Toronto and Vancouver for zero-waste takeout.

Business Model

B2B2C reusable-container-as-a-service: restaurants order Suppli containers for takeout; customers pay a small non-refundable fee (~$0.75/item, not a deposit) covering operations, and return containers within 2 weeks via QR-coded drop-off bins; unreturned containers incur a $15 flat replacement fee.
