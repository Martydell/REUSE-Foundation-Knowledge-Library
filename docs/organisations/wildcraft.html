Organisation Overview

Wildcraft is a Toronto-based, women-owned natural skincare brand making 100%-natural, handmade, small-batch face, body, and hair-care products. It offers refill options for select products (e.g., toners) and ships every order using only repurposed packaging materials, positioning itself as a low-waste, cruelty-free (Leaping Bunny certified) skincare company.

Business Model

Wildcraft sells skincare directly to consumers online and through retail partners (Whole Foods Market, The Detox Market, Well.ca, and dedicated Canadian refilleries such as Pretty Clean Shop). Reuse is embedded via refill-format products stocked at partner refilleries and a company-wide policy of shipping only in repurposed boxes/wrapping, rather than a standalone in-house refill-station operation.

Products & Services

Natural face care (creams, serums, cleansers, toners — some in refill format), body care, hair care, curated skincare routine sets.

Customers

Consumers seeking natural, cruelty-free skincare, purchasing direct-to-consumer or via retail/refillery partners across Canada and the US.

Partnerships

Whole Foods Market, The Detox Market, Well.ca; refillery retailers including Pretty Clean Shop (Toronto); member of 1% for the Planet (2023 pledge donated to Water First).

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available for waste/packaging reduction specifically; company reports 5,000+ five-star customer reviews and media features (Elle, Forbes, Vogue Business).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Wildcraft is a legitimate, well-distributed Canadian skincare brand with a genuine but modest refill offering (select refill SKUs sold through partner refilleries) rather than a dedicated in-house refill programme as the seed data implies. Reasonable relevance given verified retail distribution and 1% for the Planet membership, but refill is a product feature, not the core business model.
