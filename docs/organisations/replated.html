Organisation Overview

rePlated is a Sydney-based company providing reusable "Mealboxes" made from ocean-bound plastic as a swap-and-wash alternative to single-use takeaway food packaging. It began with a pilot in late 2019 and now serves restaurants, workplaces, events, and individual consumers.

Business Model

rePlated manufactures Australian-made reusable Mealboxes and operates a swap system through its "rePlated @ Work" B2B program and direct sales/rental to cafes, restaurants, workplace canteens, and event organisers. Used containers are washed and recirculated; rePlated also takes containers back at end of life. The company provides an online impact calculator estimating containers saved, waste avoided, and CO2 reduced.
