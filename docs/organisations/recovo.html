Organisation Overview

Recovo is a Barcelona-based circular-fashion technology company operating a B2B marketplace where fashion brands and manufacturers buy and sell surplus fabric, deadstock, and production leftovers, alongside a white-label recommerce (resale) platform and upcycling services. Founded in 2021 by Mónica Rodríguez Corral, Marta Iglesias, and Gonzalo Sáenz Escudero.

Business Model

Recovo generates revenue through transaction fees on its B2B deadstock/surplus fabric marketplace and through SaaS-style licensing of its "Recommerce" white-label platform, which lets fashion brands launch branded resale storefronts integrated into their own e-commerce sites. It also offers paid upcycling/design services that turn surplus material into new products. The company operates across more than 16 EU countries with a team of 20+.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Recovo addresses textile overproduction at the B2B level through deadstock resale and recommerce infrastructure, a scalable model for reducing fashion industry waste, though it is more resale/recommerce-as-a-service than reuse of finished garments per the seed description.
