Organisation Overview

Turtle Green Refillery is a mobile refill business serving Caledon and the surrounding Greater Toronto Area, delivering earth-friendly home and personal-care products — including DIY bodycare ingredients and household cleaning refills — in minimal or customer-reused packaging, with routine neighbourhood stops and farmers'-market attendance.

Business Model

B2C mobile refill delivery: customers order online and receive refills delivered to their door in returnable/reusable containers, or purchase at farmers' markets and pop-up stops, reducing single-use packaging for everyday bodycare and household products.

Products & Services

Bulk/refill home and personal-care products (cleaning supplies, DIY bodycare ingredients) delivered via mobile route or sold at farmers' markets.

Customers
