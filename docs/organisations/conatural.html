Organisation Overview

Conatural is described as Pakistan's first 100% natural and organic hair and skincare brand, founded in 2014 in Lahore (sources vary on exact founder names — reported as either sisters-in-law Rema Taseer and Shehrbano Taseer, or sisters Myra Qureshi and Rema Taseer). The company sells naturally-derived skincare and haircare products free from sulfates, parabens, and silicones, positioned around sustainability and eco-friendly packaging, and raised $825,000 in pre-Series A funding (MENAbytes reporting) to grow the brand.

Business Model

Conatural formulates and sells natural/organic skincare and haircare products direct-to-consumer and via retail partners in Pakistan, emphasising ingredient transparency (free of harsh chemicals) and general sustainability/eco-friendly packaging messaging. No specific refill or reusable-packaging mechanism was confirmed in sources accessed — general "eco-friendly packaging" and "reducing single-use plastics" language was found, but not a described refill vending-machine system as stated in seed data.
