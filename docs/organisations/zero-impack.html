Organisation Overview

Zero Impack is a Milan-based startup founded in 2021 that provides reusable container systems for the takeaway and food-delivery industry, aiming to displace single-use packaging waste. It combines reusable stainless-steel containers with a digital tracking app and reverse-logistics (collection, washing, redistribution) service.

Business Model

Zero Impack operates a packaging-as-a-service model: it supplies reusable stainless-steel containers with silicone lids to food service operators, then manages collection, washing/sanitisation, and redistribution of containers. Customers use a QR-code app to borrow and return containers within a set window. Clients include school canteens, university campuses, corporate offices, and cities; the digital platform also provides inventory and environmental-impact (e.g., CO2 savings) tracking.
