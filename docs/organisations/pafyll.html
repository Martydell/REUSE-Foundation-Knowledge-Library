Organisation Overview

På(fyll) is a circular refill service for household products (detergents, soaps, sprays) developed by Orkla ASA with innovation partners Æra, Bakken & Bæck, and Form Us With Love. Customers order online, receive filled reusable containers, and reorder/return via QR code.

Business Model

Customers order household products online; På(fyll) delivers reusable HDPE containers (0.9L and 2L) to the doorstep. When empty, the customer scans a QR code on the container to trigger pickup and a fresh refill, closing the loop without disposable packaging. An independent LCA by NORSUS found a potential 65-80% footprint reduction versus equivalent disposable-packaged products.
