Organisation Overview

reCIRCLE Denmark is the Danish national operation of the reCIRCLE reusable takeaway-packaging system, a "packaging-as-a-service" deposit scheme for restaurants and cafes. The parent reCIRCLE brand was founded in Bern, Switzerland in 2016 by Jeannette Morath and expanded into Denmark, along with the Netherlands, Estonia and Italy, in June 2021.

Business Model

Restaurants and cafes join the reCIRCLE partner network and offer takeaway food/drink in durable, reusable PBT containers and cups. Customers pay a deposit on top of the food price, and can return the container/cup at any partner location (in Denmark or across the wider European network) for a refund or a fresh replacement; containers are collected, washed and recirculated hundreds of times.

Products & Services

Reusable takeaway food containers (multiple sizes/shapes) and drink cups, offered through partner restaurants/cafes under a deposit-return model, supported by an app for finding partner return locations.

Customers

Restaurants, cafes and other food-service businesses in Denmark that join the reCIRCLE partner network, and their end consumers.

Partnerships

Operates as part of the pan-European reCIRCLE network (recirclenow.com) alongside reCIRCLE AG (Switzerland, parent brand) and other national operations; Danish market entry occurred alongside expansion into the Netherlands, Estonia and Italy in June 2021.

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available at the Denmark-specific level (network-wide figures, e.g. 14 million+ disposable packages and 1,400 tonnes of CO2 saved in 2021, are reported for the whole Swiss/European network, not Denmark alone).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A well-established deposit-return packaging system for food service, part of a proven multi-country network model; strong candidate as a reference case for reusable takeaway packaging infrastructure. Priority: ★★★★★ Essential
