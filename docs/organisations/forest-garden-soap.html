Organisation Overview

Forest Garden Soap & Sundry is a family-owned soap maker operating from an off-grid homestead in the Shuswap region of British Columbia, Canada. Its sea-salt-based soaps use wildcrafted herbs, ingredients grown on the family's own property, and honey/beeswax from their own bees. The company was started, per its own materials, out of a desire for a soap product completely free of artificial ingredients.

Business Model

Forest Garden Soap & Sundry manufactures and sells soap and related personal-care products made from self-grown, wildcrafted, and locally sourced ingredients (sea salt, herbs, honey, beeswax from the family's own bees), sold direct-to-consumer and via retail partners (e.g. Portia-Ella). The business model centres on ingredient sourcing and self-sufficiency (off-grid homestead production) rather than a documented packaging-reuse or refill mechanism — no evidence was found of a bottle-return, refill-station, or similar reuse system.
