Organisation Overview

Noms is a Dublin (Phibsborough) organic and zero-waste grocery store certified by the Organic Trust. It stocks bulk dry goods, plant-based foods, and household/cleaning products, with roughly 99% of its range available refill-style via customer-supplied or in-store containers.

Business Model

Independent bricks-and-mortar retail grocer generating revenue from in-store bulk/refill sales plus an online delivery service for organic, plastic-free produce boxes. Positioned as a "closed loop" retailer that avoids introducing new packaging waste, differentiating itself from conventional supermarkets.
