Organisation Overview

AriaPack is a Turkish B2B packaging manufacturer based in Ümraniye, Istanbul, supplying food-service packaging — kraft and non-woven bags, paper cups, cardboard/Kraft-Bristol salad bowls with PET/PP lids, and plastic film and sheet — to cafes, restaurants, and food businesses. The company markets its products with an "eco-friendly" tagline ("Clean Environment, Healthy Future") and offers custom flexo/offset printing. Its product range is dominated by single-use, disposable food-service items rather than reusable consumer goods.

Business Model

AriaPack operates as a contract/custom packaging manufacturer, selling directly to businesses (cafes, restaurants, food producers) rather than consumers. Its core offering is custom-printed disposable paper and plastic packaging — paper cups for hot/cold drinks, kraft and non-woven bags, cardboard salad bowls with plastic lids, and plastic film/sheet — produced via flexo and offset printing to customer specification. The website emphasises responsiveness and design customisation rather than any refill, reuse, or deposit-return service. No revenue, staff, or production-volume figures are disclosed, and no ownership or leadership information is published on the site.
