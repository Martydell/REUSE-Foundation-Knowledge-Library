NOPA Move

Vietnam-made modular reusable bottle and sustainable lifestyle platform for Ho Chi Minh City.

Organisation Overview

NOPA Move designs and produces a modular, double-wall vacuum-insulated reusable bottle/tumbler in Vietnam for the Southeast Asian market, aimed at replacing single-use cups. It also runs a "sustainable lifestyle platform" rewarding users for bringing their bottle to partner cafés and wellness spaces across HCMC.

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer sales of its branded reusable bottle (580ml capacity, 64 colour combinations) through its own online store, combined with a rewards network of 100+ partner cafés/studios that incentivise reuse over single-use cups — generating revenue from bottle sales and partner engagement.

Products & Services

Nopa Move modular reusable drink bottle; partner-café reward network for reuse behaviour in Ho Chi Minh City.

Customers

Urban consumers in Ho Chi Minh City and the wider Southeast Asian market who commute with coffee/tea.

Partnerships

100+ partner cafés, studios and wellness spaces across HCMC (per saynopa.com).

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available (site references a per-bottle plastic-saving goal but no aggregate figures).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A well-designed consumer reusable-bottle product with an interesting café-reward network model for driving behaviour change, though scale and impact data remain unverified. Priority: ★★★ Medium
