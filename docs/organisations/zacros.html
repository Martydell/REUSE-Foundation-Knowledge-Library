Organisation Overview

ZACROS is a long-established Japanese flexible packaging manufacturer founded in 1914 as Fujimori Kogyo Co., Ltd., renamed ZACROS Corporation in October 2024. It is a pioneer of self-standing refill pouches (introduced in 2000 under the FLOWPACK brand) for personal care and household products, and continues to develop monomaterial refill packaging.

Business Model

ZACROS manufactures flexible films and pouches, supplying refill packaging (including its FLOWPACK and MONOSOLUTIONS monomaterial lines) as well as liquid containers and high-clean packaging to personal care, home care, and food manufacturers. Revenue comes from B2B packaging sales to major consumer goods companies, primarily in Japan, with an international arm (Zacros America) serving overseas refill-pouch markets.
