Organisation Overview

TheCircularLab is Ecoembes' open innovation centre for the circular economy, opened in 2017 in Logroño, Spain — described as the first such centre in Europe dedicated to packaging circularity. It researches, tests and develops eco-design, reuse and recycling solutions with universities, tech centres, companies and entrepreneurs.

Business Model

Non-profit R&D and innovation-lab model funded through Ecoembes (Spain's packaging producer-responsibility organisation): TheCircularLab runs pilots, challenges and collaborative projects (e.g. the Circular Packaging Challenge) with external partners rather than selling products directly to consumers.
