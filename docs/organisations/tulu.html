Organisation Overview

Founded in 2018 by architect Yishai Lehavi and environmental entrepreneur Yael Shemer at MIT's DesignX incubator, TULU installs smart locker units in residential/commercial buildings, letting residents rent items like vacuum cleaners, tools and gaming consoles via a mobile app instead of owning them individually.

Business Model

B2B2C shared-access infrastructure: TULU partners with property owners/managers to install physical smart-locker hardware plus a companion app, monetising through building/property partnerships and per-use or subscription resident fees, converting under-used storage space into a shared-item library.

Products & Services

Smart locker-based rental of household items (tools, cleaning equipment, electronics), mobility options (e-scooters/bikes) and consumables, via a mobile app.
