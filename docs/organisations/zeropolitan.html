Organisation Overview

Zeropolitan is an Australian e-commerce store selling reusable and plastic-free personal care and household items — bamboo toothbrushes, cork deodorants, soy wax candles, and related zero-waste goods — aimed at helping customers reduce single-use plastic consumption.

Business Model

Zeropolitan operates a direct-to-consumer online shop (zeropolitan.com.au), selling reusable and compostable alternatives to disposable personal-care and household products, some locally made in Melbourne. Revenue comes from retail product sales, with free shipping thresholds for domestic and international orders. The brand positions itself around consumer education, framing "a Zeropolitan" as an identity for waste-conscious shoppers rather than just a customer segment.
