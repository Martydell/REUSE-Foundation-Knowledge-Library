Organisation Overview

Green Come True is a small, family-run zero-waste soap and household-product refill business based in Lawrence, Kansas, described in local coverage as Lawrence's original zero-waste soap refill store. It makes its own locally-crafted household cleaning and personal-care soaps, sold plastic-free through a physical storefront, seasonal farmers-market stalls (Lawrence and Overland Park), online ordering, and a "milkman-style" free home-delivery route serving Lawrence and the greater Kansas City area. The business also frames itself around broader "climate activism" and sustainability education, per the seed listing, though most verifiable public content focuses on its retail/refill operations rather than a distinct activism programme.

Business Model

Green Come True manufactures its own line of household and body-care soaps (cleaning products, hand and body soaps) locally, then distributes them through multiple no-single-use-plastic channels: an in-store/by-appointment retail location in Lawrence, Kansas; seasonal stalls at the Lawrence Farmers Market (Saturdays) and Overland Park Farmers Market; an online store for direct ordering; and a "milkman-style" home delivery service (free delivery) reaching Lawrence on Fridays and the Kansas City/Overland Park area on Saturdays. Customers either bring reusable containers for refills or receive products in reusable/returnable packaging via the delivery route, with products delivered in eco-friendly market bags. The business also offers bulk and commercial pricing for larger customers. As a small, locally-rooted operation, its business model depends on direct community relationships (farmers markets, local delivery) rather than national e-commerce scale or franchise expansion.
