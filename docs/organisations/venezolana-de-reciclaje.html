Organisation Overview

Venrecicla is a Venezuelan recycling company based in Barquisimeto that recovers solid waste and processes it into secondary raw materials for domestic and export markets. It operates an office in Barquisimeto's Industrial Zone III and a processing plant in Cabudare's La Montañita Industrial Zone, positioning itself around "efficiency, reliability, and responsibility in recycling."

Business Model

The company generates revenue by collecting and processing solid waste (including corporate waste under a "Servicios a Empresas" business line) into secondary raw materials, which it then sells to manufacturers domestically and abroad. The seed data's description of the material stream as specifically "plastic waste" was not confirmed by the company's own site, which describes solid waste recovery more broadly without itemising material types; this should be treated as unverified pending further detail.
