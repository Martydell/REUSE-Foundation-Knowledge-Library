Organisation Overview

CP Group is a large Thai multinational conglomerate spanning agribusiness, food, retail, telecommunications, and other sectors across Thailand and ASEAN. In February 2025 it signed a memorandum of understanding with Mitsubishi Electric Corporation to collaborate on carbon neutrality and circular-economy initiatives, combining Mitsubishi Electric's technology/data capabilities with CP Group's business infrastructure. As of this research, the partnership is at the goal-setting and exploratory stage rather than an operating reuse system.

Business Model

CP Group's core businesses are unrelated to reuse (agribusiness, retail, telecom). The circular-economy-relevant activity researched here is a single cross-industry partnership: the February 2025 MOU with Mitsubishi Electric, under which both companies committed to net-zero greenhouse gas emissions from their own factories/offices by 2030 and value-chain net-zero by 2050, alongside unspecified "measures to reduce waste and environmental impact... through more efficient and circular use of resources, including recycling." The first named initiative is applying Mitsubishi Electric's technology and data capabilities to CP Group's Thai business infrastructure; both companies state further collaboration (AI, robotics) may follow. No concrete reuse programme, product, or metric had been announced as of the MOU signing.
