Organisation Overview

Natures Organics is an Australian-made, family-owned manufacturer of cleaning and personal-care products, run by the Dowel family. Its roots trace to Terry Dowel's father's company Trydel Research, a contract-filling bath-cube manufacturer; the business was renamed Natures Organics in 1991 when Justin Dowel joined.

Business Model

Natures Organics manufactures its own brands (including Natural State and My Soda) at its Ferntree Gully factory, selling through Australian grocery retail. In 2020 it became the first grocery supplier in Australia to introduce personal-care refill options, using an aluminium bottle paired with lower-plastic refill pouches/sachets.

Cleaning and personal-care products (laundry liquid, surface cleaner, body wash, hair care) sold with reusable aluminium starter bottles and refill pouches/sachets containing up to 80% less plastic.

Measurable Impact

First grocery supplier in Australia to introduce refill options in the personal-care category (2020); refill pouches contain 80% less plastic than standard bottles.
