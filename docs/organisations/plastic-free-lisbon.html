Organisation Overview

Plastic Free Lisbon is an online zero-waste store based in Lisbon, Portugal, selling sustainable, ecological, and waste-free household and personal-care products. Orders are dispatched within 48 hours of payment, largely using reused packaging materials to minimize additional waste.

Business Model

Plastic Free Lisbon operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce shop (plasticfreelisbon.com) curating plastic-free and zero-waste products, shipped via registered mail predominantly in reused packaging rather than new materials.
