Organisation Overview

Founded by Shabnam Melwani, Sun Moon Rain opened its first Santa Monica, California store in April 2023, offering a refill bar with 72 plastic-free home, kitchen, laundry, skin and body-care products, serving both in-store and online customers.

Business Model

B2C sustainable lifestyle retailer: refill bar lets customers refill personal-care and household product containers; also sells "prefills" (pre-filled reusable containers) for customers without existing bottles, both in-store and via online ordering.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems
  • Reusable Packaging

Products & Services

Refill bar for hand/body, personal-care and household products (72 SKUs); prefill and online ordering options.
