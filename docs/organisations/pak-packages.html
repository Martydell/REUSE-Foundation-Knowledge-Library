Organisation Overview

Pak Packages is a Lahore-based flexible packaging manufacturer producing multi-layered plastic films using extrusion technology. Available sources describe it as a conventional flexible-packaging producer rather than confirming a dedicated reusable/resealable pouch line.

Business Model

Pak Packages manufactures flexible packaging films and pouches for B2B customers using extrusion-based production, positioning on product quality, durability and "latest trends" in flexible packaging — standard B2B packaging-manufacturer economics rather than a documented reuse-as-a-service model.
