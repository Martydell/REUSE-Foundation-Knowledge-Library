Organisation Overview

A Singapore-based zero-waste advocacy and retail business that grew from a blog into an online and showroom-based shop in 2018, selling low/zero-waste lifestyle products and operating an in-store refill station.

Business Model

E-commerce and showroom retail (WCEGA Tower, Bukit Batok) selling sustainable products (bamboo toothbrushes, shampoo bars, soaps, tooth powder) at accessible price points; proceeds fund zero-waste education efforts and support for local makers; showroom includes a refill station for laundry powder, hand sanitiser and soap nuts using customer-supplied containers.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems (laundry powder, hand sanitiser, soap nuts refill station)
  • Reusable Packaging
  • Consumer Education
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Zero-waste lifestyle products (bamboo toothbrushes, shampoo bars, soap, tooth powder) sold online and in showroom; in-store refill station for household liquids/products.

Customers

Singapore-based consumers, online and showroom shoppers.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A small but genuine zero-waste advocacy-retail hybrid with an operating refill station; modest scale (single showroom plus e-commerce) limits systemic impact, but its education/advocacy mission aligns with REUSE Foundation's consumer-education goals.
