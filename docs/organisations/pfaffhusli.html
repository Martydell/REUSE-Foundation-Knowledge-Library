Organisation Overview

Pfaffhüsli is a small unpackaged ("Unverpackt") shop and café in Pfaffhausen, Switzerland, offering package-free bulk goods. Customers bring their own containers, weigh them at the store, and fill them with the desired quantity of product, reducing single-use packaging waste at the point of sale.

Business Model

Pfaffhüsli operates a bricks-and-mortar bulk/refill retail model combined with a café. Customers pay by weight for bulk goods dispensed into reusable containers they supply themselves; the shop also offers a "bring container" tare-weighing process at checkout. Revenue comes from in-store retail and café sales.
