Organisation Overview

QYOS is a digital-based refill startup founded in 2020 within Enviu Indonesia's Zero Waste Living Lab (launched 2019). It places automatic refill-vending stations for household and personal-care products in residential and retail locations across Indonesia, and now carries "by Algramo" branding reflecting a partnership/integration with the Chilean reuse-tech company Algramo.

Business Model

QYOS installs smart vending machines that dispense refills of partner FMCG brands' products (detergent, dish soap, food items) into customers' own or reusable containers, priced by weight, cutting single-use sachet/bottle packaging. Revenue comes from brand partnerships (Unilever, Nestle, Kao) that pay to distribute products through the refill network as part of the #REFILLution initiative.

Products & Services

Automatic refill-vending kiosks for household cleaning products (Rinso, Sunlight), food/beverage products (Nestle Milo, Koko Krunch), placed in apartment complexes and retail locations.

Customers

Residents of apartment complexes and shoppers at partner retail sites in Jakarta and other Indonesian cities; FMCG brand partners.

Partnerships

Unilever Indonesia, Nestle Indonesia/Nestle R&D Singapore, Kao, and Enviu (incubator); recent "by Algramo" branding indicates a tie-up with Chile's Algramo reuse-tech platform.
