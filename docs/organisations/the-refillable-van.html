Organisation Overview

A mobile, van-based zero-waste refill business serving south-east Kent, UK. Customers bring their own containers to be weighed, filled and re-weighed at farmers' markets, village pop-up refill stations, or via home-delivery/home-shop bookings.

Business Model

Mobile retail via a converted van attending scheduled market/pop-up locations across south-east Kent, plus a bookable "home shop" service bringing products to customers' doorsteps; sells plastic-free household, self-care, beauty and dried food/herb/spice products refilled using a bring-your-own-container (BYOC) weigh-fill-reweigh system.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems (BYOC weigh-fill refill model)
  • Reusable Packaging
  • Consumer Education
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Refillable household, self-care and beauty products, plus dried food, herbs and spices, dispensed at mobile market pop-ups and via bookable home-shop visits.

Customers

Residents across south-east Kent, UK (Dover, Deal, Sandwich, Ashford, Canterbury, Ramsgate areas).
