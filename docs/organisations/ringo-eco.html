Ringo Eco

Estonian B2B reusable-packaging platform providing deposit handling, collection, and industrial washing services for restaurants and retailers.

Organisation Overview

Ringo Eco operates an open reusable-packaging platform in Estonia, letting retailers, restaurants and food producers join with reusable containers while Ringo Eco centrally manages deposit handling, collection, and industrial washing. Founded in 2020 in Tallinn, it has built what is reported to be Northern Europe's largest food-packaging cleaning centre.

Business Model

Ringo Eco runs a centralised B2B2C reusable-packaging infrastructure: partner restaurants and retailers charge customers a deposit on reusable containers, and customers return empty containers to Ringo Eco return bins (available in M/L/XL sizes) by scanning a QR code, after which deposits are refunded to customer accounts. Ringo Eco handles logistics — collecting, industrially washing, and redistributing containers to partners — operating across multiple Estonian cities including Tallinn, Tartu, and Pärnu.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Reusable Packaging (multi-partner container platform)
  • Deposit Return (QR-code deposit/refund system)
  • Reverse Logistics (centralised collection and industrial washing)
  • Digital Tracking (QR-code scanning, mobile scanner app)

Products & Services

Open reusable-packaging platform for food retailers/restaurants; return-bin network (M/L/XL); centralised industrial washing facility; deposit management and mobile scanner app.

Customers

Restaurants, retailers, and food producers across Tallinn, Tartu, Pärnu and surrounding Estonian municipalities; end consumers who order food/drinks in returnable packaging.

Partnerships

Ülemiste City (Tallinn business campus) adopted Ringo's reusable packaging system to move away from single-use packaging.

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available (specific waste-diversion or container-circulation figures not found; company has publicised building "Northern Europe's largest food packaging cleaning center").

Ringo Eco is a verifiable, centrally-managed reusable-packaging platform with real commercial partnerships (e.g., Ülemiste City) and dedicated washing infrastructure, giving it strong direct relevance to the REUSE Foundation's reusable-packaging and deposit-return focus. ★★★★ High
