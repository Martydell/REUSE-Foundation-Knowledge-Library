Organisation Overview

Delivery Refillery is a US refill-delivery service allowing customers to order household and personal-care products priced and sold "per ounce" (by weight), with orders delivered to the customer's home — part of a wider category of regional US "refillery" delivery businesses (alongside comparable services such as The Daisy Refillery in California, Mi Mobile Refillery in Michigan, and Wonderfill Refillery in Tennessee) that emerged to bring bulk/refill retail's waste-reduction benefits to a delivery-based model rather than requiring an in-person shop visit.

Business Model

Customers order consumer goods (likely cleaning, personal-care, or similar categories, priced by the ounce like the comparable services identified) which are delivered to their door, refilling containers rather than requiring purchase of new single-use packaging with each order. This model removes the need for customers to visit a physical refill shop while retaining the core refill mechanic (paying for product volume rather than packaged units).
