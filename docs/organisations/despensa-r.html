Organisation Overview

Despensa R is a bulk-goods ("granel") retail store in Algés, Portugal, that opened in June 2023 with a mission to revive traditional package-free shopping in a modern setting. Customers bring their own containers, jars or bags to fill with organic and artisanal food products — legumes, dried fruits, flours, herbs, spices, cereals, granola, teas and fresh-ground coffee — as well as home and personal-care items. The store also features an in-store nut-butter machine allowing customers to grind fresh nut butter directly into their own container, and offers sourdough bread to order, kombucha and artisanal ice cream.

Business Model

Despensa R operates a single physical bricks-and-mortar retail location in Algés (Lisbon area) built around the bulk/refill retail model: products are stored in dispensers or bulk containers and sold by weight, with customers supplying their own reusable containers to avoid single-use packaging. Revenue comes from in-store retail sales across three main categories — Food, Home & Personal Care, and sustainable-lifestyle accessories — plus an online store (despensar.pt) that also lists products, though the core reuse mechanic (bring-your-own-container refilling) is inherently tied to in-person shopping. The business sources national and organic food producers and artisanal suppliers, positioning itself within Portugal's small but growing granel/refill retail niche alongside peers like Maria Granel. As an independent single-location shop, its business model relies on community/neighbourhood loyalty and a value proposition of reduced packaging waste plus buying only the quantity needed.
