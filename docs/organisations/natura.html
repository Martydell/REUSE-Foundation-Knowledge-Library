Organisation Overview

Natura was founded in 1969 by Antônio Luiz Seabra and is now part of Natura &Co, a publicly listed Brazilian global personal-care group. Natura was the first Brazilian cosmetics company to offer product refills, a program it has run for over 40 years and modernised using sugarcane-based renewable plastics since 2010.

Business Model

Natura sells cosmetics and personal-care products (notably its Ekos line) direct-to-consumer via a large direct-sales representative network as well as retail, offering refill pouches/sachets alongside durable primary packaging to cut plastic use. As a listed group, it reports sustainability metrics publicly, including material circularity targets.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Cosmetics and personal-care products with refill pouches/sachets, including sugarcane-based "green plastic" packaging for its Ekos line.

Customers

Consumers in Brazil and internationally, reached via direct-sales representatives and retail.

Partnerships

Part of Natura &Co Holding S.A. group structure (formerly including Avon, The Body Shop, Aesop at various points). Not publicly available in further detail.

Measurable Impact

84.6% of materials reusable, refillable, recyclable, or compostable as of 2024, with a target of 100% by 2030 (per Natura reporting).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Natura is a large-scale, decades-long refill-system pioneer with strong public reporting and measurable sustainability targets, making it a significant and credible reference case for refill systems at scale.
