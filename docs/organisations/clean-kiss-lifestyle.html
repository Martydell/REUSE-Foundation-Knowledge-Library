Organisation Overview

Clean Kiss Lifestyle is a Canadian, women-owned skincare and personal-care brand founded in 2014, based in Ontario with small-batch, in-house manufacturing. It targets midlife women and those experiencing menopause under a "pro-aging" positioning, selling skincare, body care, hair care, and bar soaps direct-to-consumer. Its sustainability messaging emphasises clean ingredients and "responsible packaging" rather than a documented refill or reuse system.

Business Model

The company formulates and manufactures its own products in small batches at its Ontario facility, sold via direct-to-consumer e-commerce with products described as delivered "fresh to your doorstep." Its target demographic is explicitly midlife/menopausal women, differentiating it from broader zero-waste or refill-focused competitors. The founder holds credentials as a Holistic Nutritionist, Aesthetics Nutrition Practitioner, and Certified Organic Skincare Formulator, and the brand has received consumer media coverage (Breakfast Television, Global News, Today's Parent, Canadian Living, InStyle) focused on its product formulations rather than sustainability infrastructure.
