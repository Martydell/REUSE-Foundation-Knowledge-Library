Organisation Overview

Refill.Swiss is a campaign run by Good For My Planet Association, a Swiss non-profit, bringing the UK-originated "Refill" model to Switzerland by mapping free water-refill locations via a shared app, with plans to expand into coffee and low-waste shopping.

Business Model

Refill.Swiss operates as a non-profit awareness and mapping campaign rather than a revenue-generating business. Participating cafés, shops, and public locations sign up for free, display a window sticker, and appear on the shared Refill app so users can find nearby free water refills; the campaign is funded through its parent association, Good For My Planet, and partner/sponsor support rather than transaction fees.

Products & Services

Free public app/map of refill-water stations across Switzerland; business sign-up toolkit; planned expansion to coffee-to-go and low-waste shopping listings.

Customers

General public seeking free water refills; participating Swiss businesses as station hosts.

Partnerships

Operates under Good For My Planet Association; part of the international Refill network originating in the UK.

Measurable Impact

Cites access to over 5,000 public water fountains referenced for bottle refills nationwide (per company website, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Refill.Swiss extends the proven UK Refill model into Switzerland with a clear non-profit structure and stated expansion plans; a useful additional national case study for refill-station mapping networks.
