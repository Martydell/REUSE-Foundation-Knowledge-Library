Organisation Overview

Nashonuma describes itself as Pakistan's first environment-friendly, eco-driven e-commerce store, delivering biodegradable, compostable and reusable products nationwide, including reusable stainless-steel and bamboo straws and sustainable food packaging out of Lahore.

Business Model

Nashonuma operates as a direct-to-consumer and B2B e-commerce store, selling reusable products (steel/bamboo straws, stainless-steel bottles) alongside compostable and biodegradable food packaging (bagasse bowls, kraft boxes, paper straws) and offering food-packaging services to businesses in Lahore.

Products & Services

Reusable stainless-steel and bamboo straws, reusable stainless-steel water bottles, plus compostable/biodegradable food packaging and printing services.

Customers

Individual consumers across Pakistan via e-commerce; food businesses in Lahore for packaging services.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Nashonuma sells some genuinely reusable items (steel/bamboo straws, bottles) but its broader catalogue is dominated by single-use compostable/biodegradable packaging rather than reuse systems. Modest, product-level relevance rather than a systemic reuse operator.
