Organisation Overview

Re-Up Refills is a US non-profit operating a community refill shop in Rockridge, Oakland, California, providing bulk bath, body, food, kitchen and cleaning products in refillable containers. Its stated mission is to build a better grocery system with minimized waste, responsibly-sourced products, and informed consumers.

Business Model

Customers bring their own containers or reuse Re-Up's containers, refilling bulk products priced by weight so they pay only for what they use. The non-profit sources plant-based, non-toxic, biodegradable products meeting Environmental Working Group (EWG) safety standards, and also offers select shippable refill products online.

Measurable Impact
