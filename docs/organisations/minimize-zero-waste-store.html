Organisation Overview

Minimize is a Subang Jaya, Malaysia-based retail store founded in 2021 by Jayne and Tan, specialising in eco-friendly and refillable products for waste reduction. The founders frame their approach around incremental, sustainable behaviour change rather than requiring customers to adopt zero-waste practices all at once, serving residents across Subang Jaya, the wider Klang Valley, and Malaysia more broadly.

Business Model

Minimize operates as a bricks-and-mortar (and likely online) retail store selling refillable and eco-friendly household and personal-care products, accepting standard digital payment methods (Visa, Mastercard, Google Pay, Apple Pay, FPX, GrabPay) alongside social-media-based customer engagement (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp). The seed tracker's description of a "used cooking oil buy-back program" was not independently confirmed in the source accessed during this research pass.
