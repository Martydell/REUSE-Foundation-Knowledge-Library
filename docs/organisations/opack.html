Organisation Overview

OPACK is described as Malaysia's first operator of reusable cup and food container systems, providing a "rentNreturn" service to replace single-use plastic foodware for events, catering and canteens, paired with digital tracking and an ESG dashboard.

Business Model

Businesses contract OPACK for reusable cutlery, cups and containers; OPACK manages collection, professional washing, logistics, and provides a digital tracking/ESG dashboard so clients can report measurable sustainability outcomes. Revenue comes from B2B service contracts with F&B operators, event organisers and corporate clients.
