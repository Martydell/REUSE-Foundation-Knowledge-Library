Organisation Overview

EarthWay Refill is a small, independently owned package-free refill store in Midleton, East Cork, Ireland, opened in summer 2020 by Wendy Hickey and her partner Andrea Sivieri. Wendy was inspired by refill dispensers she encountered in an Italian health food shop and, as a parent of a large family struggling to reduce single-use plastic, set out to bring the same model to East Cork. The shop sells dry goods, oils, cleaning products and body-care items by weight, with customers supplying their own reusable containers.

Business Model

EarthWay Refill operates a bricks-and-mortar, packaging-free retail model at 3 Main Street, Townparks, Midleton, Co. Cork. Customers bring their own containers (or reuse ones purchased in-store) and pay by weight for bulk dried goods such as pasta, grains, flour, seeds, cereal, nuts, sweets, tea, coffee and herbs, plus bulk oils, and refillable household cleaning products and body-care items. This eliminates single-use packaging at the point of sale and encourages repeat, low-waste shopping habits. As a single-location independent retailer, the business relies on local footfall and community/environmental-group visibility rather than e-commerce or franchising. It has been highlighted publicly as a case study for reuse by environmental NGOs, and its founders have spoken about the difficulty small-scale reuse retailers face in accessing conventional retail funding and financial supports in Ireland, since their model does not fit standard retail categories.

Products & Services

Bulk/refill dry goods (pasta, grains, flour, seeds, cereal, nuts, sweets, tea, coffee, herbs), bulk olive oil, refillable soaps, shampoos and household cleaning products, and reusable containers for customer use.

Customers

Local households and consumers in Midleton and East Cork seeking package-free, low-waste shopping options.

Partnerships

Featured and supported by VOICE Ireland (environmental charity promoting waste reduction/reuse) and profiled by We Choose Reuse as a case study. No formal commercial partnerships are publicly documented.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

EarthWay Refill is a genuine, grassroots example of packaging-free refill retail, directly aligned with the REUSE Foundation's mission, but it is a single small shop with no publicly available quantitative impact data, no digital infrastructure, and limited scalability evidence. It is nonetheless a useful qualitative case study of community-level reuse retail and the funding barriers such businesses face in Ireland.
