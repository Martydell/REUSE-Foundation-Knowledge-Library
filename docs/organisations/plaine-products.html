Organisation Overview

Plaine Products is a US bath and body care brand founded in 2017 by sisters Lindsey and Alison Delaplaine to eliminate single-use plastic from bathrooms. It sells shampoo, conditioner, body wash, lotion, and facial products in refillable aluminum bottles, and is a Certified B Corporation and 1% for the Planet member.

Business Model

Plaine Products sells vegan, cruelty-free personal-care products via direct-to-consumer subscription and retail. Customers return empty aluminum bottles using the same shipping box their refills arrive in; bottles are cleaned and refilled rather than discarded, reducing single-use plastic packaging.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Plaine Products is an early and well-documented US example of a bottle-return refill model for personal care, with credible third-party certifications (B Corp, 1% for the Planet). Its scale remains a small DTC brand without published unit-level reuse data. ★★★★ High
