The Refillery

Organisation Overview

A plastic-free, ethical refill shop based in Newington, Edinburgh, selling dry goods, eco-cleaning products, soaps and local grains/pulses/teas by weight, with UK-wide delivery and click-and-collect refill options.

Business Model

Bricks-and-mortar refill retailer operating from a single Edinburgh storefront, generating revenue from in-store weigh-and-fill sales (BYOC or in-store jars/bags) plus UK-wide mail-order delivery (Royal Mail/DPD, free over £50); positions itself as making "plastic free and ethical shopping" broadly accessible.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems (weigh-fill dry goods, cleaning and soap products)
  • Reusable Packaging (customer-supplied containers, in-store jars)
  • Reverse Logistics (click-and-collect container refill)
  • Consumer Education

Products & Services

Bulk/refillable dry goods, eco-cleaning products, soaps, grains, pulses and teas; UK-wide delivery and click-and-collect refill service.

Customers

Local Edinburgh residents and UK-wide mail-order customers.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

An established single-location UK refill shop with a genuine weigh-fill model and national delivery reach. Modest scale limits systemic relevance, but it is a solid grassroots refill example.
