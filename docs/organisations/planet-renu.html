Organisation Overview

Planet Renu is an e-commerce retailer selling reusable organic cotton mesh produce and shopping bags intended to replace single-use plastic grocery bags. Products are marketed as washable, durable, and foldable, for use in grocery shopping, food storage, and general household organization.

Business Model

Planet Renu sells multi-packs of organic cotton mesh bags direct to consumers online, positioning the product as a reusable substitute for single-use plastic produce bags at grocery stores.
