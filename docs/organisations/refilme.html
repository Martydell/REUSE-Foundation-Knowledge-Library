Organisation Overview

RefilMe supplies "sustainable hydration stations" (filtered/chilled water dispensers) and mobile hydration trucks for corporate, cultural, and sporting events across Brazil, replacing single-use plastic water bottles while offering brands an out-of-home advertising surface.

Business Model

RefilMe operates a B2B event-services model: it delivers, installs, brands, and provides on-site technical support for static hydration stations and mobile hydration trucks at client events, charging per-event fees based on custom quotations. The branded equipment also functions as an advertising/activation channel for sponsors, giving it a dual revenue rationale (waste reduction plus marketing value).
