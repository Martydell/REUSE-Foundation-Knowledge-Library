The Wally Shop

Brooklyn zero-waste grocery delivery service using a closed-loop reusable-packaging courier model.

Organisation Overview

A Brooklyn-based zero-waste grocery delivery service founded in 2017 by Tamara Lim, sourcing local and organic goods from farmers markets and bulk suppliers and delivering them in reusable packaging that couriers later collect for reuse.

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer grocery delivery using a closed-loop reusable-packaging system: couriers drop off orders in reusable containers and retrieve empties on a subsequent visit for cleaning and reuse, removing single-use packaging from the delivery process; revenue from grocery/product sales plus delivery service fees, targeting environmentally-conscious urban consumers.

Circular Economy Activities

  • Reusable Packaging (closed-loop reusable delivery containers)
  • Refill Systems
  • Reverse Logistics (courier container pickup/return)
  • Consumer Education
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Grocery and consumer-product delivery (local/organic foods, household items) in reusable packaging with courier pickup/return of empties.

Customers

Environmentally-conscious grocery-delivery consumers, primarily in the New York City/Brooklyn area.
