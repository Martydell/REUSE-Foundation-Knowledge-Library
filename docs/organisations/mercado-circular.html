Organisation Overview

Mercado Circular is a Chilean circular-economy technology company founded in 2017 in Valparaíso. It designs and operates refill vending machines and accompanying software that let consumers purchase products such as cleaning and personal-care items into reusable containers instead of buying new single-use packaging each time. The company positions itself as a "reuse solutions" provider, working with retailers, municipalities, shopping centers, and consumer goods brands across Chile and, more recently, Mexico. Note: seed data listed Mexico as the company's home country; verified sourcing shows Chile as the origin/HQ, with Mexico as an expansion market.

Business Model

Mercado Circular operates a B2B technology and services model rather than selling directly to end consumers as its core revenue driver. It offers: (1) custom-designed refill vending machines installed in retail, mall, transit, and municipal locations; (2) proprietary software providing real-time monitoring, remote machine operation, and sustainable-packaging/traceability data for client brands; (3) circular-economy advisory/consulting services helping companies transition from linear to reuse-based packaging; and (4) reuse pilot programs and consumer-habit analysis. Its first international refill point launched in a Walmart-owned Sam's Club in Mexico City in partnership with Procter & Gamble, refilling Pantene and Head & Shoulders hair-care products. Revenue appears to derive from machine leasing/licensing to retail and brand partners plus consulting fees, though exact contract terms are not publicly available.

Products & Services

Custom refill vending machines for personal-care, cleaning, and other consumable products; companion software platform for remote machine control, product traceability, and packaging-impact monitoring; circular-economy consulting and pilot-program design for retail and brand clients.

Customers

Retailers, shopping malls, metro/transit stations, municipalities, condominiums, neighborhood stores, and consumer goods brands (e.g., Procter & Gamble) seeking to offer refill formats to shoppers in Chile and Mexico.

Partnerships

Verified partnership with Procter & Gamble (P&G) for its Mexico expansion, refilling Pantene and Head & Shoulders products at a Sam's Club location in Mexico City, operated in coordination with Walmart Mexico. Broader "partners and clients" referenced on the company site without specific names disclosed.

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available (no dated, verified volume, waste-reduction, or emissions figures found).

Technology

Proprietary refill dispensing hardware paired with cloud-based software for real-time machine monitoring, remote operation, and product/packaging traceability analytics.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Mercado Circular is a genuine reuse-infrastructure innovator bringing refill vending technology to Latin American retail at scale, including a notable multinational brand partnership (P&G) inside a major retail chain (Walmart/Sam's Club). Its consulting-plus-hardware-plus-software model is a useful case study for how refill systems can be deployed as a service to established retailers rather than requiring new standalone stores. Verifiable impact data (litres refilled, packaging avoided) is currently absent from public sources, which limits assessment of real-world scale. Priority: ★★★ Medium.
