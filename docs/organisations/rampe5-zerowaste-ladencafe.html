Organisation Overview

Rampe5 is a zero-waste "Ladencafe" (store-café) in Zurich, Switzerland, described as one of the city's original zero-waste outlets. Customers bring their own containers to fill with unpackaged organic food and household products; a small in-store café hosts workshops and community events. It operates under the Genossenschaft grassrooted cooperative, having taken over from a predecessor store, FOIFI.

Business Model

Membership-based cooperative retail store where customers refill their own containers with bulk organic food, household and hygiene products, paying by weight/quantity rather than for packaging; a café element and workshop space generate community engagement and additional revenue. Non-members can also shop.
