Organisation Overview

OceanSaver is a UK eco-cleaning brand producing concentrated, plant-based, water-soluble cleaning pods ("EcoDrops") that dissolve into a reusable spray bottle with water, eliminating repeat plastic bottle purchases. Founded in Leeds, the company recently raised further growth funding (reported December 2025).

Business Model

Direct-to-consumer and retail sales of low-weight, low-packaging cleaning pods designed to refill a reusable "Bottle for Life," reducing plastic and shipping weight versus pre-mixed liquid cleaners. Revenue from pod multi-packs and bottle sales via its own site and retail partners (e.g., Ethical Superstore, EcoLiving).

Circular Economy Activities

  • Refill Systems
  • Reusable Packaging
  • Waste Reduction

Products & Services

Water-soluble concentrated cleaning pods for glass/multi-surface/bathroom cleaning, plus a reusable "Bottle for Life" spray bottle.

Customers

UK consumers purchasing direct or via eco-retail partners; some export/retail presence noted.

Partnerships

Retail stockists including Ethical Superstore, EcoLiving, The Green Turtle; donates 1% of sales to the Blue Marine Foundation for marine habitat restoration in the Solent.

Measurable Impact

Saved over 26 million pieces of plastic packaging compared to conventional bottled cleaners (per official site, cumulative claim).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

A commercially established refill-pod brand with a credible reusable-bottle system and a stated cumulative plastic-reduction figure, making it a solid consumer-facing reuse example, though the 2019 founding year in seed data is corrected to 2018. Priority: ★★★★ High
