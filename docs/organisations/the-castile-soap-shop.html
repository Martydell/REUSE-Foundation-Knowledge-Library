Organisation Overview

The Castile Soap Shop is a Singapore-based e-commerce and wholesale retailer specialising in castile soaps (predominantly the US Vermont Soap brand) and related natural personal-care and household cleaning products, marketed as USDA Certified Organic, vegan, cruelty-free, and free from petroleum, phthalates, and formaldehyde. Beyond retail sales of packaged products, the company operates a refill program allowing customers to bring their own bottles to refill soap and cleaning products, plus a "Mobile Refills" house-call service described as reducing, reusing, and recycling, along with home refill delivery. Products are sold direct-to-consumer online and through Singapore retail partners including Cold Storage and Robinsons.

Business Model

The Castile Soap Shop combines conventional e-commerce retail of bottled castile soap and natural personal-care/cleaning products with a genuine refill offering: customers can bring their own bottles into contact with the company to refill favoured scents/products at a discount ("the more you refill the more you save"), and the company also runs a monthly "Mobile Refills" house-call service and offers refill home delivery, explicitly framed on its own site as promoting "the reuse of our plastic bottles" to "reduce waste and protect our planet." The core product supplied is Vermont Soap-brand castile soap and adjacent lines (body wash, shea butter products, hair care, deodorant, pet shampoo, home cleaning, baby care), sold both to individual consumers online and via wholesale relationships with Singapore retailers Cold Storage and Robinsons. This directly confirms and substantiates the seed data's premise — refill/reuse is genuinely core to the brand's model, not incidental.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

The Castile Soap Shop is confirmed to be a genuine refill-oriented business, not merely a natural/biodegradable soap retailer — this directly resolves the seed data's flagged uncertainty. Its bring-your-own-bottle refill discount, monthly Mobile Refills house-call service, and refill home delivery collectively represent a real, multi-channel reuse infrastructure that is relatively unusual for a small Singapore retailer. It also has verifiable retail distribution through established Singapore chains (Cold Storage, Robinsons). Gaps remain around founding year, ownership, and any quantified impact data, all of which are undisclosed publicly. Overall, a credible small-scale example of refill retail worth including.
