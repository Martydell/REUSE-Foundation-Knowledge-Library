Organisation Overview

REkrabička ("food box") and REkelímek ("cup") form a reusable food-and-drink container deposit system used by restaurants, bistros, and cafes across the Czech Republic as an alternative to single-use takeaway packaging. Containers are manufactured domestically and designed for roughly 400 reuses.

Business Model

Customers receive food or drinks in reusable REkrabička containers (three sizes) or REkelímek cups (small/medium/large) against a refundable deposit (80 CZK and 50 CZK respectively, plus an optional service fee of up to 5 CZK). Deposits are reclaimed when the rinsed container is returned to any of the system's roughly 537 partner establishments, which pay to participate in the pooled reuse network operated by Zero Waste Solutions, s.r.o.

Products & Services

Reusable food containers (REkrabička, 3 sizes) and reusable drink cups (REkelímek, 3 sizes) with a pooled deposit-return system across partner venues.

Customers

Restaurants, bistros, and cafes across the Czech Republic (approx. 537 partner locations) and their takeaway/delivery customers.

Partnerships

Network of 537+ partner restaurants, bistros, and cafes in the Czech Republic (specific brand names not disclosed on the fetched page).

Measurable Impact

Not publicly available (containers cited as reusable ~400 times; no aggregate waste-reduction figures disclosed).

A well-established, domestically manufactured deposit-return network for takeaway packaging with meaningful partner-venue scale for a single country. Lack of published aggregate impact data limits full assessment.
