Organisation Overview

Lana is a Filipino-made natural skincare and zero-waste lifestyle brand offering skincare formulated and packaged with sustainability in mind, including recyclable/repurposable packaging. The brand operates one physical refill station, hosted at Let's Glow Aesthetics in Las Piñas, where customers can bring back empty Lana containers to be refilled rather than purchasing new packaging each time. Lana also sells complementary zero-waste lifestyle items such as yoga mats alongside its core skincare range.

Business Model

Lana sells natural, cruelty-free skincare products (moisturisers, sunscreens, serums, soaps and bundled skincare sets targeting specific skin concerns) direct-to-consumer via its e-commerce website and through a single physical refill partner location. The company frames its packaging strategy around three pillars: eco-friendly sourcing and reduced carbon footprint, cruelty-free formulation, and recyclable/reusable packaging. Its refill model currently depends on a single third-party host site (a beauty aesthetics clinic) rather than a dedicated company-owned refill network, and the company supplements this with pop-up and bazaar appearances promoted through social media rather than a broader retail footprint. Revenue is primarily generated through direct product and bundle sales rather than a refill-as-a-service subscription structure.
