Organisation Overview

Refill Lab is Argentina's first refillable cosmetics and home-care company, founded in January 2021 by two sisters-in-law with combined experience in the fragrance and cosmetics industry, offering hair care, creams, sanitiser, and home-fragrance products via a refill model.

Business Model

Refill Lab sells products in bulk 2-liter containers (replacing 10-20 conventional bottles) through online sales nationwide and a network of roughly 35 wholesalers/retail partners. Customers return containers for refilling and receive a monetary voucher (reported ~$100 ARS) as an incentive, creating a closed-loop refill economy for natural, vegan, cruelty-free cosmetics and home-care products.

Products & Services

Refillable hair care, skin creams, hand sanitiser gel, home fragrances/diffusers, and reusable refill containers, sold online and through wholesale partners.

Customers

Individual consumers across Argentina (online direct sales) and a network of roughly 35 wholesale/retail partners.

Not publicly available (specific brand or retail partner names not identified in sources reviewed).

Measurable Impact

Company reports having prevented disposal of more than 633,000 containers since launch (per company/press sources, not independently audited).

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Refill Lab is a notable first-mover refill model in the Argentine cosmetics market with a clear reuse incentive (voucher-for-return) and reported large-scale container diversion; a strong Latin American case study for refill-based consumer goods.
