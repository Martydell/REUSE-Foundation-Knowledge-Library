Organisation Overview

Coles Local Chatswood is a concept supermarket store operated by Coles Group, opened 11 November 2020 in Westfield Chatswood, Sydney. It features what Coles describes as Australia's first in-supermarket refill station for shampoo, conditioner, body wash, and laundry liquid, alongside a broader locally-focused format sourcing from 35+ Sydney-based suppliers and additional sustainability features (recycled-material trolleys, food composting).

Business Model

The refill station allows customers to select a bottle size, choose a product, fill it themselves, and check out — using refillable bottles made from recycled and sugarcane-derived plastic rather than single-use packaging. This sits within a broader "Coles Local" concept-store format emphasising hyper-local sourcing (35+ Sydney suppliers including Pasta Pantry, Sonoma, and Field to Fork), an expanded plant-based and Asian-grocery range, and additional sustainability features such as trolleys made from recycled milk bottles and REDcycle soft-plastics, plus composting of unsold food. As a Coles Group subsidiary location, revenue follows standard supermarket retail economics; the refill station is one feature within the broader store concept rather than the store's primary business model.

REUSE Foundation Assessment

Coles Local Chatswood is a notable example of a major national supermarket chain embedding a refill station directly into a mainstream retail format, described by the company as an Australian first — relevant as a proof point that refill infrastructure can be integrated into large-scale conventional grocery retail rather than remaining confined to independent zero-waste shops. Its relevance is somewhat limited by being a single concept store rather than a chain-wide rollout, and by the absence of published refill-specific impact metrics.
