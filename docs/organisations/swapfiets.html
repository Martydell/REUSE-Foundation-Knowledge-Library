Organisation Overview

Founded in 2014 by student friends in Delft, Swapfiets pioneered "bicycle-as-a-service": subscribers get a maintained bike with 48-hour repair/swap guarantee for a monthly fee, eliminating bike ownership. It has grown to 280,000+ subscribers across 8+ European countries and is majority-owned by Pon Holdings (Pon.bike).

Business Model

B2C subscription rental: bikes designed for durability and easy repair are rented monthly rather than sold; when broken, Swapfiets repairs or swaps the bike within 48 hours at no extra cost, extending each bike's useful life across many subscribers rather than each user buying/discarding their own.

Subscription bicycle and e-bike rental with maintenance/repair/swap service.

Customers

Measurable Impact

280,000+ active subscribers; operations in 8+ European countries; 10-year anniversary celebrated in 2024.
