Organisation Overview

Established July 2019 by John Tedders — whose family transitioned from conventional to organic farming — The Filling Station Eco Store in Galway supports organic producers with hundreds of refillable food, cleaning and personal-care dispensers, using only recyclable materials.

Business Model

B2C zero-waste grocer: bulk dispensers for dried fruit/nuts, cleaning products and personal-care items refilled by customers, sourcing from local organic producers and stocking items like bamboo toothbrushes and locally-produced natural skincare.
