Organisation Overview

Vegan Dükkan describes itself as Turkey's first all-vegan product market, operating in Istanbul's Cihangir neighborhood. It began in 2004 as an ecological and health-food store and transitioned to a fully vegan model in 2012, selling organic, vegetarian/vegan, and cruelty-free products.

Business Model

Independent retail grocery selling organic and vegan food, cosmetics, and household products (bread, plant milks, tofu, bio cosmetics, cleaning products) to health- and ethics-conscious consumers; also operates a related restaurant, Vegan Dükkan Lokanta.
