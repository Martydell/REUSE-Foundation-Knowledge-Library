Organisation Overview

Returnr is an Australian company ("Powering reuse since 2018") that manufactures and distributes reusable containers — insulated stainless-steel cups, bowls, trays, and storage solutions — designed to replace single-use disposable packaging for takeaway food and drink.

Business Model

Returnr supplies businesses, particularly cafes, caterers, and workplaces, with durable reusable containers under a deposit/return-style model where customers return items for reuse. It sells workplace bundles for groups of 100+ people and accessories such as thermal tote bags, generating revenue through direct product sales and workplace program contracts.

Products & Services

Insulated stainless-steel cups (8-16oz), polymer bowls and trays, storage containers, workplace bundle programs, and thermal tote bag accessories.

Customers

Cafes, caterers, and workplaces across Australia seeking reusable takeaway packaging alternatives.
