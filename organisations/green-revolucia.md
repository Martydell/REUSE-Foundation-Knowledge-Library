## Organisation Overview

Green Revolucia was founded in 2017 by Vera Markova as Bulgaria's first zero-waste shop, with a mission to make a zero-waste lifestyle affordable and accessible. It has since evolved into an online retailer focused substantially on sustainable menstrual health products — menstrual cups, discs, and swimwear/underwear, organic cotton pads — alongside natural cosmetics, children's reusable items (organic cotton/hemp underwear), and educational materials on menstrual health and sustainability. The company received EU regional development funding in 2020 and operates a blog and workshop/consulting offering alongside its retail store.

## Business Model

Green Revolucia sells direct-to-consumer via its own e-commerce website, shipping across Bulgaria through logistics partners Speedy and Econt, with flexible payment options (cash-on-delivery, card, PayPal) and free shipping above 100 BGN (~€51). Its product range centres on reusable menstrual health items — menstrual cups and discs, period underwear and swimwear, and organic cotton pads, categorised by absorption level — alongside natural cosmetics (hair, body, face, and shaving products) and children's reusable items (hemp/organic cotton underwear). Beyond retail, the company runs a blog covering menstrual health and sustainability topics, and offers corporate gift services, workshops, and consulting on sustainability/zero-waste topics. The business received EU regional development funding in 2020, which appears to have supported its scale-up from its original "zero-waste shop" concept toward its current menstrual-health-focused retail model.
