## Organisation Overview

Returnity, founded in 2016 and based in Brooklyn, New York (formerly Reusable Solutions Group), develops "The Last Box," a durable, collapsible reusable tote for B2B shipping that integrates into existing carrier networks such as FedEx without requiring software or workflow changes.

## Business Model

Returnity sells/leases reusable shipping totes designed for 50+ reuse cycles as a drop-in replacement for single-use corrugated boxes, serving retail, fashion/footwear, third-party logistics (3PL), traditional and direct-to-consumer retail, and grocery sectors. It offers a full-service integration model including cleaning, repair, and replacement of totes, plus custom B2B and consumer reusable bag solutions.

## Partnerships

Integrates with existing carrier networks, including FedEx.

## Measurable Impact

Company states its totes have eliminated 100,000+ corrugated boxes, cut unpacking labor by 50%, reduce CO2 by 0.12 lb per item returned versus corrugate, and show fewer damage incidents than corrugate across 50+ reuse cycles.
