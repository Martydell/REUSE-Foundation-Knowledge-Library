## Organisation Overview
Trash Hero Czech Republic is the national chapter of the global Trash Hero movement, running local clean-up events and a free water-refill network across cities including Prague, Jablonec nad Nisou, Plzeň, Hradec Králové, Jihlava and Nymbursko, letting people refill their own Trash Hero bottles for free at participating businesses.

## Business Model
Volunteer-driven non-profit: local chapters organise community clean-ups and recruit local businesses as free water-refill points on the global Trash Hero map; funded via donations, bottle sales and chapter fundraising rather than commercial revenue.

## Products & Services
Free public water-refill points mapped online; reusable Trash Hero bottles for sale; community litter clean-up events.

## Customers
Czech residents and visitors seeking free water refills; local businesses hosting refill points.

## Partnerships
Network of 579 participating refill-host businesses across the global Trash Hero network (not Czech-specific figure).

## Measurable Impact
Global Trash Hero refill network prevented an estimated 1.9 million single-use plastic bottles and 104.4 tonnes of CO2 emissions in 2024 (network-wide figure, not Czech Republic-specific).
