## Organisation Overview
SF Express is China's largest courier and logistics company, piloting reusable packaging in over 20 cities for scenarios including fresh cold-chain delivery and government document transport, as part of a broader "Zero Waste Cities" and full-lifecycle packaging sustainability strategy.

## Business Model
Large-scale logistics operator integrating reusable plastic boxes into inter-station transportation (alongside peers JDL Express and Deppon), replacing single-use packaging for repeat-cycle logistics routes as part of an industry-wide closed-loop packaging standard-setting effort.

## Products & Services
Reusable plastic transit boxes for cold-chain, government-document and other repeat logistics routes; packaging recycling programme.

## Customers
