## Organisation Overview

Mottainai Refill is a Medellín, Colombia startup refilling household and personal-hygiene products, aiming to reduce single-use plastic via a home-refill delivery model, using Instagram (6,100+ followers) as its primary customer-engagement and scheduling platform.

## Business Model

Customers bring/reuse containers and pay by weight for biodegradable household and personal-care refills, delivered/scheduled via Instagram in Medellín.
