Dispo Plastik is a Turkish thermoform-plastics manufacturer based near Istanbul, producing plates, cups, cutlery, food containers, fruit-packaging trays, nursery/seedling trays and related disposable tableware products at reported production volumes exceeding 1,000 tons per month.
