## Organisation Overview

asa Beauty is a Mumbai-headquartered, direct-to-consumer clean beauty brand launched in 2020 by Asha Jindal Khaitan (co-founder of nutraceutical brand Pure Nutrition) and her daughter-in-law Sukriti Jindal Khaitan. The brand positions itself at the intersection of luxury and sustainability, offering natural, vegan, and cruelty-free makeup — lipsticks, lip & cheek tints, highlighters, SPF face sticks, and lip balms — built around a refillable packaging system intended to reduce single-use plastic waste while maintaining a premium look and feel. It has received coverage in Vogue India, Grazia, Elle, and the Indian Express.

## Business Model

asa Beauty sells makeup and skincare-adjacent products direct-to-consumer (primarily online, via its own site and social channels), built around a "buy once, refill repeatedly" packaging model: outer cases are designed for reuse while the cosmetic contents are replaced via refill inserts, reducing packaging waste per repurchase. Packaging materials are stated to use aluminium and FSC-certified paper, described by the company as chlorine- and heavy-metal-free, biodegradable, and recyclable. The brand emphasises an inclusive shade range and a "conscious lifestyle"/"clean awakening" positioning. No revenue, funding round amounts, or unit-sales figures are publicly disclosed as of this research.
