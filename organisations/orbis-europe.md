## Organisation Overview
ORBIS Europe develops and manufactures reusable transport packaging — foldable large containers (FLCs), pallets and specialty load carriers — for industrial, food & beverage, and logistics customers, drawing on ORBIS Corporation's reusable-packaging heritage.

## Business Model
ORBIS Europe sells and services durable plastic returnable packaging (FLCs, pallets, battery shipping boxes) to manufacturers and retailers as an alternative to single-use cardboard/wood, positioning reuse as a way to cut transport packaging costs and waste across supply chains. It operates as part of the wider ORBIS/Menasha group with a European manufacturing and sales presence including Poland and Germany.
