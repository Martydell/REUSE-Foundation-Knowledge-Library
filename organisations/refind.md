## Organisation Overview

Refind is described as Sri Lanka's first and exclusive online thrifting marketplace, connecting buyers and sellers of second-hand clothing, furniture, home decor, beauty items, and more. It aggregates multiple independent vendor stores under one platform with nationwide delivery.

## Business Model

Refind operates a multi-vendor online marketplace: independent thrift and second-hand sellers list products (clothing, furniture, electronics, home decor) that shoppers purchase through the platform via one of 15 supported payment methods, with nationwide delivery across Sri Lanka. Refind's mission is framed as reducing fashion's environmental footprint by extending product life through resale.
