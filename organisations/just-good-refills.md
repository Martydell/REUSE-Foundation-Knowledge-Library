## Organisation Overview

Just Good Refills is a refill shop based in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, offering refillable and swappable containers for personal-care and home-care products. It operates via both a dedicated website and a Facebook presence (justgoodcambodia), positioning itself around product quality alongside its refill/reuse model.

## Business Model

Just Good Refills sells personal-care and home-care products through a refillable/swappable container system: customers presumably purchase or use a durable container initially, then reorder product refills rather than buying new packaged units each time — the standard refill-shop mechanism common across the zero-waste retail sector. Specific details on collection logistics, container ownership, and product categories were not confirmed in sources accessed during this research pass.
