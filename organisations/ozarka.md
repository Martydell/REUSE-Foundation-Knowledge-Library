## Organisation Overview
Ozarka provides reusable, durable food and drink containers (silicone, borosilicate, polypropylene) as a pay-per-use replacement for single-use takeout packaging, delivering, collecting, washing and returning items for hospitality clients under the motto "you do the food, we do the dishes."

## Business Model
Ozarka operates a full-service reuse loop: restaurants, caterers and canteens pay per use for reusable containers; Ozarka handles delivery, pickup, washing and redelivery on a scheduled basis, removing the need for clients to manage storage or cleaning infrastructure themselves.
