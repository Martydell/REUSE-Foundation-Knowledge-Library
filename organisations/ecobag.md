## Organisation Overview

EcoBag is a small French company based in Livron-sur-Drôme that has specialised for over two decades in personalised, reusable bags and gift packaging made from natural materials — primarily jute canvas, cotton, kraft paper, and non-woven fabric. Registered as a SARL (SIREN 432 086 437) since July 2000, the company supplies businesses, retailers, producers, and public institutions with custom-branded, durable bags intended to replace single-use packaging, positioning environmental durability alongside client branding and design.

## Business Model

EcoBag operates a B2B custom-manufacturing model: clients submit logos, dimensions, and design requirements and receive a quote (typically within 24 hours) for personalised bags and boxes, with delivery around 20 days later. Its core material is jute canvas, chosen as a natural, biodegradable, and reusable alternative to plastic packaging, supplemented by cotton, kraft paper, and non-woven fabric options. Target sectors include breweries, wine merchants, caterers, libraries, market gardeners/producers, local authorities, and retail shops — businesses seeking durable, reusable, branded carrier bags rather than disposable packaging. The company is a small operation (3–5 employees, €15,000 capital per registry data) generating modest but stable revenue (net result €51,900 in the 2025 filing year versus €232,300 in 2024, per public company registry data), reflecting a niche, long-established regional manufacturer/distributor rather than a large-scale industrial producer.
