## Organisation Overview

Plastic Free Life is a Bulgarian eco-goods retailer based in Sofia, selling plastic-free home and cosmetic products online and, since September 2022, through a physical store on Iskar Street. Its range includes reusable cotton tea bags, biodegradable sponges, and eco shaving sets, marketed as free of plastic and animal-tested ingredients.

## Business Model

Plastic Free Life operates an e-commerce storefront (plasticfreelife.bg) alongside a Sofia retail location, selling curated plastic-free household and personal-care products direct to consumers. The brand maintains an active social media presence (Instagram, TikTok) to promote its plastic-free lifestyle mission.
