## Organisation Overview

Bulle Verte is a French, family-owned manufacturer of ecologically certified cleaning products, hand cleaners, and (since 2019) cosmetics, based in the Drôme Provençale region. According to trade press (Bio Linéaires), the company originated in 1986 as CDS, a soap-distribution business serving mechanics, began manufacturing hand cleaners in 1988, and adopted the "Bulle Verte" brand in 1994. It initially built its business supplying professional clients (garages, dry cleaners, restaurants, local authorities) before expanding into organic/bio retail. Since 1995 it has offered its products in bulk ("vrac") format via gravity-fed dispensing systems for retailers, alongside individually packaged versions. It is not a Belgian retail store as originally assumed — it is a French manufacturer distributed into Belgium by third-party distributors.

## Business Model

Bulle Verte manufactures and sells a range of over 100 certified cleaning, hand-hygiene, and cosmetic products, most certified by Ecocert (Ecodétergent) with cosmetics certified by Nature & Progrès. Its core differentiator is offering essentially its entire product catalogue in bulk/vrac format — using refillable cubitainers for liquids and bins for powders — for gravity-fed in-store dispensing, in addition to conventional packaged retail units. The company's original customer base was B2B professional services (automotive garages, dry cleaners, restaurants, municipalities/collectivities); it later expanded distribution into organic/bio retail chains (e.g., Biocoop network) and independent bulk/zero-waste stores across France, and is distributed into Belgium via local distributors (identified in research as Biodis and Naturathome) rather than operating its own Belgian retail presence. The business remains family-owned across three generations.

## Products & Services

Bulk (vrac) and packaged eco-certified cleaning products (laundry detergents, stain removers, hand cleaners, household and kitchen cleaners) and, since January 2019, a cosmetics line (gels, soaps, shampoos); refillable gravity-fed dispensing equipment (cubitainers, powder bins) supplied to retail partners.

## Customers

B2B: professional/institutional clients (garages, dry cleaners, restaurants, local authorities/collectivities) and retail partners (organic/bio grocery chains such as Biocoop, independent bulk/zero-waste stores in France, and distributors in Belgium). Not primarily a direct-to-consumer retailer.
