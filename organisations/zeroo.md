## Organisation Overview

Zeroo is a Portuguese smart-packaging venture, launched in 2023 as a co-creation between The Loop Co. and Loja do Zero, that develops automated bulk-dispensing systems and RFID-enabled reusable cups for retailers. Its technology targets bulk, refill and takeaway retail operations, replacing manual weighing with automated, traceable dispensing.

## Business Model

Zeroo is a B2B provider supplying its "Smart Bulk System" — robotised, automated dispensers that fill standard-sized reusable "Zero Cups" without manual weighing — to retailers and brands running bulk or refill operations. The reusable cups carry RFID tags and QR codes that log usage counts, plastic saved, product origin, nutritional information and expiry data, viewable by consumers through a companion app showing a "digital pantry" of purchased cups. Auchan is a named retail partner deploying the system.
