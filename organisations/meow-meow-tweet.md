## Organisation Overview

Meow Meow Tweet is a Brooklyn-based vegan, all-natural personal-care brand founded in 2009 by Jeff Kurosaki and Tara Pelletier, who met in art school. The brand's product line spans soap, natural insect repellent, deodorant, and a full facial-care regimen, using plant- and mineral-based formulas developed with Tara's background as a chef and herbalist. The company is a Certified B Corporation and is credited as the first company to popularize the zero-waste deodorant stick, packaged in 100% biodegradable paper that is compostable in both municipal and home composting systems.

## Business Model

Meow Meow Tweet manufactures and sells natural, vegan personal-care products direct-to-consumer and via retail partners (e.g. Integrity Botanicals, Sage Refill Market). Its packaging-innovation angle centres on the deodorant stick format — using biodegradable, compostable paper packaging rather than a plastic tube — positioning the brand as a pioneer of the now-common "paper deodorant tube" format that other brands have since adopted.
