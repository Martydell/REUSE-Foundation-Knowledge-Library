## Organisation Overview

Well Spent Grain Ltd, trading as "Born Again Snacks," is a Dublin-based food company that transforms brewery spent grain — a byproduct typically discarded after beer production — into nutritious snack bars. Founded in 2022, it is based in Walkinstown, Dublin, and has been supported by Irish innovation programmes including Teagasc, Climate Launchpad, Food Academy, and New Frontiers.

## Business Model

The company sources spent grain (a high-fibre, high-protein byproduct of brewing that breweries would otherwise discard) and processes it into snack bars sold under the "Born Again Snacks" brand, generating revenue through retail listings in SuperValu supermarkets across Dublin and Wicklow and foodservice sales to venues like The Edge Café Tallaght and Fitzwilliam Lawn Tennis Club.
