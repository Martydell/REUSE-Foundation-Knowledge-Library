## Organisation Overview
Tierra Verde Bulgaria offers natural, toxin-free household products — cleaning, laundry, dishwashing and baby-care lines — sold in reusable packaging designed to be refilled repeatedly from bulk stores or larger containers rather than discarded.

## Business Model
B2C consumer goods brand: sells natural cleaning/personal-care products through its own webstore and retail partners (e.g. Zoya), with reusable tubes/bottles intended for repeat refilling from bulk-size packs, reducing single-use plastic per purchase cycle.
