## Organisation Overview

Refill.ie is an Irish non-profit social enterprise, founded in 2017, that maps free drinking-water refill locations on its "Tap Map" app, aiming for refill access within 400m of anywhere in Ireland, alongside waste-free event services and mobile hydration station rentals.

## Business Model

Refill.ie operates as a non-profit: any surplus from paid services (waste-free event services, mobile hydration station/water cube rentals for events) is reinvested into growing the free public Tap Map network. Businesses join as free members, displaying window stickers, and are listed on the Tap Map app so the public can find no-cost water refill points; funding also comes from grants and philanthropic/council support.
