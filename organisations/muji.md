## Organisation Overview

Muji originated in December 1980 as a product brand of supermarket chain The Seiyu, launching with 40 products across apparel, household goods, and food, and has since grown into the global Ryohin Keikaku retail company. Select flagship stores offer refill stations for liquid detergent and weight-based pantry staples.

## Business Model

Global minimalist retail conglomerate (apparel, household goods, food); select flagship stores run refill stations for liquid detergent and bulk pantry staples sold by weight.
