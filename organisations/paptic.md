## Organisation Overview
Paptic Ltd, founded in 2015 and based in Espoo, Finland, developed Paptic®, a renewable-wood-fibre material engineered to replace plastic film in flexible packaging while remaining recyclable alongside cardboard and paper streams. Industrial-scale production began in 2018.

## Business Model
Paptic licenses/sells its fibre-based material to converters and brands (e.g., furniture packaging for Artek) as a drop-in plastic substitute usable on existing paper-converting machinery, targeting the >€200B global flexible-packaging market with a recyclability-first value proposition rather than a reuse-loop model.
