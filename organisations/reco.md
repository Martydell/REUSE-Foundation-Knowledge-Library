## Organisation Overview

Reco is a UK online retailer selling concentrated, low-waste alternatives to conventional single-use plastic personal-care and cleaning products, including laundry detergent sheets and toothpaste tablets, sold in recyclable cardboard packaging.

## Business Model

Reco operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce model, selling pre-measured detergent sheets and tooth tabs shipped in lightweight cardboard packaging (letterbox-sized) rather than heavy plastic bottles or tubs. Revenue comes from repeat online sales of consumable products rather than a reuse or refill loop; the sustainability pitch centers on lower shipping weight/carbon versus liquid equivalents and elimination of plastic bottle waste, not on reusable packaging itself.
