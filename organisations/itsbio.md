## Organisation Overview

It's Bio (ItsBio) is a package-free retail shop operating from Port Artur shopping centre in Pärnu, Estonia, run by the registered company Biokaup OÜ. It sells organic and local food (nuts, seeds, dried fruits, grains, spices, superfoods) largely in bulk/package-free form, alongside natural cosmetics and personal-care products, home items, and children's products. Its stated mission is to reduce packaging use and offer plastic-free products, summarised in its own tagline "Vähem prügi, kergem elu!" ("Less waste, easier life"). The company also runs a webshop with nationwide Smartpost parcel-machine delivery and occasional workshops.

## Business Model

ItsBio operates a bricks-and-mortar package-free retail model complemented by e-commerce. Customers shop in-store using bulk dispensers/refill for dry goods (grains, nuts, spices) and other package-minimised product formats, reducing single-use packaging at the point of sale. The business also sells pre-packaged organic and natural personal-care items where bulk dispensing is impractical (e.g., some cosmetics), and supplements retail income with an online store offering nationwide delivery via Smartpost parcel machines (free above a minimum order threshold) and gift cards/gift packages. Revenue appears to derive from standard retail margins on organic/local food and natural-product lines rather than a subscription or platform-fee model. The company also offers workshops, suggesting a secondary consumer-education/community-engagement revenue or engagement stream, though details on these are limited in public sources.

Bulk/package-free organic food (nuts, seeds, dried fruits, grains, spices, superfoods); natural personal-care and body-care products (soap, deodorant, toothpaste, skincare); home and lifestyle items (candles, essential oils, home fragrance); children's products; gift cards/packages; workshops; online store with nationwide delivery.
