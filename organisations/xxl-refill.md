## Organisation Overview

XXL Refill is a Portuguese retail franchise specialising in refilling and recycling ink and toner printer cartridges on-site, alongside selling compatible toners and offering basic computer assistance. The network operates outlets in shopping centres and standalone stores across multiple Portuguese cities/regions, including Covilhã, Leiria, Figueira da Foz, Maia, Aveiro and Vila Nova de Gaia.

## Business Model

XXL Refill franchises retail storefronts (often in shopping centres or supermarket precincts) that refill customers' empty ink/toner cartridges on the spot rather than requiring customers to buy new ones, extending cartridge life and reducing e-waste; stores also sell new toners and compatible cartridges and offer computer support services. Revenue is generated per-refill and via product/service sales at each franchise location, with the network having expanded through new franchise openings across Portugal.
