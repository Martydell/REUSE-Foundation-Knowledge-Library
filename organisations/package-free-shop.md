## Organisation Overview
Package Free Shop began as a 3-month popup in Williamsburg, Brooklyn in April 2017, founded by zero-waste advocate Lauren Singer following her viral "waste fits in a mason jar" story. It has grown into an online and physical retailer of package-free and reusable goods.

## Business Model
Package Free operates an e-commerce and physical retail marketplace curating zero-waste, reusable and low-impact products across beauty, cleaning, pet, baby and household categories, shipping orders plastic-free. Revenue is generated through direct product sales rather than a refill/return infrastructure.
