## Organisation Overview

Zero Waste Malaysia is a non-profit NGO, co-founded in 2016 and formally registered in 2018, that works to make zero-waste living accessible across Malaysia. It builds educational content and free public resources, including a crowdsourced map of zero-waste and refill locations, and engages individuals, community groups and corporates on waste reduction.

## Business Model

As a registered non-profit, Zero Waste Malaysia is funded through donations and does not sell products directly. It produces and maintains free public resources — a nationwide zero-waste/refill-spot map, a "Zero Waste Education Series," educational videos, and a "Trash Encyclopedia" — and engages corporates, community groups and individuals through campaigns and pledges (e.g. its "Zero Waste Pledge" programme).
