## Organisation Overview
Unibag manufactures reusable non-woven (TNT) bags and compostable plant-fiber bags for retail, e-commerce, and produce use. Founded in Chile as the country's first reusable-bag factory, it has since expanded to Peru and Mexico, supplying major retail chains.

## Business Model
B2B manufacturer selling reusable polypropylene (TNT) bags and corn-starch-based compostable bags to retail chains, pharmacies, and e-commerce companies, positioning products as substitutes for single-use plastic bags under bag-ban regulations in Chile and Peru.

## Products & Services
Reusable TNT (non-woven polypropylene) bags, compostable vegetable-fiber produce bags, e-commerce delivery bags, reusable masks.

## Customers
Major retail chains (e.g., Sodimac, Inkafarma) and companies seeking sustainable packaging in Chile, Peru, and Mexico.

## Partnerships
Retail partners including Sodimac and Inkafarma in Peru; member of the Chilean Plastics Pact (Pacto Chileno de los Plásticos).
