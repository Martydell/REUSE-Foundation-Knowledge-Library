## Organisation Overview

Avo & Co. is a Singapore-based online grocery delivery company founded by brothers Clement and Cedric, who were dissatisfied with the quality of fruit and vegetables available through conventional supermarkets. The company sells curated and customisable fresh produce boxes — fruit, vegetables, organic items, microgreens, edible flowers and pantry staples — via subscription (weekly, fortnightly or monthly, with no lock-in) or one-off orders, delivered island-wide in Singapore. It positions itself on produce quality, direct sourcing relationships with farmers, and reduced packaging compared to conventional plastic-wrapped supermarket produce.

## Business Model

Avo & Co. operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce and subscription-box model, sourcing fresh produce from farms and producers and delivering it to households and some businesses across Singapore, typically with next-day delivery for orders placed before a daily cutoff. Customers can choose pre-curated seasonal boxes or build custom orders, with recipe cards included to help reduce food waste at the household level. On packaging, the company's own marketing and customer feedback point to loose or paper-wrapped produce and minimal, largely biodegradable packaging in place of plastic wrap; however, no verified public source (own website or independent reporting) could be found describing a formal returnable-crate collection-and-reuse system as referenced in the seed data. The business appears to be a small, founder-run operation rather than a large logistics-scale company, and is listed among "zero-waste"-aligned Singapore brands in local sustainability roundups.
