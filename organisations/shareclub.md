## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2022 by Sarah Fliessbach, Shareclub provides businesses with reusable hot/cold drink cups and bowls for workplace canteens, cafés, corporate events and festivals, tracked via a mobile app (QR code borrow/return) or RFID tags for higher-frequency settings.

## Business Model
B2B packaging-as-a-service: clients rent reusable cup/bowl fleets; Shareclub's app or RFID system tracks borrowing and return, providing clients with impact dashboards (CO₂ savings, waste-reduction metrics) for sustainability reporting, funded partly via EPA Green Enterprise Innovation grant.

## Measurable Impact
Cites Ireland-wide context: 22,000 disposable cups used hourly, <1% recycled/composted.
