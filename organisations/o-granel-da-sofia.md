## Organisation Overview
O Granel da Sofia is an organic and bulk grocery store in Loures, Portugal, offering food staples (cereals, spices, legumes, chocolates) plus bulk cleaning and cosmetic products. It prioritises national producers and actively promotes a bring-your-own-container (BYOC) refill system.

## Business Model
Bricks-and-mortar retail store (Centro Comercial da Portela) plus an online store (ograneldasofia.pt) offering delivery within continental Portugal and the islands. Revenue from bulk food and household product sales, with BYOC refill encouraged to reduce packaging.
