## Organisation Overview

ecostore JAPAN Co., Ltd. is the Japanese licensee of the New Zealand natural home and body-care brand ecostore, bringing its plant-based cleaning and personal-care products, and in particular its refill-station model, to the Japanese market. Trade press dates the Japan operation's founding to around 2017, with its "Refill Station" concept introduced to Japanese retail — including convenience-store partnerships (Natural Lawson) — from August 2020. It is a distinct, separately incorporated licensee rather than an ecostore-owned subsidiary, reflecting the parent brand's regional licensing model in Asian markets.

## Business Model

ecostore Japan distributes and markets ecostore's plant- and mineral-based cleaning and body-care products in Japan, and operates a bulk in-store refill network built through partnerships with existing Japanese retailers rather than dedicated standalone refill shops. As of 2021 the network had grown to reportedly 45 refill stations, more recently described as 40+ partner locations spanning from Hokkaido to Okinawa, hosted inside stores such as Biople and Cosme Kitchen outlets, independent natural/lifestyle retailers, and (on a pilot basis from 2020) Natural Lawson convenience stores. Customers bring their own clean, sealable containers and purchase dish soap, laundry detergent, fabric softener, and body/hand wash by weight (reported at roughly ¥99–¥143 per 100g, tax included), at a price positioned below equivalent bottled products. This partner-embedded model lets ecostore Japan scale refill access without the capital cost of dedicated retail real estate.

## Products & Services

Refillable dish-wash liquid (multiple scents), laundry detergent, delicate wash, fabric softener, and body/hand wash sold by weight at partner-store refill stations; ecostore's standard packaged product range through Japanese retail.

## Customers

Japanese retail consumers, accessed through partner stores (Biople, Cosme Kitchen, independent natural-lifestyle retailers) and, on a pilot basis, Natural Lawson convenience stores.

## Partnerships

Retail hosting partners: Biople, Cosme Kitchen, Natural Lawson (convenience-store refill pilot from August 2020), and independent Japanese retailers. Corporate relationship: licensee of parent brand ecostore (New Zealand).

## Measurable Impact

Press coverage (2021) reported 45 refill stations in Japan at that time, up from an initial 8 stations at the August 2020 convenience-store launch; more recent company material describes "40+" partner locations. These figures come from trade press rather than a dated primary ecostore Japan disclosure and may not reflect the current exact count.

## Technology

In-store bulk-dispensing equipment for liquid products; no proprietary digital tracking or app-based system identified.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

ecostore Japan is a well-documented example of a refill-station network scaled through retail partnerships rather than dedicated stores, giving it wide geographic reach (Hokkaido to Okinawa) at relatively low capital intensity, including a notable convenience-store pilot (Natural Lawson) that tests refill viability in high-footfall, small-format retail — a format most refill operators have not cracked. As a licensee of an established 30-year parent brand with a credible refill track record, it merits inclusion as a distinct but connected profile to ecostore NZ.
