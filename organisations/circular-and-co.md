## Organisation Overview

Circular&Co. is a UK reusable-drinkware company founded in 2003 by Dan Dicker, a former Dyson designer, based in Cornwall. It manufactures reusable coffee cups and travel mugs made from recycled single-use paper cups, plus water bottles made from recycled stainless steel, selling both direct-to-consumer and as branded corporate merchandise. The company states its mission as making circular design the global standard for drinkware.

## Business Model

Circular&Co. sells reusable cups, travel mugs, and bottles manufactured using recycled input materials — notably reusable cups made partly from recycled single-use paper cups (diverting a waste stream into a new reusable product) and bottles from recycled stainless steel. Revenue comes from two channels: direct-to-consumer retail/wholesale sales and B2B corporate branding/merchandise contracts, distributed across the UK, Europe, and the US. The company has a documented commercial relationship with Starbucks (announced via press release, July 2020), and is UK-headquartered in Cornwall with global wholesale distribution.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

Circular&Co. is a well-established (20+ year) UK reusable-drinkware brand with a genuine circular-materials input model (recycled cups into new reusable cups) and a notable commercial partnership with Starbucks, giving it real-world commercial validation at meaningful retail scale. It is a solid example of product-level reuse and material circularity in the consumer drinkware category, though it operates in a well-populated market segment (reusable cups/bottles) rather than a novel reuse-system category.
