## Organisation Overview
Saponetti launched in 2016 from a garage as Toronto's first online refill delivery service, founded by Nikki Self and Christian von Seydlitz. By 2018 it added a Bloordale storefront, offering Canadian-made soaps and cleaning products delivered/sold in reusable jars and jugs.

## Business Model
B2C delivery and in-store refill model: customers receive or return reusable glass jars/jugs of soap, detergent and cleaning products; Saponetti collects, cleans and redistributes containers. Also operates a B2B channel (b2b.saponetti.ca) and a "Saponetti Essentials"/"Refill Depot" line.
