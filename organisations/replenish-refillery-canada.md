## Organisation Overview

Replenish Refillery & Zero Waste Store is a Canadian eco-friendly retailer at 9921 Main Street, Summerland, BC, offering bulk refillable cleaning, bath, and body products in-store, alongside biodegradable and non-toxic packaged goods shipped online for a zero-waste lifestyle.

## Business Model

The store runs a hybrid retail model: exclusive bulk refillable products (cleaning supplies, bath and body care) are sold in-store only, filled into customer containers, while pre-packaged eco-friendly, biodegradable, and non-toxic products are sold online with free shipping over $125. Revenue comes from in-store refills and online retail sales.
