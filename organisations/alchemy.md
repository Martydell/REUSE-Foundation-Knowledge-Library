## Organisation Overview

Alchemy is a Dublin, Ireland-headquartered company operating in the circular electronics market, managing the full device lifecycle from trade-in and reverse logistics through grading, data-wiping, repair, refurbishment, and remarketing of smartphones and other consumer electronics. Founded in 2017 by Gary Noone, James Murdock, and Walter Vicente, it has grown into what it describes as the fastest-growing company in the global circular tech market, operating facilities in 11 countries and selling refurbished devices in more than 60 markets. It works with major OEMs, carriers, and retailers to power trade-in programmes and resells refurbished inventory through its own consumer and B2B brands. Note: this "Alchemy" (wearealchemy.com) is unrelated to Alchemy Investments Acquisition Corp 1 (NASDAQ: ALCY), a separate SPAC entity that surfaces in search results under the same name.

## Business Model

Alchemy operates a portfolio of brands spanning the device reuse value chain: Alchemy Circularity/Recover provides trade-in and recommerce infrastructure for OEMs and retailers; Alchemy Trading and Callisto serve as B2B/wholesale marketplaces for bulk buyers and resellers of secondary-market tech; and Loop Mobile/Loop Business are consumer- and SME-facing storefronts selling graded, refurbished devices with CO2-savings certification. Revenue is generated by managing end-to-end trade-in, refurbishment, and remarketing services on behalf of OEM, retailer, and carrier clients (reported partners include Apple, Samsung, Best Buy, T-Mobile, Walmart, Amazon, Verizon, and eBay — see Partnerships for sourcing caveats), plus direct sales of refurbished devices to consumers and businesses. The company reports processing roughly 2 million devices annually and operating facilities in Ireland, the UK, the US, and other markets, employing an estimated 201–500 to 300+ people depending on source and date.

## Products & Services

Device trade-in programme design and execution; grading, data-wiping, and refurbishment of smartphones and electronics; wholesale/B2B remarketing (Alchemy Trading, Callisto); direct-to-consumer refurbished device sales (Loop Mobile); refurbished tech for SMEs (Loop Business); device protection and leasing services.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

Alchemy is a substantial, verifiable player in industrial-scale electronics reuse — its core business is trade-in, refurbishment, and resale of consumer devices at multi-million-unit scale, directly reducing e-waste through product-life-extension. The company's scale (11 countries, 60+ resale markets, hundreds of employees) and roster of major OEM/retail partners (subject to further direct verification) make it a strong example of industrial/product reuse infrastructure, distinct from packaging-reuse models more common elsewhere in this library. Impact figures are company-reported only, with some inconsistency across the company's own materials, which should be flagged to readers. The seed data's "5M+ devices" claim understates figures found in current company materials (12M+ reported); this should be corrected upward, with the caveat that no audited source confirms it.

**Priority Rating: ★★★★ High** (verifiable, large-scale industrial reuse operator; impact figures self-reported/unaudited)
