## Organisation Overview

Bio Pac, Inc. is a small, family-run American manufacturer of biodegradable, super-concentrated cleaning products (laundry detergent, dish soap, household cleaners), founded in 1991 by Collin Harris. The idea originated from Harris's experience volunteering at a recycling center in Maine, where he observed large volumes of discarded cleaning-product containers; Bio Pac's first bulk soap filling station opened at the Belfast Co-op in Belfast, Maine, in spring 1991. In 1996 the company acquired Oasis Biocompatible Cleaners from greywater expert Art Ludwig. Bio Pac remains 100% family owned and sells wholesale to natural food stores and co-ops across the United States.

## Business Model

Bio Pac manufactures super-concentrated, biodegradable cleaning products (laundry liquid, dishwashing liquid, all-purpose cleaners) sold in bulk to retailers — primarily independent natural food stores and co-ops — for in-store refill/bulk-fill stations, as well as in standard retail containers and via online marketplaces such as Amazon. The company operates a wholesale-to-retailer model rather than direct-to-consumer refill; it does not appear to operate its own retail refill stores, instead supplying formula concentrate to third-party stores that offer bulk dispensing to shoppers who bring or purchase reusable containers. Concentration reduces packaging and shipping volume relative to ready-to-use products. Bio Pac also donates a portion of profits to wilderness and watershed preservation groups, per its own site.
