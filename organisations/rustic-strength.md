## Organisation Overview

Rustic Strength manufactures concentrated, plant-based cleaning and wellness products free of phthalates, parabens, phosphates, SLS, and SLES, made at a family-run factory in the Ozarks. It supplies these products in bulk to a network of refill stations and retail partners across the United States, operating a "Close The Loop Program" for container recycling and sanitisation.

## Business Model

Rustic Strength runs a B2B wholesale platform, offering approved retail and refill-station partners up to 50% off retail pricing on bulk-size concentrated products, along with private-label options, custom scents, and retailer starter kits. Products are typically manufactured and shipped within the same week, supporting a fast-turnaround supply chain for refill retailers rather than selling directly to end consumers.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems (bulk concentrated products supplied to refill stations)
- Reusable Packaging (container recycling/sanitisation via "Close The Loop Program")
- Waste Reduction (concentrated formulas reducing packaging/shipping volume)

## Products & Services

Concentrated, plant-based cleaning and wellness products in bulk sizes for wholesale; private-label manufacturing; retailer starter kits; container take-back/sanitisation program.
