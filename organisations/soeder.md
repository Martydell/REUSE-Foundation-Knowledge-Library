## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2013 by Hanna and Johan, Soeder produces natural soaps and personal-care products in its own Zurich soap factory. It operates a "true closed-loop" refill model with over 130 refill stations across Switzerland, including flagship stores in Basel and Zurich-Stadelhofen railway stations.

## Business Model
B2C high-end personal care with refill infrastructure: customers refill in-store at partner locations or receive home refill deliveries in metal/recycled-plastic containers, alongside standard wholesale distribution; positioned as premium natural skincare with sustainability as a core differentiator.
