## Organisation Overview

Refill 'n' Refresh is a home, bath, and beauty destination in Prescott, Arizona, specialising in handcrafted soaps, lotions, and bath products with natural ingredients, operating as part of the US refillery movement that lets customers refill their own containers.

## Business Model

Refill 'n' Refresh sells handcrafted soaps, lotions, bath products, essential oils, lip balms, home fragrances, and shaving supplies through a bricks-and-mortar refillery and online shop. Customers can bring their own containers to refill liquid products (soaps, lotions), reducing single-use packaging, alongside packaged handcrafted goods; revenue comes from direct retail sales.
