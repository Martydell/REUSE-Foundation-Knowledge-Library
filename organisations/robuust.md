## Organisation Overview

Robuust is described as Belgium's first packaging-free shop, founded in August 2014 by Savina Istas in Antwerp. It sells organic, local, seasonal produce and bulk dry goods, dairy, and beverages without packaging, letting customers buy exact quantities using their own containers.

## Business Model

Robuust operates a single independent bulk-retail storefront where customers bring reusable containers or bags to purchase food and household goods by weight, eliminating packaging at the point of production and sale rather than only at the point of purchase. The shop's product range spans seasonal fruit and vegetables, dry staples (rice, pasta, sugar, tea, coffee), dairy, and beverages (beer, wine, natural juices), sourced with an organic and local emphasis.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems (bulk goods sold by weight, customer-supplied containers)
- Waste Reduction (packaging eliminated at the production/distribution level)
- Consumer Education (zero-waste shopping model)
