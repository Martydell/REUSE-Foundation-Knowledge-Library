## Organisation Overview

Your Sustainable Store is an online-only retailer founded in 2018 by Dawn Chen after she struggled to find affordable, accessible sustainable products in Singapore. It sells a curated range of reusable items — bamboo coffee cups, beeswax food wraps, silicone storage bags, bamboo toothbrushes and similar goods — aimed at making low-waste living convenient and budget-friendly.

## Business Model

The company operates purely as an e-commerce store (no physical retail presence, keeping overhead low) selling reusable, plastic-free lifestyle products sourced and curated by the founder, targeting budget-conscious Singaporean consumers who want sustainable alternatives without premium pricing. Revenue comes from direct online retail sales rather than a refill, rental or subscription model.
