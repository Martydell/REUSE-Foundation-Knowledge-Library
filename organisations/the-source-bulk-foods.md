## Organisation Overview

Australia's largest specialised bulk-food retailer, evolved from a Mullumbimby health-food store (Eden's Landing, acquired by founders Paul and Emma in 2007) into The Source Bulk Foods brand. Now a franchise network of 40-50+ stores across Australia, with additional locations in New Zealand and the UK, selling 400+ package-free wholefood and pantry items.

## Business Model

Franchise retail model — franchisees operate individually-owned stores under The Source Bulk Foods brand, typically on 5-year franchise agreements (renewable) alongside separately negotiated property leases; revenue comes from bulk, by-weight sales of organic wholefoods and pantry staples, encouraging customers to bring their own containers, supplemented by franchise fees to the parent company.
