## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2018 by Rachel Kois in Denver, Simple Switch is an online marketplace for ethical and sustainable shopping, vetting every supplier on manufacturing, labor practices, materials and packaging before listing home essentials, gifts and other everyday products.

## Business Model
B2C curated e-commerce marketplace: vets and lists third-party suppliers of sustainable/ethical goods, generating revenue via product sales; positions itself as a one-stop alternative to conventional retail for consumers seeking pre-vetted ethical options, and has been Climate Neutral certified since 2019.
