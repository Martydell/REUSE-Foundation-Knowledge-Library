## Organisation Overview
Ong Hut Co produces drinking straws from Lepironia articulata (grey sedge grass) harvested wild in Vietnam's Mekong Delta wetlands. The straws are biodegradable, chemical-free, and sold fresh or dried as a plastic-straw alternative.

## Business Model
The company harvests, cleans, and packages grass straws for sale in bundles (fresh or oven-dried), targeting cafes, restaurants and consumers seeking plastic-free alternatives. Straws are single-use/compostable rather than reusable, priced around US$0.02-0.04 each, and shipped internationally in banana-leaf wrapping.

Ong Hut Co is a genuine plastic-alternative innovator, but its product is biodegradable/compostable single-use rather than reusable, placing it at the edge of the REUSE Foundation's reuse-focused scope. Useful as a waste-reduction case study rather than a core reuse system.
