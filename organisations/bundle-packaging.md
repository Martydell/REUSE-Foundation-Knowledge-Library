## Organisation Overview

Bundle Packaging Limited is a small, privately owned UK packaging supplier headquartered in Huddersfield, West Yorkshire, founded in 2019. It provides custom-branded packaging — boxes, mailers, tape, tissue paper, stickers, and bags — to e-commerce and small-to-medium brands, positioning itself around sustainability, stating its materials are "fully recyclable and biodegradable" and plastic-free. The company was founded by individuals with a combined background in the luxury packaging industry. Company size appears small (2–10 employees per LinkedIn), serving a B2B customer base of e-commerce brands seeking custom unboxing experiences.

## Business Model

Bundle Packaging operates a B2B custom-packaging supply model, selling branded corrugated boxes, mailers, tissue paper, tape, stickers, kraft/paper bags, and tote bags to e-commerce and retail brands, from small stock-size runs to large custom orders. Its sustainability positioning centres on recyclability and biodegradability of materials and the elimination of plastic, rather than on packaging reuse: the company's own "about" content explicitly avoids overstating environmental claims, noting it does not make compostability or recycling assertions "not backed up by the infrastructure needed to support them" — a notably candid stance. However, no evidence was found of a reusable-packaging system, take-back scheme, or closed-loop reuse offering; the packaging sold is single-use (recyclable/biodegradable) rather than designed for multiple reuse cycles, which is a meaningful distinction from true "reusable packaging" businesses.
