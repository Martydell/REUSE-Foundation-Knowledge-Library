## Organisation Overview

Eco Tienda Verde is an ecological retail store based in León, Spain, at Avenida Padre Isla 107, operating both a physical store and an e-commerce site (ecotiendaverde.es). It sells certified organic and ecological products across Spain, including organic and vegan foods, natural cosmetics, bio hygiene products, eco-friendly household cleaners, medicinal plants/herbal supplements and aromatherapy products. The company holds ecological certification (registration ES-ECO-028-CL.724-0070674.2026.001) and offers some Demeter-certified (biodynamic) food products, alongside 24-48 hour delivery within mainland Spain and a 30-day return policy.

## Business Model

Eco Tienda Verde operates a hybrid bricks-and-mortar plus e-commerce retail model, selling certified organic/ecological groceries, natural cosmetics, household cleaning products and herbal/health items to consumers across Spain. Its business emphasises "fair trade consumption," sourcing of local and Spanish-origin products, pesticide-free/GMO-free standards, and stated "zero-waste practices," though the specific mechanics of any package-free bulk-buying or refill system (as referenced in the seed data) could not be independently confirmed from the pages reviewed — the company's own materials focus primarily on organic/ecological product certification rather than describing a formal refill or deposit infrastructure. As an independent regional retailer with more than two decades of background in the phytotherapy (herbal medicine) sector, its differentiation is largely around certified organic sourcing and health-food specialism rather than reuse-system innovation per se.
