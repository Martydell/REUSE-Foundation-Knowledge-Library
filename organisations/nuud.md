## Organisation Overview
Nuud is a Netherlands-founded (not Swedish, per seed data) natural deodorant brand created by Martijn van Seters in 2017. Its cream anti-odorant, packaged in a bioplastic sugarcane tube, uses microsilver and zinc oxide and is designed to last 3–7 days per application, sold globally with a UK-specific storefront.

## Business Model
Direct-to-consumer subscription and one-off e-commerce sales of its deodorant cream, with a subscription option offering a 10% discount and automatic reorder to reduce packaging waste from repeat single tube purchases. Distributed globally online plus retail partners.
