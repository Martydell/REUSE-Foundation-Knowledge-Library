## Organisation Overview
O Cantinho do AL supplies amenities, signage and complementary products to Portugal's "Alojamento Local" (short-term rental) and hotel sector, including branded product lines (Aroma de Portugal, Atlântico, Saudade, Luxury) with 5-litre refill formats for shampoo, shower gel, liquid soap and body lotion.

## Business Model
B2B e-commerce supplier selling directly to hospitality and short-term rental operators, with 5L bulk refill containers reducing the need for repeated small single-use amenity bottles. Also supports operators with compliance-related products for accommodation inspections. All products stated as Made in Portugal.
