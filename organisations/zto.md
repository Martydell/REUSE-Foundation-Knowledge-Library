## Organisation Overview

ZTO Express is one of China's largest express-delivery and logistics companies, operating a nationwide parcel sorting and delivery network. Alongside its core courier business, ZTO has deployed reusable packaging (bags, turnover baskets, cartons) at scale within its sorting centres to cut single-use packaging waste.

## Business Model

ZTO operates a franchise-partner express delivery network across China, handling billions of parcels annually for e-commerce and business customers, monetised through per-parcel sorting and delivery fees. As part of its sustainability programme, ZTO has invested in reusable bags, circular folding turnover baskets, and cardboard-carton recovery/reuse processes within its sorting centres, aiming to reduce reliance on single-use shipping packaging across its network rather than selling reuse as a separate product line.

## Products & Services

Express parcel delivery and sorting network services; internal reusable-bag and turnover-basket packaging systems; cardboard carton recovery and reuse programme.

## Customers

E-commerce platforms and merchants (e.g. Alibaba-linked sellers), business shippers, and consumers across China.

## Partnerships

Long-standing delivery partnership with Alibaba/e-commerce platforms; also referenced in logistics-distribution cooperation agreements with international e-commerce firms (e.g. Jowell Global).

## Measurable Impact

By end of 2025, cumulatively deployed 60.19 million reusable bags with 100% reusable-bag utilization at sorting centres; used recyclable packaging for over 25.22 million parcels and recovered over 146.52 million corrugated cardboard boxes for reuse (company-reported).

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

A very large logistics operator with meaningful, quantified reusable-packaging deployment at national scale — highly relevant as an industrial-scale reverse-logistics/reuse case study, even though reuse is a sustainability initiative within a much larger conventional delivery business.
