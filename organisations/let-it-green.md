## Organisation Overview

Let it Green describes itself as "la tienda zero waste" — a Mexican zero-waste store acting as a sales platform for products that are 100% plastic-free and environmentally friendly. It curates and sells goods from small Mexican producers sharing an environmental and social-responsibility ethos, including natural, vegan, plastic-free personal-care items, cruelty-free cosmetics with natural pigments, and reusable household alternatives such as silicone storage bags and bamboo cutlery sets.

## Business Model

Let it Green operates as a curated e-commerce and social-media-driven retail platform, sourcing and reselling products from small Mexican companies focused on plastic-free, biodegradable and fair-trade personal care and lifestyle goods. Rather than manufacturing its own products, it functions primarily as a marketplace/showcase for aligned local producers, supporting their market access while offering customers a single point of purchase for plastic-free living essentials — reusable bags, bamboo utensil sets, natural cosmetics and personal-care items free of parabens and synthetic colourants. The company supplements retail sales with environmental-education activities, stating that proceeds help fund digital content, events, workshops and conferences on environmental awareness, positioning it as much as an advocacy/education platform as a pure retailer.
