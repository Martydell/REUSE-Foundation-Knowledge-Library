## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2015 by Stephen Enzell (originally as MyGreenFills) after his son had a reaction to conventional laundry detergent, Truly Free Home makes non-toxic laundry and cleaning products sold via an online subscription: customers get reusable jugs/bottles once, then receive concentrated refill shipments to top them up.

## Business Model
Direct-to-consumer subscription model: first order includes reusable containers; subsequent shipments deliver only concentrated refills (diluted with water at home), cutting packaging and shipping volume versus repeatedly buying full pre-diluted bottles.
