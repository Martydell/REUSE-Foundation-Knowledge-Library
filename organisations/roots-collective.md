## Organisation Overview

Roots Collective is a Philippine curated marketplace, established in 2016, connecting conscious consumers with Filipino social enterprises and sustainable brands. It represents over 120 brands and nearly 4,000 products spanning food, personal care, fashion, home goods, books, and zero-waste items, through both a flagship Taguig City store and an e-commerce platform.

## Business Model

Roots Collective operates a curated online-and-physical marketplace model, sourcing products from social enterprises across 40+ Philippine provinces and providing them distribution, visibility, and wholesale procurement channels. Revenue comes from retail markups on curated products; the company also runs "The Spark Corner," a support initiative helping Filipino brand founders develop and scale sustainable businesses.
