## Organisation Overview
Founded by mother-daughter duo Kerry and Barbette, Suds Refillery started as a pop-up in May 2022 and opened a Shawnee, Kansas storefront that September. It refills soaps, detergents, lotions and cleaning products, encouraging repeated container reuse to reduce single-use plastic.

## Business Model
B2C refillery: customers bring reusable containers (or buy them in-store) and refill laundry detergent, hand/dish soap, body wash, shampoo and other liquids, avoiding new plastic bottle purchases each time; positioned around growing a local environmentally-minded consumer community.
