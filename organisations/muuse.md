## Organisation Overview

Muuse is a Singapore-based reuse-as-a-service company founded in 2018 after its co-founders witnessed takeaway packaging waste while surfing in Bali. It provides QR-tagged reusable cups and containers to F&B businesses as an alternative to single-use packaging, tracking each item through its lifecycle.

## Business Model

Muuse operates a B2B model, charging F&B outlets a flat monthly service fee covering the reusable packaging, logistics, washing and platform/tracking software. Borrowing and returning is free for end consumers, removing the need for a deposit since each item carries a unique QR code for tracking and inventory management. Revenue comes from client subscriptions rather than consumer fees.
