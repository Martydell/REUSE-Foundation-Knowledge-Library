## Organisation Overview

Natural Living is a Danish web shop selling aesthetic, sustainable everyday and wellness products, with an emphasis on reusable, plastic-free kitchen goods such as drink bottles, reusable coffee capsules, lunch boxes and household items. It ships using FSC-certified and recycled packaging materials.

## Business Model

Natural Living operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce model, curating and reselling third-party sustainable brands (kitchenware, cleaning products, home goods) rather than manufacturing its own reuse system. Values emphasised are trustworthiness, transparency and honesty, with fast (1-2 business day) Danish delivery.
