## Organisation Overview

Verde Market is a zero-waste grocery and bulk-foods store founded in Miami in May 2018 by co-owners Martha Balaguer and Pam Barrera, who saw a gap in the local market for package-free shopping. It now operates two Miami-area locations (Midtown and South Miami) stocking over 300 bulk food and plastic-free personal/home-care products.

## Business Model

Verde Market sells natural and organic groceries and household goods in bulk, letting customers bring their own containers (or use in-store paper bags/reusable containers) to fill only what they need, with pricing by weight deducted at checkout. Revenue comes from retail sales at its two physical Miami stores and via delivery partners; the model reduces single-use packaging compared to conventional pre-packaged grocery retail.
