## Organisation Overview

Mind the Trash is described as Portugal's first online zero-waste store ("1ª Loja online desperdício zero em Portugal"), founded in 2017 in Lisbon by Catarina Matos and Christian Andersen, with the brand's social media presence dating back to 2016 in London. The company sells sustainable alternatives across home goods, kitchen items, cosmetics, cleaning supplies, stationery, baby products, and personal care, both to individual consumers and via wholesale resale to shops nationally and internationally.

## Business Model

Mind the Trash operates an e-commerce retail model selling reusable, refillable, and otherwise zero-waste household and beauty products, combined with a B2B wholesale/resale arm supplying other retailers in Portugal and internationally. The company has pursued environmental credentials beyond its product range: it operates on Google's green (100% renewable energy) servers, redesigned its website in June 2023 to reduce CO2 emissions per click by over 50%, and holds Green Web Foundation certification.
