## Organisation Overview

Damn Plastic is an Austrian sustainability retailer founded by co-founders referred to on the company's site as Victoria and Steph, operating physical stores (Salzburg flagship, Linz, and additional locations), an e-commerce platform, B2B event consulting and corporate gifting services, and a franchise system extending into Slovenia. It sells curated "impact items" across beauty, household, accessories, kids, and travel categories, under a stated philosophy of "passive sustainability" — positioning itself as handling the sustainability effort so customers don't have to research it themselves.

## Business Model

Damn Plastic operates a multi-channel retail model: physical stores in Austria (Salzburg, Linz, plus franchise locations), a direct e-commerce platform, and B2B services including event consulting and corporate gifting. Products are curated around three criteria — made from waste, reduce waste, or produce no waste — while also supporting unspecified social projects. The franchise system has expanded the brand into Slovenia, and the company reports significant media/industry recognition (120+ press features, "4 Game Changers" winner, Startup of the Year via #TrendingTopics, Global Retail Business of the Year).
