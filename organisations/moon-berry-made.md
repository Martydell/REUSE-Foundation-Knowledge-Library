## Business Model

Moonberry Mercantile and Trading operates as a physical specialty-retail storefront (a "mercantile") in Tarpon Springs, Florida, curating natural and low-toxicity everyday goods: raw/unprocessed dairy, local meat, organic sourdough bread, apothecary/herbal items, and craft-kitchen products. Alongside its curated grocery and household goods, the store operates a refillery section allowing customers to purchase non-toxic household cleaning products by refilling their own containers rather than buying new single-use packaging each time.
