## Organisation Overview

Eco Eclectic is a small, independently owned sustainable retail business founded in 2020 by Sara Fagen in the Jacksonville Beach/Neptune Beach area of Florida. Founded to make sustainable living more accessible and affordable to her coastal community, the business combines an e-commerce platform, "microshop" physical pickup/self-service locations, and community events such as pop-up markets and children's clothing swaps. It markets itself as "Jacksonville's Zero Waste Shop."

## Business Model

Eco Eclectic operates a hybrid retail model combining online ordering with local delivery (Tuesday/Friday within 24 hours) and physical "microshop" locations that use self-service QR-code purchasing for local pickup, reducing overhead versus a traditional full-service storefront. Its core product line spans bulk refill cleaning and personal care products (dispensed into customer-returned or reusable containers), reusable household swaps (bamboo toothbrushes, beeswax wraps, sponges), and children's products, often sold as "mix & match" bundles. Beyond retail, the business runs community-facing programming — pop-up markets at local events and recurring clothing swaps (e.g., "The Little Clothing Swap" for baby/children's clothing sizes 0–5) — that build community engagement around reuse and waste reduction rather than pure product sales, positioning the brand as a local sustainability hub rather than solely a retailer.
