## Organisation Overview
Launched in May 2021 by Cosmetic Chemist and Holistic Nutritionist Terri Kirkham, The Eco Alchemist makes vegan hair and skin care manufactured on-site in Squamish, BC, with a boutique offering in-person refills alongside bulk sizes for wholesale partners.

## Business Model
B2C manufacturer-retailer: handcrafted vegan personal-care goods made at company HQ; sold online, at the Squamish boutique with refill options, and wholesale in bulk sizes to partner stores for in-store refilling.
