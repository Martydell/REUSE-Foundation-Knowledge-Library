## Organisation Overview

A Dutch clothing-swap retail concept founded in 2019 as a Rotterdam pop-up by Laura Suijkerbuijk and Monique Drent, now operating four stores across Rotterdam and Amsterdam where customers exchange used fashion items for store credit ("swaps").

## Business Model

Customers hand in clothing/fashion items and receive "swaps" (credit) based on item desirability, redeemable — alongside a per-item service fee — toward other secondhand items in-store, online or at events; also runs monthly repair/upcycling worklabs and sells upcycled clothing and tote bags made by local designers, diversifying revenue beyond pure exchange fees.
