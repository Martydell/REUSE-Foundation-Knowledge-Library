## Organisation Overview

Naber si was a package-free zero-waste retail store in Prague 7, Czechia, offering roughly 400 food products and 250 drugstore/cosmetic items sold without packaging. The website now displays "trvalé uzavřeno" (permanently closed), indicating the business has ceased operations.

## Business Model

Customers brought their own reusable containers (jars, bottles, bags) to purchase bulk food and drugstore goods by weight, avoiding single-use packaging. The store provided complimentary secondhand glass jars for customers without containers and also sold new ones, following a "5R" waste-hierarchy philosophy.
