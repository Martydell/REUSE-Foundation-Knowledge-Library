## Organisation Overview
The Clean Kilo opened 16 June 2018 in Birmingham after an Indiegogo crowdfunding campaign raising ~£20,000, billing itself as the UK's largest zero-waste supermarket, selling food, drink, toiletries and cleaning products by weight for customers to refill.

## Business Model
B2C zero-waste supermarket: sells groceries and household products loose by weight, letting customers bring reusable containers to buy exactly the quantity needed, avoiding pre-packaged single-use retail formats.
