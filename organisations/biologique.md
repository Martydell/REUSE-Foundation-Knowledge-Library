## Organisation Overview

Biologique is a South African direct-to-consumer company selling natural, toxin-free concentrated household cleaning products — all-purpose, kitchen, bathroom, laundry, dishwashing, pet-care and personal hygiene lines — designed around a refill model: customers buy an initial ready-to-use bottle, then purchase 200ml or 500ml concentrate refills that are diluted at home, extending the life of the original bottle. According to the company's own account, the founder, previously in hospitality management, started the brand after becoming a parent and developing an interest in toxin-free living. No independent press coverage, registry data, or third-party verification of founding date or ownership structure was found.

## Business Model

Biologique sells concentrated, "100% Natural" cleaning products online directly to consumers in South Africa, structured around a refill economy: an initial full-size bottle purchase is followed by lower-cost, lower-volume concentrate refills (200ml yielding roughly 13-26 diluted refills, 500ml yielding roughly 26-33, per product) that the customer dilutes into the original bottle at home. This reduces packaging and shipping volume per use compared to buying pre-diluted ready-to-use bottles repeatedly. The company markets a B2B channel supplying "plastic-free packaging stores," suggesting some wholesale distribution alongside direct e-commerce, though no named retail partners were confirmed. Products span household cleaning, laundry, dishwashing, pet care and hand hygiene categories.
