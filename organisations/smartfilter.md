## Organisation Overview

SmartFilter is the official Mexican distributor of Elkay drinking fountains and bottle-filling stations (including the ezH2O line), selling water filtration and hydration equipment to businesses, hotels, resorts, schools and public spaces to reduce reliance on bottled water.

## Business Model

B2B/B2C sales and installation of water fountains, filters and bottle-refill stations, targeting hospitality, corporate and public-space clients. Revenue from equipment sales, installation and likely maintenance/filter-replacement contracts.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems
- Waste Reduction

## Products & Services

Elkay-branded water fountains, sensor-activated bottle-filling stations, coolers and water filtration/purification systems for hotels, resorts, offices and public parks.

## Customers

Hotels, resorts, businesses and public/institutional clients across Mexico.

## Partnerships

Official distributor partnership with Elkay (US water fountain manufacturer).
