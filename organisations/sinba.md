## Organisation Overview

Sinba is a Peruvian social enterprise and Certified B Corp that collects food waste from restaurants and businesses and converts it, via a biotechnological process, into animal feed ("Punba") and fertiliser, while also recovering recyclable materials in partnership with informal recyclers.

## Business Model

Sinba provides a B2B waste-collection service to restaurants, hotels and households, charging for pickup while generating revenue from sale of the resulting animal feed and fertiliser products. It also runs circular-economy consulting, formalising the livelihoods of waste-picker associations it partners with.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Industrial Reuse

## Products & Services

Organic food-waste collection and conversion into animal feed (Punba) and fertiliser; inorganic recyclables recovery; circular-economy consulting for businesses.

## Customers

Restaurants, hotels, gastronomy businesses and households in Peru; corporate and public-sector clients for consulting services.

## Partnerships

Works with formal and informal recyclers' associations to recover inorganic recyclable materials (plastic, cardboard, glass, metal).

## Measurable Impact

Over 12,000 tons of waste repurposed and 60 recyclers formally enrolled over seven years of operation, with waste and associated pollution reduced by up to 90% for participating clients.
