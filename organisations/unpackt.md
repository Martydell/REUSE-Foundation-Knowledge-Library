## Organisation Overview
Unpackt is Singapore's first zero-waste bulk store, co-founded by Florence Tay and Jeff Lam. Customers bring their own containers, weigh them, and fill up with self-serve grains, pastas, oils, teas, and other bulk goods, avoiding single-use packaging entirely.

## Business Model
Bulk-goods retail social enterprise operating a physical store (Ang Mo Kio) where products are dispensed package-free from self-serve bins by weight, eliminating pre-packaged inventory and encouraging reusable container use.
