## Organisation Overview
Opened in December 2019 by twin sisters Tannis and Mara Bundi, The Green Jar is a Toronto eco-friendly refillery offering package-free personal-care and home goods in bulk, developing a bottle-deposit system during the city's first COVID lockdown to support online delivery.

## Business Model
B2C refillery with jar-deposit system: customers pay a small deposit on jars/bottles, refill them with bulk personal-care and home products, and return them for reuse — a model the founders adapted from sustainable retail practices seen in Switzerland, Germany and Jamaica.
