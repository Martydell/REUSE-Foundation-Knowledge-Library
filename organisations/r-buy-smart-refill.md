## Organisation Overview
R Buy Smart Refill is an Indian startup building a "refill-as-a-service" platform, deploying smart kiosks that refill home-care and laundry products into customers' own reusable containers. It is incubated at the Atal Incubation Centre, IIIT Hyderabad, and describes itself as building India's largest tech-enabled refill network.

## Business Model
R Buy places refill kiosks at residential and retail properties, partnering with home-care/laundry manufacturers who supply product in bulk rather than single-use packaging. Consumers locate and pay for refills via the R Buy mobile app; the model claims roughly 90% lower packaging cost for manufacturers and 10-30% savings for consumers versus packaged products.

## Products & Services
Smart refill kiosks dispensing laundry liquid, utensil cleaner, floor cleaner and toilet cleaner, paired with the "Smart Refill" mobile app for locating and paying at kiosks.

## Customers
Residential communities and retail locations in Bengaluru, expanding across India; home-care/laundry product manufacturers as supply partners.
