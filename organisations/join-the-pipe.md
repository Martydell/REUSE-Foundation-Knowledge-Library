## Organisation Overview

Join The Pipe is an Amsterdam-based non-profit foundation operating a social-enterprise model: it sells branded reusable water bottles (including sugarcane-material variants) and refill tap stations to hotels, events, municipalities, retailers, and offices, using that commercial revenue to fund water and sanitation projects — including refill infrastructure, water kiosks at schools, and hand-pump distribution in rural areas — primarily in developing regions of Africa and Asia. Its mission statement centres on "refill, reuse, reduce" to combat both plastic pollution and water-access gaps.

## Business Model

Join The Pipe's revenue model combines commercial product sales (branded reusable bottles, refill tap stations, water filtration systems sold to hotels, event organisers, municipalities, retail businesses, and offices) with non-profit programme delivery: proceeds fund water/sanitation infrastructure projects such as community water kiosks at schools and hand-pump installations in rural areas of developing countries. This dual commercial-plus-charitable structure lets the organisation both promote plastic-bottle reduction in wealthier markets (via its branded refill products) and directly fund water-access infrastructure in underserved regions.
