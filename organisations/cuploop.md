## Organisation Overview

Cuploop is an Estonian reusable-packaging company, founded by CEO Lauri Luik and CTO Christopher Juul, based in Tallinn. It operates "smart kiosks" using RFID technology that let customers deposit empty reusable cups and receive instant refunds via phone or card under a "Drop, Tap & Go" model, supported by a cloud-based inventory-management system (LoopMate®) and a monthly service package including automated deposit transactions and ESG reporting for client businesses.

## Business Model

Cuploop's core offering is B2B deposit-return infrastructure: vending-machine-style smart kiosks equipped with RFID scanning accept returned reusable cups (tagged with RFID adhesive labels) and instantly refund the deposit to the customer's phone or card. Client businesses (venues, retailers, etc.) subscribe to a service package that includes certified washing, integrated inventory scanning, cup packing/transport logistics, and kiosk management/monitoring, plus ESG reporting — positioning Cuploop as a full-service reuse-infrastructure provider rather than just a hardware vendor. Named partners/clients include Avery Dennison (RFID tagging materials), Mastercard and SEB (payment/refund processing), and Gebas, re-universe, and cirqle (sector partners, roles not detailed in sources accessed).
