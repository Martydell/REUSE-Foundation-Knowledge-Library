## Organisation Overview

Pizza 4P's is a farm-to-table pizza restaurant chain founded in 2011 in Ho Chi Minh City by Japanese entrepreneurs Yosuke Masuko and Sanae Takasugi. It has grown to roughly 32 locations across Vietnam, Cambodia, India, Indonesia, and Japan, and runs sustainability programmes including a jar return scheme and dedicated zero-waste restaurant sites.

## Business Model

Pizza 4P's operates dine-in restaurants emphasizing house-made ingredients (including its own dairy/cheese production) sold direct to diners. Its Jar Return Programme collects and reuses glass jars used for yogurt and pudding at Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi locations, and its Hikari Binh Duong and Cambodia sites operate as dedicated "zero waste" concept restaurants.
