## Organisation Overview
Oaklands Farm is a family-owned dairy farm near Nelson, New Zealand (owned by the Saxton/Raine family since 1842) that bottles fresh A2 milk in glass and sells it through home delivery, retail stockists, and self-service vending machines in Nelson, Tasman and Marlborough.

## Business Model
Vertically integrated dairy producer selling milk via three channels: home delivery, stockist swap-a-bottle points, and coin/card-operated vending machines dispensing into reusable 1-litre glass bottles that customers return and swap for credit — a deposit-return-style reuse loop replacing single-use plastic milk bottles.
