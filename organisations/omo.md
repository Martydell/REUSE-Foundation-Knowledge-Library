## Organisation Overview
OMO is Unilever's international laundry detergent brand, sold in Turkey under Unilever's "Dirt is Good" portfolio. It is a mass-market FMCG brand rather than a dedicated reuse company, with recent sustainability efforts focused on recycled-content packaging.

## Business Model
OMO operates as a conventional retail FMCG detergent brand manufactured and marketed by Unilever, sold through supermarkets and retailers across Turkey and other markets. Unilever has introduced packaging with recycled plastic content and concentrated formulas (e.g. "Wonder Wash") to reduce packaging and water use, but no independently verified refill-dispenser network in Turkey was found during this research.
