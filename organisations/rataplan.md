## Organisation Overview
RataPlan is a Dutch non-profit foundation, founded in 1982 (originally as Stichting Kringloopwinkel) in Den Helder, now operating over 40 thrift stores ("kringloopwinkels") across North Holland, South Holland and Flevoland. In 2024 it absorbed the Noppes thrift-store chain, expanding its footprint.

## Business Model
RataPlan collects donated second-hand goods (clothing, furniture, electronics, bicycles, toys, household items) and resells them through its retail stores, generating revenue that funds work-reintegration jobs for people with distance to the labour market. It carries the "100% Recycle!" quality mark for the Dutch thrift sector.
