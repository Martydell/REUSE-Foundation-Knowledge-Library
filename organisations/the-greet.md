## Organisation Overview
The Greet operates Korea's most extensive reusable-container sanitisation network under its "Drimm" brand — 38 standardised, QR-tracked container types for packaging, delivery, festivals, catering and funeral services — using a proprietary 7-stage sanitisation process developed with partners from Germany, Japan and the US.

## Business Model
End-to-end container-as-a-service: manufactures, distributes, collects, sanitises and redistributes reusable containers for retailers/cafés; its "Drimm Platform" synchronises QR-code data in real time to track resource circulation across clients, generating revenue through per-use service fees.

## Measurable Impact
300,000+ containers in daily circulation; 71.5 million cumulative container deployments (as of Dec 2024); carbon offset equivalent to 33,500 pine trees; Korea's first "Eco-Label" for reusable container services; selected as a Global Unicorn Project "Baby Unicorn."
