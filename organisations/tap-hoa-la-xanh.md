## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2018 by Phạm Ngọc Trâm Anh, a former flight attendant turned eco-entrepreneur, Tạp Hóa Lá Xanh is one of the three most prominent zero-waste refill shops in Saigon, selling dried food and condiments (rice, cereal, tea, pasta) without packaging.

## Business Model
B2C bulk grocery retail: customers bring their own containers to purchase unpackaged dry goods and condiments; the shop partners with other local eco-brands (period pads, candles) to broaden its zero-waste product range beyond food.
