## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2009 by Leslie VanKeuren Campbell, Sustain LA is a certified woman-owned social enterprise providing zero-waste event services and a refill station offering household cleaners, body-care products and bulk goods to Los Angeles-area customers.

## Business Model
B2C/B2B hybrid: retail refill station lets customers refill everyday products (laundry detergent, dish soap, powders) using their own bottles; separately, the business provides zero-waste consulting/services for events and businesses seeking to reduce waste.
