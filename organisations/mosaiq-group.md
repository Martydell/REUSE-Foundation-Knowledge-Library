## Organisation Overview

Mosaiq Group was formed in 2024 uniting five Italian luxury-packaging companies (Bulgarelli Production 1970, Valtenna 1963, Brand Label 2009, D'Auria 1966, CoRi Sacchettificio 1985), reaching €70M annual turnover with 300 employees, serving luxury brands including EssilorLuxottica, Gucci, Valentino, Fendi, Prada, and Dior.

## Business Model

Holding company combining five specialist packaging producers (boxes, shoppers, woven labels, tags, dust bags) for fashion/luxury sector; majority-backed by Arca Space Capital private equity, with founding entrepreneurs retained as partners.
