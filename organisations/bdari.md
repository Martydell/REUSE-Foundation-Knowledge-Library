## Organisation Overview

BDARI is a Malaysian household-goods brand launched in August 2020, evolving out of an earlier household-essentials business, AZ Homefix PLT, which had been operating in household products since early in the COVID-19 pandemic. BDARI's core product is a range of designer stainless-steel tiffin carriers (multi-tier food containers), sold as a sustainable alternative to single-use plastic bags and containers for meals, gifting and events, alongside a broader catalogue of reusable food containers, bottles, bags and cutlery. The company positions itself around reducing single-use plastic waste in Malaysia and promoting a "bring your own container" (BYOC) lifestyle.

## Business Model

BDARI operates primarily as an e-commerce retailer (via its own Shopify-based website) with an accompanying physical retail presence, described as having "over 10 nationwide points of sale" in Malaysia. It sells stainless-steel tiffin carriers in three tiers — Standard (SUS410 steel), Premium (SUS304 steel), and an insulated Deluxe variant that keeps food warm for up to five hours — alongside customisation options (personalised text, names, or designs) for individual and bulk/corporate orders. The company targets three customer segments in its own marketing: working professionals seeking sustainable packed-lunch solutions, female workers, and households with children. Revenue is generated through direct product sales, customisation/personalisation upsells, and bulk corporate or event orders; no subscription, deposit-return, or rental mechanism was identified — customers purchase and own the tiffin carriers outright rather than participating in a shared/returnable system.
