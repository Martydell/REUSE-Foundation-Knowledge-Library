## Organisation Overview

Saffron & Serai is a Kuala Lumpur-based enterprise producing handcrafted, plastic-free products, most notably reusable beeswax food wraps marketed as an alternative to cling film, alongside coconut bowls, batik bags, and other sustainable home goods.

## Business Model

Saffron & Serai sells directly to consumers through its e-commerce site, offering individual products and gift sets, and also serves business customers through corporate gifting, private-labelling, and wholesale programmes. Products are handmade, positioning the brand around durability and reuse rather than single-use alternatives.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Reusable Packaging (beeswax food wraps as a reusable alternative to cling film)
- Waste Reduction (plastic-free product range)
- Consumer Education (brand messaging around reducing single-use plastic)
