## Organisation Overview

MyEarth (trading as L'earth/Mylearth) is a Singapore-headquartered company producing bio-composite and green-technology packaging products, with award recognition at the WorldStar Packaging Awards (2015) and Asia Star Awards (2014). It also operates in Vietnam through a joint-stock subsidiary.

## Business Model

The company manufactures and sells biodegradable/bio-composite plastic packaging and food-service products, positioning itself around green technology and food-safe materials. Products are distributed through retail (e.g., NTUC FairPrice, Shopee) rather than a business-to-business reuse-service model.
