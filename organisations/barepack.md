## Organisation Overview

barePack is a reusable-packaging service founded in Singapore in 2019 by Roxane Uzureau together with co-founders Maximilien Mason, Valerian Fauvel and Clement Hochart, after Uzureau's idea for a reusable-container sharing model gained traction at a Singapore zero-waste event. The company built a membership-based system letting customers order takeaway and delivery food in reusable containers rather than single-use packaging. In February 2022, barePack was acquired by Swedish circular-packaging company &Repeat, which itself merged with Helsinki-based Kamupak in August 2023 to form a broader Nordic/international circular-packaging group; barePack now operates as part of that combined network. Seed data listed only a Facebook page, but the company maintains a proper website at barepack.co.

## Business Model

barePack provides returnable, reusable food-service packaging — branded FlexBox (rigid containers with silicone seals, microwave- and freezer-compatible) and KindCup (stainless-steel/silicone beverage cups) — integrated with food-delivery platforms including Foodpanda, GrabFood and Deliveroo, as well as direct restaurant partnerships. The service is free to registered members; non-members pay a deposit. Containers must be returned within 14 days to any of over 150 participating food-and-beverage vendors in Singapore, where they are collected, washed and recirculated. The company also runs a Corporate Programme offering in-office collection and logistics for business clients. Since its 2022 acquisition, barePack operates within &Repeat's wider circular-packaging infrastructure, extending reusable packaging to delivery platforms in Paris and Lyon, France, in addition to its original Singapore market.

## Products & Services

FlexBox reusable food containers and KindCup reusable beverage cups, a membership app for ordering and tracking returns, vendor-facing collection/washing logistics, and a Corporate Programme for office-based reuse and collection.

## Customers

Individual food-delivery and takeaway consumers in Singapore and (via &Repeat's network) France; participating F&B vendors and delivery platforms; corporate clients including named users PwC, Novartis and KPMG for the Corporate Programme.

## Partnerships

Foodpanda, GrabFood and Deliveroo (delivery platform integrations); over 150 F&B vendors in Singapore, including SaladStop! and Sufood; corporate clients PwC, Novartis and KPMG; parent/group company &Repeat (Sweden) and, via &Repeat's 2023 merger, Kamupak (Finland).

## Measurable Impact

Reported (source: plasticfree.com, year of underlying data not specified): containers designed for reuse "over 500 times" with claimed CO2 reduction of "up to 90%" versus single-use disposables; potential vendor savings of approximately $0.36 per order; and a claim that "100,000 disposable items can be saved per office of 50 people." A separate figure reported post-acquisition cites more than 70,000 users across the combined barePack/&Repeat network (source and year not independently specified). These are company- or press-reported figures rather than independently audited metrics.

## Technology

Membership-based mobile app for ordering in reusable containers and tracking returns; vendor-facing collection and washing logistics infrastructure operated in partnership with F&B locations.
