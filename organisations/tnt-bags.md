## Organisation Overview
TNT Bags manufactures reusable shopping bags made from non-woven (TNT/spunbond polypropylene) fabric in Peru, positioning them as a durable, water-resistant, multi-use alternative to single-use plastic bags for retail and promotional use.

## Business Model
B2B manufacturer/supplier: produces non-woven fabric bags for retailers and businesses seeking reusable-bag alternatives to plastic, selling in bulk/custom orders (branding, sizes) as an eco-alternative to disposable shopping bags.
