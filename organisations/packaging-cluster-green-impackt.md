## Organisation Overview
The Packaging Cluster is a Spanish packaging-industry association (working with 30+ entities across 19 countries) that leads Green Impackt, an EU-funded project connecting European sustainable-packaging solution providers with Colombian SMEs to accelerate their transition to circular plastics/packaging practices.

## Business Model
Green Impackt is a grant-funded technical assistance programme (not a commercial company) delivering training, matchmaking, company visits and international collaboration to roughly 150 Colombian SMEs, funded through the EU's AL-INVEST Verde programme to build capacity for circular, resource-efficient packaging practices in Latin America.

## Partnerships
Funded by the EU's AL-INVEST Verde programme; Packaging Cluster network of 30+ entities across 19 countries.

## Measurable Impact
By project end, 10 participating Colombian SMEs reported increased business activity directly attributed to Green Impackt involvement.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment
Green Impackt is a capacity-building/matchmaking programme rather than an operating reuse company; it is a useful ecosystem-development case for Latin America but has limited direct reuse-system relevance.
