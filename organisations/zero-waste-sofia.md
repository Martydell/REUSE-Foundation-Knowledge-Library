## Organisation Overview

Zero Waste Sofia is a Bulgarian sustainable-living initiative founded by corporate-communications professional Simona Stiliyanova(-Pavlova), aimed at reducing single-use plastic and bottled-water consumption. Its flagship project, the "Fountains of Bulgaria" map, catalogues around 1,000 public water fountains and refill-friendly venues nationwide.

## Business Model

Zero Waste Sofia is a volunteer-driven, non-commercial initiative rather than a retail business. It maintains a public, web-based (non-app) map of water fountains and venues — including around 39 restaurants — willing to refill customers' bottles with tap or filtered water for free. Hundreds of volunteers contribute new locations via a website submission form, and venues can request to be added.
