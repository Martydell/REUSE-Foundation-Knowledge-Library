## Organisation Overview

Green Upshot is a Lahore-based e-commerce startup founded in 2023, started, per its own account, "in a small garage" by an environmentalist founder after witnessing plastic pollution in local rivers and trees. The company sells eco-friendly, reusable lifestyle products — including bamboo toothbrushes and hairbrushes, reusable bottles and travel accessories (straws, cutlery, tumblers), and sustainable corporate gifting sets — shipping nationwide across Pakistan from its Lahore head office. It has grown into what it describes as one of Pakistan's fastest-growing eco-friendly brands, graduating from the National Incubation Center Lahore (NICL) and other startup programmes, and won the SEE Pakistan 2025 World Startup Championship in the Sustainability category.

## Business Model

Green Upshot is a direct-to-consumer and B2B e-commerce retailer of reusable and biodegradable lifestyle products — bamboo toothbrushes and hairbrushes, wheat-straw coffee cups, reusable glass tumblers, and reusable travel accessories (straws, cutlery) — alongside a wholesale channel supplying retailers and a corporate-gifting line for businesses and organisations seeking sustainable branded merchandise. All orders are processed, packed, and shipped from the company's Lahore head office, with free nationwide shipping in Pakistan above a Rs 4,000 order threshold. The company has been supported through Pakistani startup-incubation infrastructure, including graduation from the National Incubation Center Lahore (NICL) and involvement with the National Aerospace Science and Technology Park (NASTP), and has built partnerships with NGOs, universities, schools, and corporations for sustainability engagement and bulk/corporate orders. Recognition includes winning the Sustainability category at the SEE Pakistan 2025 World Startup Championship and inclusion in a "30 Under 30 Connected Pakistan" list.
