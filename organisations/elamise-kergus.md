## Organisation Overview

Elamise Kergus OÜ is a small Estonian company, registered in February 2018 and based in Viimsi (near Tallinn), that sells household, cosmetics and lifestyle products described as environmentally conscious, recyclable and durable. It operates primarily as an e-shop, supplemented by a small representative store in Viimsi, and ships customer orders in cardboard/paper packaging (avoiding plastic), including reused packaging materials where possible. The company has stated ambitions to expand sales into Latvia, Finland and other markets alongside its core Estonian customer base.

## Business Model

Elamise Kergus operates a direct-to-consumer e-commerce model, curating and reselling third-party "eco-living" branded household goods, personal-care items and compostable products (e.g., BioBag compostable waste bags) not typically stocked by mainstream Estonian retail chains. Its differentiation is stated as offering conscientiously selected products aimed at convenience and environmental preservation, combined with plastic-free, reused shipping packaging. Public Estonian business-registry data show the company's turnover fell from approximately €247,885 in 2021 to a forecast of €0 in 2026, with current liabilities reported as exceeding assets and its VAT registration having lapsed in April 2024 — indicating the business may be winding down or largely inactive at the time of this research, despite the online store domain remaining reachable.
