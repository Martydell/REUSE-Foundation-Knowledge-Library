## Organisation Overview
Z.W. Mercantile is a small eco-friendly general store based in Ames, Iowa, selling bulk and package-free cleaning, personal care, and pantry goods. It operates as one of the businesses housed within The Shops at Clark & Main, serving local and regional customers through in-store shopping and curbside pickup.

## Business Model
The store sells everyday household goods — cleaning supplies, personal care items, and pantry staples — in bulk format, encouraging customers to bring their own clean containers to refill rather than purchasing new single-use packaging. Revenue comes from in-store retail, online orders with curbside pickup, and shipping of select non-bulk items. It positions itself as a practical, affordable resource for low-waste living rather than a premium eco-boutique.
