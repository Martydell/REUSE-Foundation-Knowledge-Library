## Organisation Overview

Reuse Network is a UK registered charity (No. 1090824) that supports a national network of local reuse charities, helping them give donated items "a new lease of life" while alleviating poverty, reducing waste, and tackling climate change through coordinated local action and national support.

## Business Model

Reuse Network operates as a membership body: reuse charities join as members to access networking, knowledge-sharing, and coordination support, while a newer "Associate Membership" tier allows businesses, public sector bodies, and individuals to support the sector. It also runs a platform for finding and donating items and facilitating volunteering. Funding likely combines membership fees, donations, and grants.

## Customers

Member reuse charities across the UK; low-income households receiving reused goods; associate members (businesses, public sector, individuals) supporting the mission.

## Partnerships

Network of UK reuse charity members; associate membership programme for businesses and public sector organisations.

## Measurable Impact

Per its 2025 report: 83,262 tonnes of CO2 saved, 1,350,000 low-income households supported, and 77,470 tonnes of waste redirected from landfill.
