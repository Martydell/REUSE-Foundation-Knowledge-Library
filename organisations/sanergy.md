## Organisation Overview
Sanergy operates "Fresh Life" urine-diverting dry toilets across informal settlements in Nairobi, franchised to local entrepreneurs. Waste is collected regularly and processed at a treatment facility into organic fertiliser and insect-based animal feed, closing a sanitation-to-agriculture loop for smallholder farmers across Kenya.

## Business Model
Franchise model: entrepreneurs ("Fresh Life Operators") buy a subsidised toilet unit including installation and one year of daily waste collection, plus training. Sanergy transports collected waste to a treatment facility, combining it with sawdust and micro-organisms to produce WHO-standard fertiliser, sold to farmers, alongside insect protein for animal feed.
