## Organisation Overview

Green Pear Eco is a UK plastic-free retail business founded in 2019 by husband-and-wife team Dan and Gab, who were inspired toward eco-conscious living after a career-break backpacking trip. The couple launched a mobile bulk-refill delivery van in 2020 — funded, according to press coverage, by redirecting their wedding fund during the COVID-19 pandemic — to deliver plastic-free household and personal-care refills across the Essex/Suffolk/Cambridgeshire border area. The business has since grown to include a physical retail shop, "The Pear in Clare," alongside nationwide 5-litre bulk refill sales and local whole-food delivery.

## Business Model

Green Pear Eco operates a three-channel plastic-free retail model: (1) a local mobile refill delivery service covering the Essex/Suffolk/Cambridgeshire border area, where customers' own containers or purchased 5-litre cans are filled with household and personal-care products; (2) a physical shop, "The Pear in Clare," open seven days a week, selling plastic-free household and personal-care goods plus whole foods and gift boxes ("Pear Post"); and (3) an online store offering nationwide delivery of 5-litre bulk refill cans of products (alongside packaged whole foods such as organic tea, honey, chocolate, kombucha, and juice) for customers outside the local delivery radius. The mobile refill service began in 2020 when the founders purchased a van using funds originally set aside for their wedding, reflecting a bootstrapped, locally-rooted growth model rather than external investment. The company also offers refill services to local businesses. A small team (Dan, Gab, plus named staff Gee, Sophie, and Kelly) runs both the shop and delivery operations.
