## Organisation Overview

Sixth + Zero is a small-town zero-waste market and refillery that grew out of the original Sixth Street Soapery. It hand-makes soaps and skincare, and stocks bulk pantry staples and household goods for customers to refill using their own containers.

## Business Model

Direct retail sales through a physical store (and online shop) combining handmade soap/skincare production with a bulk refill station for pantry staples, personal care and household products. Revenue comes from product sales and refill transactions, plus a lending library encouraging customer education and repeat visits.
