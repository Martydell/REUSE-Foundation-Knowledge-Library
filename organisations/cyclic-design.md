## Organisation Overview

Cyclic Design is a Leipzig, Germany-based sustainable-design consultancy founded in 2019, specialising in sustainable packaging design and lifecycle analysis for the circular economy. Its best-documented product is Nepenthes, a capless, refillable personal-care bottle for hair and body-care products made from roughly 50% bio-based polyethylene derived from sugarcane, positioned as a space- and plastic-saving refillable packaging design.

## Business Model

Cyclic Design operates primarily as a design/consultancy service, developing sustainable packaging concepts, conducting lifecycle analysis, and creating reduced-impact product designs for clients pursuing circular-economy packaging strategies, rather than being a direct-to-consumer brand itself. Its flagship demonstrated product, Nepenthes, is a refillable hair/body-care bottle design that other brands could potentially adopt or license, serving as both a consultancy showcase and (potentially) a licensable/sellable packaging format. Specific commercial terms, client list, and revenue model were not confirmed in sources accessed during this research pass.
