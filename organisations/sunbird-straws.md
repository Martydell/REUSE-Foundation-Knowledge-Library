## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2020 by Christ University professor Saji Varghese and Chirag M G, Sunbird Straws makes biodegradable, chemical-free straws from fallen coconut leaves, previously burned as agricultural waste. Production is run at women-operated centres in Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu.

## Business Model
B2B/B2C manufacturer: sources discarded coconut leaves as free feedstock, processes them via steaming and rolling into multilayered straws, and sells to cafés, hotels and consumers as a plastic-straw alternative — creating rural women's employment in the process.

## Measurable Impact
Replaced ~2 million plastic straws; saved 10 tons of coconut leaves from burning; produced across 3 Indian states.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment
A well-documented material-recovery and social-impact venture, though the end product is single-use/compostable rather than reusable — relevant primarily for waste-stream diversion, not core reuse. ★★★ Medium
