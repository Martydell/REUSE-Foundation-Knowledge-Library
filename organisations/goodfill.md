## Organisation Overview

Goodfill is a Cape Town-based eco-friendly cleaning products startup that makes biodegradable, plant-based concentrated cleaning tablets designed to be dissolved in tap water inside a reusable spray bottle. The model avoids the environmental cost of manufacturing and transporting single-use plastic bottles filled mostly with water. Products are locally made in Cape Town and sold direct via the company's own website as well as through South African natural/organic retailer Faithful to Nature. No independently verifiable founding date, founder name, or company registration details were found in public sources.

## Business Model

Goodfill sells a "buy once, refill forever" system: customers purchase a reusable spray bottle once, then buy low-weight, low-volume tablet refill packs (e.g., a 6-tablet pack making 1.5L of All Purpose Cleaner) which dissolve in tap water. This avoids shipping the water content of conventional liquid cleaners, reducing packaging weight, transport carbon footprint, and single-use plastic bottle waste. The company sells directly via its own Shopify-based e-commerce site (goodfill.co.za) and through third-party retail partner Faithful to Nature, a long-established South African natural/organic goods e-commerce platform (founded 2006). Goodfill's "Homecare Collection" spans multiple cleaning categories (all-purpose, bathroom, and others), each following the tablet-plus-reusable-bottle format. No evidence of a physical retail storefront, refill-station network, or B2B/wholesale channel was found; the business appears to operate as a direct-to-consumer online brand with essential-oil-infused, plant-based formulations as a secondary differentiator alongside the packaging-reduction proposition.
