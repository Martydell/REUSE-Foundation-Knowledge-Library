## Organisation Overview

Aroundrs is an Italian startup, headquartered in Florence, that provides a reusable-packaging system for food takeaway and delivery. Conceived in 2020 during COVID-19 lockdowns when co-founder Giulia Zanatta and colleagues observed a surge in single-use takeout waste, the company was formally established in 2021 as a Società Benefit (an Italian benefit-corporation legal form combining commercial and social/environmental purpose). Aroundrs supplies durable, reusable containers — tracked through a mobile app — to restaurants, supermarkets, corporate cafeterias, and event caterers as an alternative to single-use packaging.

## Business Model

Aroundrs operates a business-to-business-to-consumer model described on its own site as "Reusable Packaging-as-a-Service." Partner businesses (restaurants, supermarkets such as CRAI chain locations in Turin, corporate cafeterias, caterers, and event/social canteens) pay Aroundrs for customised reusable-container packages; end consumers use the service free of charge via a mobile app, providing a credit card as guarantee against non-return, with a stated €10 charge applied if a container is not returned. Containers (lunch boxes, pizza boxes, deli boxes, cups, and other formats) are designed to be microwave- and dishwasher-safe and are stated to be usable roughly 200–300 times before being recycled. The company has received Tuscan regional/EU co-funding (referenced on its site as "POR FESR Toscana 2014-2020") and lists collaboration with Plastiz and local Tuscan institutions.
