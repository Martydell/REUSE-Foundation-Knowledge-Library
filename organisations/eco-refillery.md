## Organisation Overview

Eco Refillery is a bricks-and-mortar BYOB (bring-your-own-bottle) refill store located in Burnaby, British Columbia (Metro Vancouver), operated as a retail division of Eco Sanitizer Inc., a company founded by Matthew Nowak in 2020 that originally produced cruelty-free, plant-based cleaning and sanitizing products. The Eco Refillery storefront held its grand opening on June 24, 2022, offering proprietary in-store filling stations where customers refill and reuse their own containers with locally sourced and manufactured cleaning and personal-care products, explicitly aiming to reduce waste and costs while building a sustainability-focused community.

## Business Model

Eco Refillery operates a single physical refill storefront in Burnaby, BC, where customers bring their own bottles (or purchase/reuse store containers) to refill household and personal-care liquids — hand soap, dish soap, laundry detergent, hair care and hand & body products — dispensed from proprietary filling stations. As a retail extension of its manufacturing parent, Eco Sanitizer Inc. (and its wholesale brand "Eco Company"), the refillery has a vertically integrated supply chain: the parent company manufactures and distributes the cleaning/sanitizing products that are then sold through the refill storefront, differentiating it from refillers that source products from multiple third-party brands. The business also retails hand sanitizers, wipes, masks and gloves, reflecting its origins in sanitizing-product manufacturing. Its combination of local manufacturing plus direct-to-consumer refill retail is intended to reduce both packaging waste and product cost versus single-use bottled equivalents, per its stated mission.
