## Organisation Overview
Zero Waste Delivery is a Guildford-based refill company that delivers household products directly to customers' doors using zero-emission electric vans, avoiding single-use plastic packaging. It serves London and the South East with van delivery and offers UK-wide courier delivery as an alternative.

## Business Model
Customers order refillable household products (hand wash, shower gel, cleaning products, laundry, haircare, dishwashing liquid, toilet roll, foods, and candles) via the company's website; orders are delivered either in reusable pre-filled bottles or refilled directly from the van, with free electric-van delivery across the local London/South East area and free courier delivery to the rest of UK mainland. The company also offers a 10% discount to NHS workers.
