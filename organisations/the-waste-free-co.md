## Organisation Overview
Founded by Estefania Coral (originally from Colombia, moved to Sweden 2017), The Waste Free Co installs refill stations for food, snacks and household goods in supermarkets, hotels and offices across Sweden, letting customers buy only the quantity they need in reusable packaging.

## Business Model
B2B2C model: the company supplies and services refill/dispensing stations placed in partner venues (retail, hospitality, workplaces), earning revenue from station leasing/servicing and product refills; recently expanded into "waste-free snack stations" for office environments via a partnership with property company Castellum.
