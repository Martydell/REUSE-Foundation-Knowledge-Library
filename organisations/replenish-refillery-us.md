## Organisation Overview

Replenish Refillery is a zero-waste boutique in Dover, New Hampshire, describing itself as "a shop, a teacher, a community builder, and a support system." It sells low-waste, non-toxic household and personal-care goods and offers refill-by-weight services alongside community education events.

## Business Model

The refillery combines retail sales of sustainable household and personal-care products with a refill service, where customers bring or buy reusable containers to fill by weight, reducing single-use packaging. Revenue is generated through in-store retail and refills, supplemented by community workshops and educational programming on low-waste living.
