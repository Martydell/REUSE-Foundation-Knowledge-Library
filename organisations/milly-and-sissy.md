## Organisation Overview

Milly & Sissy is a UK skincare brand founded by two friends with backgrounds in beauty-industry product management and healthcare, incorporated as Milly&Sissy Ltd on 16 August 2019. The company's core innovation is selling personal-care products — hand wash, body wash, and shampoo — as powders in compostable sachets, which customers mix with water at home in a reusable bottle rather than buying pre-mixed liquid in plastic packaging each time. This "use and refill" powder format is designed to cut plastic waste and transport emissions (powders are lighter and more compact than liquids) across its home and personal care product range.

## Business Model

Milly & Sissy sells directly to consumers online, plus through UK eco-retail stockists (e.g., andKeep, Natural Collection, Biodegradable Life, Earth Lover). Its core proposition is a one-time purchase of a reusable bottle, followed by repeat sales of compostable powder refill sachets that customers mix with water at home. This model shifts packaging weight and volume from liquid-filled plastic bottles (shipped repeatedly) to lightweight powder sachets, reducing shipping carbon footprint and plastic use per wash. Revenue is generated from both starter kits (bottle plus initial sachets) and ongoing sachet refill sales, positioning the brand within the broader "concentrate-at-home" segment of the refill industry alongside similar powder- and tablet-based personal care brands.
