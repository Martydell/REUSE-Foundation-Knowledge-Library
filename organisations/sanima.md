## Organisation Overview
Sanima (rebranded from X-Runner) provides urine-diverting dry toilets and weekly waste collection to low-income households in Lima informal settlements lacking sewer access. Collected waste is composted off-site using thermophilic and microbial treatment, producing soil-safe fertiliser meeting international pathogen-safety standards.

## Business Model
Households pay a monthly fee for toilet installation and weekly collection service. Waste is transported to a treatment facility and composted with efficient micro-organisms; the resulting compost is tested to Chilean/Austrian safety standards and used to enrich agricultural soils.
