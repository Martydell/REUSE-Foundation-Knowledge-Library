## Organisation Overview
Zemito is an online zero-waste store based in Slovakia selling ecological products aimed at reducing household waste. Its catalogue spans natural cosmetics, refillable coffee capsules, reusable bottles, and home composting equipment, positioning itself as a curated resource for customers adopting a zero-waste lifestyle.

## Business Model
Zemito operates as an e-commerce retailer curating and reselling third-party eco-friendly and refillable products — including reusable stainless-steel coffee capsules, reusable bottles (glass, stainless steel, and plant-based plastic), natural cosmetics, and home composters. It generates revenue through direct online sales to Slovak and regional consumers, supplemented by educational content to build customer engagement.
