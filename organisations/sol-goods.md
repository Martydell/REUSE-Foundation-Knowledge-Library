## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2019 by Steph Wood after a surf trip inspired the search for a naturally protective sunscreen, Sol Goods makes reef-safe, tested mineral sunscreens packaged in reusable/recyclable aluminium tubes and glass, and is developing a bulk dispensing solution for refill stores.

## Business Model
B2C sunscreen manufacturer selling through direct-to-consumer and stockist channels; sustainability strategy centres on 100% recyclable/reusable aluminium and glass packaging, with an in-development bulk-dispensing refill format for retail refill stations rather than a fully operational refill system today.
