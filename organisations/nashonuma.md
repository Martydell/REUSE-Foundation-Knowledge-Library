## Organisation Overview

Nashonuma describes itself as Pakistan's first environment-friendly, eco-driven e-commerce store, delivering biodegradable, compostable and reusable products nationwide, including reusable stainless-steel and bamboo straws and sustainable food packaging out of Lahore.

## Business Model

Nashonuma operates as a direct-to-consumer and B2B e-commerce store, selling reusable products (steel/bamboo straws, stainless-steel bottles) alongside compostable and biodegradable food packaging (bagasse bowls, kraft boxes, paper straws) and offering food-packaging services to businesses in Lahore.
