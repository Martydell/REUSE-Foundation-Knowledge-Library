## Organisation Overview

Wasteupso is a community-based zero-waste grocery and goods shop in Seoul, founded in summer 2018 by American expatriate Kychele Boone. It sells food, home goods, and zero-waste products with minimal packaging, located at 4F, 189 Bangbae-ro, Seocho-gu, near Naebang Station.

## Business Model

Customers bring their own containers to shop package-free in-store, or order online with goods packaged in donated, sterilised glass jars or paper bags. Members donate jars/containers to keep the loop low-cost, and a "Circle Service" lets customers ship their own containers to the shop and receive a discount upon return. The shop also monetises via paid zero-waste consultation services for businesses/schools (75,000 KRW/hour) and homes (50,000 KRW/hour).
