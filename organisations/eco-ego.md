## Organisation Overview

ECO EGO is a Polish brand of household cleaning tools — brooms, mops, buckets and related accessories — manufactured by YORK PL, a Polish company based in Bolechowo near Poznań. The brand is built around closed-loop-economy principles: products are made with a minimum of 60% recycled raw material (using certified regranulate under RecyClass/EuCertPlast and GRS/Global Recycled Standard schemes), and the manufacturing facility recycles 100% of its own polypropylene production waste back into new products. Rather than being a refill-liquid retailer, ECO EGO's "refill" concept centres on durable, modular cleaning tools (e.g. its CIRCULAR mop system) whose consumable components — such as mop heads — can be replaced/refilled independently of the whole tool.

## Business Model

ECO EGO manufactures and sells durable household cleaning equipment — sweeper and dustpan sets, the "CIRCULAR POWER" cleaning kit (bucket with wringer, flat mop, twist-on handle), the "U-COLLECT" telescopic broom, and "CIRCULAR" flat-mop refill heads — designed on an ecodesign/closed-loop basis. Its core sustainability proposition is materials-based: products are manufactured with a minimum 60% recycled polypropylene content, certified via independent schemes (RecyClass/EuCertPlast, GRS), and 100% of the company's own manufacturing plastic waste is recycled back into production. The "refill" element of the product line is a modular-tool design (replacing only the wearing part, e.g. a mop head, rather than the whole tool), which reduces material consumption over a tool's lifetime versus disposing of an entire mop or broom. This differs materially from bulk-liquid "refill" retail (the seed's description); ECO EGO's proposition is closer to durable-goods design-for-longevity plus verified recycled-content manufacturing than to a consumer refill station.
