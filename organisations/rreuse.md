## Organisation Overview

RREUSE is Europe's largest network of social enterprises active in reuse, repair, and recycling, established in 2001. It supports member organisations engaged in refurbishing, repairing, and recycling materials (notably furniture, textiles, WEEE, and mattresses) across Europe, and advocates for supportive EU circular-economy policy.

## Business Model

RREUSE operates as a membership-funded non-profit advocacy and research network rather than a commercial or direct-service operator. It publishes research on social enterprises' roles in sectors like furniture and mattress circularity and public procurement, maintains a member network map, advocates on EU policy (e.g., the Circular Economy Act), and facilitates member-exclusive events, working groups, and B2B partnership opportunities between social enterprises and commercial entities. It became a member of Social Economy Europe in 2026.
