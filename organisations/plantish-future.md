## Organisation Overview

Plantish Future is a Vancouver, Canada-based small business selling vegan, plant-based, plastic-free products for the home and bathroom, including dish brushes, solid dish soap, shampoo/conditioner bars, and bamboo hair brushes. It describes itself as a queer-owned company focused on everyday plastic-free swaps.

## Business Model

Plantish Future sells plastic-free household and personal-care goods direct to consumers online and via marketplaces such as Etsy and Faire (for wholesale). Products emphasize compostable, biodegradable, and reusable materials as substitutes for single-use plastic items.
