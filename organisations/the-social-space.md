## Organisation Overview

A Singapore social enterprise combining a café, retail store, nail salon and "Refillery" refill station, founded by Daniel Yeow and Cheryl Ou to promote low-waste living alongside inclusive employment practices.

## Business Model

Multi-concept social enterprise generating revenue across café, retail and nail-salon services at Kreta Ayer and Duo Galleria locations; the in-house "Refillery" sells natural castile soaps, shampoo and cleaning products via bring-your-own-container dispensers, complementing the broader retail offering. Inclusive hiring is a stated part of its social-enterprise mission.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems (Refillery dispensers for soap, shampoo, cleaning products)
- Reusable Packaging (customer-supplied containers)
- Consumer Education

## Products & Services

Refillable castile soaps, shampoo and household cleaning products via in-store dispensers; also operates a café and eco-conscious retail/nail-salon concept.
