## Organisation Overview

LaverVert is a Belgian manufacturer of natural, ecological household cleaning products, handcrafted in a workshop in Namur. Its core products are built around a signature black soap made from locally sourced rapeseed oil, extended into a range of multi-purpose cleaners for laundry, gardens, terraces, natural stone and general home use. The company holds certified artisan/craftsmanship status and promotes transparency in ingredients, alongside DIY recipes so customers can make cleaning products at home.

## Business Model

LaverVert manufactures and sells natural cleaning products directly through its own online shop (laververt.com/en/shop) and through retail partners in Belgium. Rather than a single-format retail model, LaverVert supports reuse through refillable packaging: at least one retail partner, Nature for Kids, offers LaverVert laundry liquid in refillable "Jean-Bottle" containers under a deposit system as part of a stated zero-waste retail policy. This positions LaverVert as a manufacturer supplying both standard retail packaging and refill-compatible formats, rather than operating its own dedicated refill stations. The company's "made in Belgium" and small-batch/artisan positioning is central to its brand, targeting environmentally conscious consumers who prioritise local, transparent manufacturing over mass-produced cleaning brands.
