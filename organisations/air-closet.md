## Organisation Overview

airCloset, Inc. is a Japanese fashion-technology company founded in 2014 and headquartered in Tokyo, operating an online clothing rental subscription service in which professional stylists select apparel for individual subscribers based on stated preferences and usage data. The company has since expanded into adjacent services including airCloset Fitting (styling advice for purchases), airCloset ABLE (in-person styling storefronts), airCloset Mall (rental/trial of household and lifestyle products), airCloset Dress (occasion wear) and airCloset Salon. airCloset listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Growth Market on 29 July 2022 (stock code 9557).

## Business Model

airCloset's core revenue model is subscription-based clothing rental: customers pay a recurring monthly fee to receive a curated box of clothing items (styled by human stylists using customer preference data and, per company materials, AI-assisted curation), wear them, and return them for the next selection — extending the useful life of each garment across many subscribers rather than each item being purchased and discarded by a single owner. The company has diversified revenue through adjacent services: airCloset Mall (manufacturer-authorised rental of higher-value lifestyle goods such as appliances and bedding), airCloset Dress (rental for weddings/ceremonial occasions, including a partnership with wedding-venue operator Anniversaire), and in-person styling consultation. As a listed company, airCloset reports financial results on a July–June fiscal year to the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Rental — subscription clothing rental extending garment lifecycle across multiple users
- Sharing — shared-wardrobe model for apparel and (via airCloset Mall) household goods
- Reverse Logistics — return, cleaning and re-circulation of rented garments between subscribers
- Consumer Education — company communications framing its model within circular-economy/SDG goals

## Products & Services

airCloset (core monthly clothing rental subscription for women), airCloset Fitting, airCloset ABLE (styling storefronts), airCloset Mall (lifestyle-goods rental), airCloset Dress (occasion-wear rental), airCloset Salon (styling/diagnosis services), and newly launched airCloset Men's (May 2026) and Chill ST streetwear rental (July 2026).
