## Organisation Overview

Little Plant Pantry is described as the first plastic-free, plant-based zero-waste bulk grocery store in Amsterdam, located at Bosboom Toussaintstraat 45 in Amsterdam West. It sells 100% vegan food ingredients — dried beans, legumes, grains, nuts, seeds, coffee and teas — with minimal or no packaging, alongside personal care and cleaning products and fresh items such as vegan cheese, nut milks and baked goods. Customers bring their own containers and bags to reduce packaging waste, and the store partners with local artisan food producers and prepares some items in-house. It also operates a small cafeteria area serving vegan pastries and meals.

## Business Model

Little Plant Pantry operates a single-location, independent retail model combining bulk/package-free grocery sales with an in-house vegan cafe. Customers purchase dry goods (grains, legumes, nuts, seeds, coffee, tea) and select personal-care/cleaning products by bringing their own containers, reducing packaging at the point of sale, while fresh items (vegan cheese, nut milks, baked goods) and light meals are sold through an attached cafeteria. The business partners with local artisan producers for supply and emphasizes in-house preparation for some products, positioning itself as both a sustainable shopping destination and a community gathering space for Amsterdam's vegan/zero-waste community. There is no public evidence of multi-site expansion, franchising, or external investment; it operates as a single independent shop.
