## Organisation Overview

RECUP & REBOWL is a German deposit-based reuse system for takeaway coffee cups (RECUP) and food bowls (REBOWL), founded in September 2016 by Fabian Eckert and Florian Pachaly, piloted in Rosenheim near Munich before expanding nationwide.

## Business Model

Customers pay a returnable deposit (€1 for a RECUP, €5 for a REBOWL) at any partner location, use the reusable container, and return it — to the same or any other partner location — to reclaim the deposit. reCup GmbH earns revenue by supplying cups/bowls and reverse-logistics/washing infrastructure to partner cafés, restaurants, and retailers, who pay to join the network rather than buying disposable packaging.

## Products & Services

RECUP reusable coffee-to-go cups and REBOWL reusable food containers, distributed via a nationwide deposit-return network with cross-location return capability.

## Customers

Cafés, bakeries, restaurants, company canteens, kiosks, and petrol stations across Germany; end consumers who use the deposit system.

## Partnerships

Over 13,000 partner distribution/return points across Germany (cafés, restaurants, bakeries, company canteens, petrol stations).

## Measurable Impact

Not independently quantified in tonnes/units in sources reviewed, though the network scale (13,000+ points) indicates substantial reach; company has received industry recognition (German Brand Award, "Best Purpose of the Year" 2023).

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

RECUP & REBOWL is one of Europe's most mature and widely deployed deposit-return reuse systems for food/drink packaging, with proven cross-location interoperability at national scale — a strong reference case for deposit-based reuse infrastructure.
