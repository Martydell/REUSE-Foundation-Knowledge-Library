## Organisation Overview
Packoorang manufactures reusable, waterproof shipping mailer bags made from recycled plastic bottles and recycled clothing, intended to replace single-use cardboard boxes and plastic mailers across e-commerce supply chains.

## Business Model
Brands order mailers from Packoorang for their shipping volumes; after delivery, customers return used mailers to nearby collection points, where Packoorang cleans and re-circulates them, supporting 100+ reuse cycles per mailer at roughly 0.10 EUR per use.
