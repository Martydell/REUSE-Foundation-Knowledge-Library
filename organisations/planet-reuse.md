## Organisation Overview

Planet Reuse, launched in September 2022, describes itself as the first European online platform and network for reusable packaging, connecting FMCG brands, retailers, reuse system operators, packaging suppliers, municipalities, NGOs, and academia. It is powered by the LoftOS Foundation and the Netherlands Institute for Sustainable Packaging (KIDV).

## Business Model

Planet Reuse operates as a knowledge and networking platform rather than a commercial product company: it hosts a database of reuse suppliers and solutions, a knowledge space, events, and working groups to help organizations find partners and scale reusable packaging systems. It is backed by strategic partners including Zero Waste Europe and the Dutch Packaging Waste Fund (Afvalfonds Verpakkingen).
