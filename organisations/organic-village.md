## Organisation Overview
Organic Village is an organic grocery retailer in Bangkok, selling fruits, vegetables, meat, dairy and pantry staples sourced from Thai producers, with both a physical store and nationwide online delivery.

## Business Model
The company sells organic groceries via an online shop and physical store, offering bulk purchase options (e.g. rice, grains) and a monthly subscription model to cut costs and reduce packaging. A dedicated "household" product line references zero-waste and sustainability positioning alongside the core grocery business.
