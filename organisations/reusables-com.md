## Organisation Overview

Reusables™ is a Vancouver-based B2B platform providing enterprise-wide reusable container programs for campus dining and other high-volume foodservice environments, using patented "Tap to Reuse™" technology to let users borrow containers by tapping a credit card or student ID.

## Business Model

Reusables™ eliminates deposits and app downloads by enabling frictionless container checkout/return via tap-to-pay technology, paired with 24/7 smart return stations that automate charging and refunds. Clients can lease or purchase containers, or supply their own inventory; Reusables™ integrates with existing payment and dining systems. Revenue comes from platform/technology licensing and container program contracts with institutions such as universities.
