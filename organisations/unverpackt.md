## Organisation Overview
Founded by Sara Wolf and Milena Glimbovski, Original Unverpackt opened in February 2014 as Germany's first unpackaged supermarket. Customers bring their own containers and dispense food, cleaning, and beauty products from gravity bins with no single-use packaging.

## Business Model
Zero-waste grocery retail model: a physical Berlin store (plus online shop) stocking several hundred package-free products, dispensed to customer-supplied containers by weight, positioning itself as the original template for Germany's package-free shop movement.
