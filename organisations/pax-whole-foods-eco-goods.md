## Organisation Overview
PAX Whole Foods & Eco Goods is a Westport-based retailer offering refills of nearly 400 organic foods alongside eco-friendly cleaning and personal-care products, described as one of Ireland's largest minimal-waste shops. It works with over 75 Irish businesses.

## Business Model
Customers bring their own jars and bottles (or use provided containers) to refill bulk/organic groceries, cleaning products, and personal-care items in-store or via online/delivery orders sold in weight-based increments (e.g., 100g units). The shop also offers business services and nationwide delivery across Ireland.
