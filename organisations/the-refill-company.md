## Organisation Overview

An independent refillery in Thunder Bay, Ontario, offering bulk personal-care and household refills sourced from Canadian and local suppliers. Customers bring their own containers, which are weighed before and after filling so they pay only for the product used.

## Business Model

Single-location bricks-and-mortar refill store; revenue from bulk-refill sales of shampoo, conditioner, dry shampoo, shower gel, body lotion, bubble bath, clay mask, bath salts, dish soap, all-purpose cleaner and castile soap, weighed via a tare-weight system. Also serves as a community collection point (Earthub for pill bottles; TerraCycle for hard-to-recycle items), adding a take-back/material-recovery service layer.
