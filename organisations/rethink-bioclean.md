## Organisation Overview

ReThink BioClean is a Regina, Saskatchewan-based company providing zero-waste commercial cleaning and janitorial supplies via a mobile refill service. It operates across Western Canada, delivering eco-friendly cleaning products directly to businesses without single-use containers.

## Business Model

ReThink BioClean runs a subscription-based B2B mobile refillery: businesses call in and a mobile unit refills their cleaning and janitorial product containers on-site within hours, eliminating single-use plastic packaging. It also offers commercial kitchen installation, equipment maintenance and repair, specialized "Hot Zone"/"Cold Zone" cleaning protocols, and commercial dishwasher services, targeting hospitality, restaurant, brewery, and janitorial clients across 7+ locations in BC, Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba.
