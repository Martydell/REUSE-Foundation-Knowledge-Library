## Organisation Overview
reCIRCLE AG is the Swiss company that founded the reCIRCLE reusable-packaging deposit system in Bern in 2016, starting with 24 partner businesses. It has since grown to around 2,000 participating Swiss businesses (as of ~2024) and expanded the reCIRCLE brand into Germany (2020), and the Netherlands, Denmark, Estonia and Italy (2021).

## Business Model
Food-service partners (restaurants, cafes, retailers such as Coop, which adopted the system nationwide in Switzerland in 2019) offer takeaway meals/drinks in reCIRCLE's durable, stackable PBT boxes and double-walled cups. Customers pay a CHF 5-10 deposit, returned when the container is brought back to any partner location or exchanged for a fresh one; reCIRCLE manages container supply, and the network handles collection/washing.

## Products & Services
Stackable reusable menu boxes (five sizes, shallow/high rim options) and double-walled reusable cups for drinks/soups, distributed through a nationwide partner network.

## Customers
Approximately 2,000 Swiss restaurants, cafes and retailers (including Coop supermarkets, which rolled out the system nationwide in 2019), and their end consumers.

## Partnerships
Coop (nationwide Swiss rollout, 2019); parent/originating entity for the wider reCIRCLE European network, which includes reCIRCLE Denmark and licensed operations such as reCIRCLE Ireland (via VOICE Ireland).

## Measurable Impact
In 2021, the network saved over 14 million disposable packaging items and an estimated 1,400 tonnes of CO2; grew from 24 partner businesses in 2016 to roughly 2,000 by 2024.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment
The founding and largest operation of a proven, large-scale reusable takeaway deposit system with strong retail partnerships (Coop) and multi-country expansion — a leading reference model for reusable food-service packaging.
