## Organisation Overview

BROS is a Malaysian brand of reusable water bottles and food containers, founded in 2004 by Terence and Zoe, who began by selling colourful reusable bottles from a small kiosk in the One Utama shopping mall. Now operated by Living Wing Sdn Bhd and based in Petaling Jaya, Selangor, BROS markets itself as "The Number 1 Brand of Refillable Water Bottles in Malaysia," selling across multiple product lines in stainless steel and plastic, including a children's range. Since 2010, the company has run the "BROS 321 Trade-In Program," allowing customers to exchange old reusable bottles or containers of any brand for a discount on new BROS products.

## Business Model

BROS sells reusable water bottles and food-storage containers direct-to-consumer via its own e-commerce site and, historically, physical retail presence, across a range of product series varying by material (stainless steel, plastic, glass) and design. Its most distinctive circular-economy feature is the "321 Trade-In Program," running since 2010, under which customers can trade in used reusable bottles or containers — of any brand, size, or material, excluding single-use/disposable plastic bottles, alcohol bottles, and non-beverage containers — for up to 50% off new BROS products. The company supplements this with a loyalty points programme (BROS Rewards) and periodic collaboration/takeover events with other brands (e.g., a reported "BROS x FORGEO" trade-in takeover) to extend the reach of the trade-in mechanic.

## Products & Services

Reusable water bottles (multiple series: Crystal, Flass, Tugo, Capa, Luna, Hana, Flip, Slide) in stainless steel and plastic; food-storage containers in plastic, stainless steel, and glass; children's product line (BROS KIDS); personalization/name-printing services; corporate/collaboration customization.
