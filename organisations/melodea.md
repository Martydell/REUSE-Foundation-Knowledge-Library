## Organisation Overview

Melodea develops water-based barrier coating technology — including MelOx NGen (an ultra-high oxygen barrier) and VBseal (a high water-vapour barrier) — designed to make packaging 100% recyclable while maintaining performance, targeting food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetics industries. The coatings are PTS and CEPI certified for sustainability. The seed tracker's description of these coatings as enabling "reuse" is not strongly supported — the verified focus is recyclability and barrier performance, not a reuse-loop mechanism.

## Business Model

Melodea licenses or supplies its cellulose-based barrier coating technology to packaging manufacturers and brand owners (B2B), replacing conventional plastic or non-recyclable barrier layers with a water-based, cellulose-derived coating that keeps the resulting packaging within standard paper/fibre recycling streams. This is a materials-innovation business model — Melodea does not operate consumer-facing products or a collection/reuse system itself, but supplies enabling technology to other companies' packaging.
