## Organisation Overview
Swapp! is a closed-loop retail concept combining refilling-station vending machines, a mobile app, reusable containers and e-commerce, deployed at Carrefour, Auchan and Superpharm locations in Warsaw, Wrocław and Kraków, selling FMCG products like cosmetics and cleaning liquids.

## Business Model
B2C refill vending: customers bring a clean container (or buy one on-site) and refill it with cosmetics/cleaning products at a vending machine partnered with major retail chains (Carrefour, Auchan, Superpharm); customers pay only for the product, not the packaging, differentiating from standard retail pricing.
