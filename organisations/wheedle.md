## Organisation Overview

Wheedle is an online marketplace, operated by ReEarth Tech Private Limited, for buying and selling pre-loved (secondhand) baby and kids' products in India. Founded in 2021 by Snehal Chaitanya and Harshada Joshi and based in Bengaluru, it raised a seed funding round in October 2023 led by Startup India.

## Business Model

Wheedle operates a consumer-to-consumer online marketplace where parents list and buy used baby/kids items — including toys, furniture, travel accessories, sports/outdoor gear, and maternity products — extending product lifecycles and reducing new-purchase demand. Revenue is generated through the marketplace platform (listing/transaction mechanics per standard resale-marketplace models); a "Sell" listing flow is available directly on the site.
