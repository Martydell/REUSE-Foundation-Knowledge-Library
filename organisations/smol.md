## Organisation Overview

smol is a UK direct-to-consumer subscription brand founded in 2018 by ex-Unilever employees Paula Quazi and Nick Green, delivering concentrated laundry capsules, dishwasher tablets and cleaning products in plastic-free, letterbox-friendly packaging.

## Business Model

Subscription-based DTC model shipping pre-measured, concentrated cleaning products directly to households on a recurring schedule, eliminating retail packaging waste and single-use plastic bottles. Revenue from recurring subscription sales; has raised over $34M in VC funding to scale.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Packaging-as-a-Service
- Waste Reduction

## Products & Services

Laundry capsules, fabric conditioner, dishwasher tablets, surface sprays and hand sanitiser, delivered via subscription in 100% plastic-free, child-lock packaging.

## Customers

UK households subscribing directly via smolproducts.com.

## Measurable Impact

Claims to power 1.5 million washes per week and to save over four tonnes of plastic waste weekly compared with conventional detergent brands.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

A large-scale, well-funded UK subscription brand demonstrating packaging waste reduction at significant volume; strong commercial proof point for concentrate/refill-style subscription models, though it eliminates rather than reuses packaging.
