## Organisation Overview

REWE Group is a major German diversified retail and tourism cooperative headquartered in Cologne, founded in 1927 as an auditing association of purchasing cooperatives. It operates supermarket chains (REWE, PENNY) across Germany and parts of Europe, and select stores have piloted in-store refill stations for bulk dry goods, cleaning products, and other items as part of broader waste-reduction efforts.

## Business Model

REWE Group operates as a cooperative federation of independent retailers pooling purchasing power, alongside company-operated supermarkets, discount stores (PENNY), and a tourism division (DER Touristik). Its core revenue comes from grocery and general retail sales; refill stations represent a sustainability-focused pilot/expansion within select stores rather than the company's primary business model, aimed at reducing packaging waste for shoppers.
