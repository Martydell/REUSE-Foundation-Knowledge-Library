## Organisation Overview
Sustenir is a Singapore-founded (2013) agri-tech company growing nutrient-rich leafy greens and superfoods in climate-controlled vertical farms, with R&D/production facilities also established in the UAE. It reduces food miles via urban indoor farming, not through a packaging reuse or refill system.

## Business Model
B2B/B2C vertical-farming operator: builds and operates indoor climate-controlled farms in partnership with local governments (Singapore, UAE), selling fresh, pesticide-free produce with minimal transport distance rather than operating any refill/reuse packaging system.
