## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2020 by Wing Chan, Shiran Zheng and Mary Wang, Sourceful helps brands build more sustainable supply chains, starting with branded packaging, via a platform offering custom packaging design, supply-chain management tools and real-time carbon-footprint data.

## Business Model
B2B SaaS/marketplace hybrid: brands use Sourceful's platform to source, design and manage sustainable packaging with visibility into carbon impact; company has raised $32.2M from investors including Index Ventures, Coatue Management and Eka Ventures.
