## Organisation Overview
Unpackaged Eco makes cleaning and soap products in Melbourne and distributes them through zero-waste online deliveries to serviced postcodes plus refill station installations at partner retailers, including Ritchies SUPA IGA locations.

## Business Model
Direct-to-consumer online ordering with local delivery to selected Melbourne postcodes (customers return containers for refill or receive package-free delivery), combined with wholesale refill-station placements inside partner supermarkets.

## Products & Services
Refillable household cleaning products and soap, zero-waste local delivery, in-store refill stations at partner supermarkets.

## Customers
