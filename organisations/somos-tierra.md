## Organisation Overview
Somos Tierra began around 2015 and first sold at market in August 2016, making solid cosmetics (solid shampoo, personal care) before "zero waste" was a known trend in Mexico. The founder grows many of the plants used in products at Rancho el Cardo Santos, Estado de México.

## Business Model
B2C artisanal manufacturer of solid personal-care products (biodegradable/reusable containers instead of liquid plastic bottles), sold direct and via retail partners, emphasising self-cultivated plant ingredients and socially-responsible sourcing rather than a refill/return system.
