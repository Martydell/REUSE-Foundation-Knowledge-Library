## Organisation Overview
Founded by Anie Rouleau, The Unscented Company makes all-natural, fragrance-free household cleaning and body care products designed for refilling at home or in-store, reducing single-use plastic packaging. It gained wider attention in 2017-2018 after being featured on Gwyneth Paltrow's Goop platform.

## Business Model
Consumer packaged goods company selling unscented, biobased cleaning and personal care products in refillable formats (including bulk refill boxes up to 10L), distributed via retail partners, refilleries, and its own online store in Canada and the US.

## Products & Services
Unscented all-purpose cleaner, dish soap, laundry and body care products, sold in refillable bottles and large-format refill boxes (800ml to 10L).
