## Organisation Overview
The Green Tap is a Cape Town zero-waste refill store that has refilled "thousands of bottles" over roughly five years of operation, offering wholesale refills of cleaning and personal-care products, plus white-label options for businesses wanting branded natural refillable products.

## Business Model
B2C/B2B hybrid: retail refill store lets customers refill cleaning/personal-care containers; a wholesale/white-label arm supplies businesses with natural refillable products under their own branding, extending reuse infrastructure beyond direct retail.
