## Organisation Overview

Refill Mill is a UK refill shop (physical store in Ongar, England, plus online delivery/pickup) selling package-free organic pantry foods, eco-friendly cleaning products, and natural beauty essentials, founded by a research scientist to help customers reduce single-use plastic.

## Business Model

Refill Mill sells goods via in-store refill (customers bring or buy containers, pay by weight for pantry items) and online ordering with delivery, click-and-collect, or in-store pickup. Products span package-free dried foods (brown paper bags), refillable cleaning liquids, and plastic-free beauty items such as bamboo brushes and vegan lip balms, each vetted by the founder's scientific background.
