## Organisation Overview
Thanks to (덕분애) is a Seoul-based zero-waste platform run by KG Traders Inc. under representative Lee Yun-kyung, offering a vegan-friendly zero-waste shop and café with bulk refills of soaps, shampoos, detergents, oils, vinegars, grains and snacks.

## Business Model
B2C zero-waste retail/café hybrid: bulk refill of personal-care and household products in reusable customer packaging, combined with a vegan café component, positioned within Seoul's broader zero-waste shop ecosystem (alongside Wasteupso, Jigu Shop and others).
