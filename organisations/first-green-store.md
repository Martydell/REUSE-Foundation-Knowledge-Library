## Organisation Overview

The First Green Store is a Singapore-based, women-led retailer specialising in sustainable, ethical, and eco-friendly promotional products for socially responsible companies. Its best-known product line is the Sprout Pencil — described as the world's first plantable pencil, replacing the standard eraser with a seed capsule — alongside recycled-newspaper pencils and other eco stationery. The company partners with schools to promote conscious-consumption awareness alongside its retail business.

## Business Model

The First Green Store sells sustainable stationery and promotional products, primarily targeting businesses seeking eco-friendly branded/promotional merchandise (B2B) alongside general retail sales. Its flagship product, the Sprout Pencil, replaces the typical single-use eraser with a plantable seed capsule, so the pencil can be planted at end-of-life rather than discarded — a material-substitution/end-of-life design feature rather than a reuse-loop mechanism. The company also partners with brands transparent about their design/manufacturing processes and runs school outreach programmes on conscious consumption.
