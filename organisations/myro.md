## Organisation Overview

Myro launched in fall 2018 as a direct-to-consumer refillable deodorant subscription brand, amassing a 16,000-person waitlist at launch. The company ceased operations in 2022, citing unsustainable costs and ongoing supply chain issues, and issued refunds to active subscribers.

## Business Model

Myro sold a reusable deodorant case paired with recurring subscription refill pods shipped direct-to-consumer, reducing packaging waste versus disposable deodorant sticks. The subscription model was designed to build repeat purchase habits around a durable, reusable primary container.
