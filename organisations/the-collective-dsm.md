## Organisation Overview
Owned by Jamie, a Des Moines native, The Collective is a sustainable-living shop and bulk refillery in Highland Park, Des Moines, offering vegan and ethically-made low-waste goods, including natural body-care products from brands like Plaine Products.

## Business Model
B2C bulk refillery and boutique retail: sells natural ingredient household and personal-care products (including third-party refillable brands), positioning itself around a broader sustainable-living philosophy rather than solely waste reduction.
