## Organisation Overview
No Tox Life is a US-based (not UK as seed data suggested) vegan personal-care and home-cleaning brand manufacturing in a solar-powered North American facility. It focuses on plastic-free formats and has extended into refillable products, including a refillable deodorant with a low-plastic refill capsule.

## Business Model
Direct-to-consumer e-commerce brand selling shampoo bars, cleaning concentrates, and refillable personal-care items such as deodorant. Revenue comes from product sales via its own site and retail/zero-waste store partners; sustainability is a core brand differentiator versus conventional single-use plastic personal-care products.
