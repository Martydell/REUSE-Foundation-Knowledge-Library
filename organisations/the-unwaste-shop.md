## Organisation Overview

A Chicago (West Loop) zero-waste store and online shop offering plastic-free, package-free products, including a dedicated in-store refill station and a range of refillable/plastic-free oral-care items such as bamboo dental floss in reusable containers.

## Business Model

Combined bricks-and-mortar (Chicago storefront with in-store Refill Station) and e-commerce retailer selling reusable/refillable everyday products; revenue from product sales plus refill top-ups; also runs a TerraCycle-based take-back program for hard-to-recycle oral-care waste (e.g., old plastic floss containers), adding a material-recovery service layer.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems (in-store Refill Station)
- Reusable Packaging (reusable floss containers, refillable bulk items)
- Take-back (TerraCycle oral-care recycling box)
- Material Recovery
- Consumer Education

## Products & Services

Plastic-free household and personal-care products; refillable bamboo dental floss in reusable bamboo/glass containers; in-store bulk refill station; TerraCycle recycling drop-off for oral-care waste.
