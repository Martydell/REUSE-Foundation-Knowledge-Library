## Organisation Overview

Zero Waste on Wheels is a Maidstone-based mobile refill service that visits customers by van to refill their own containers with household and personal-care essentials, avoiding single-use packaging. It launched to help people access plastic-free refills during COVID-19 self-isolation and has continued as a local delivery-refill business across Maidstone and surrounding Kent areas.

## Business Model

Customers submit a refill request via the company website, are contacted to arrange a delivery slot, and the van visits to refill their own clean bottles/containers on-site, charging by weight of product dispensed. Products include cleaning products, toiletries, shampoo/conditioner/body wash, cooking oils, coffee, dried/loose foods and soap bars. Payment is accepted by card, mobile pay, cash or invoice.
