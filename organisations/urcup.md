## Organisation Overview
UrCup provides a deposit-based reusable cup system for events, festivals, and catering in Poland. Customers pay a small deposit to "borrow" a durable, dishwasher-safe cup, return it at any partner outlet, and UrCup handles washing, storage, and redistribution.

## Business Model
B2B service model: UrCup produces and rents branded reusable cups (0.3-0.5L) to event organizers, festivals, offices, and hospitality venues, then manages the full deposit-return loop — collection, industrial washing, and cup redistribution — as a turnkey service without requiring extra venue staff.
