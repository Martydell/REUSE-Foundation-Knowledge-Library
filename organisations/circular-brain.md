## Organisation Overview

Circular Brain is a Brazilian digital platform ("Circularidade como Serviço" / Circularity as a Service) coordinating reverse logistics, residential and corporate e-waste collection, trade-in programmes, and traceability for the electronics industry. It connects brand manufacturers, collection points, and recycling operators through a management platform (Plataforma Circulare), reporting a network of 17,000+ collection points and 38+ recycling-operator partners as of 2024. Customer brands include major appliance and electronics manufacturers such as Whirlpool, Gree, Ventisol, Hytera, and Tectoy.

## Business Model

Circular Brain operates as a B2B digital-infrastructure provider rather than a consumer-facing brand: manufacturers and retailers of electronics/appliances contract with Circular Brain to manage the reverse-logistics side of extended producer responsibility — residential collection, corporate e-waste disposal, dedicated collection points ("Eletroponto"), and trade-in programmes — coordinated through its Circulare software platform, which also handles data-privacy protection for traded-in devices. Revenue comes from service contracts with brand manufacturers who need to meet Brazilian e-waste/circular-economy regulatory or sustainability commitments. The company is a signatory to the UN Global Compact and listed in the Ellen MacArthur Foundation's Circular Startup Index.
