## Organisation Overview

Neat (branded "neat." and also referred to as "Neat Essentials") is a UK-made, plant-based refillable cleaning-products company. It replaces single-use bottles with reusable aluminium spray bottles and small concentrated refills, aiming to cut plastic and water waste from shipping.

## Business Model

Neat sells starter kits containing a reusable aluminium bottle plus concentrated 30ml refills that customers dilute with water (470ml water + 30ml refill = 500ml of cleaner). Products (hand wash, surface cleaner, washing-up spray, dishwasher tablets, toilet cleaner) are sold direct-to-consumer and via zero-waste retail partners.

## Products & Services

Refillable home-care range: hand wash, body wash, surface cleaner, washing-up spray, dishwasher tablets and toilet cleaner, built around a reusable aluminium spray bottle.

## Customers

UK consumers buying direct online and through retail partners such as Zero Wasted, Peace with the Wild, ecoLiving, Plastic Freedom and REGN.

## Partnerships

Stocked by multiple UK zero-waste/eco retailers (Zero Wasted, Peace with the Wild, ecoLiving, Plastic Freedom, REGN, Buy Me Once). Not publicly available in further detail.

## Measurable Impact

Since 2020, reports having helped save 374 tonnes of CO2 and prevented 2,070,000 litres of water from being shipped, by concentrating refills and removing water weight from transport.
