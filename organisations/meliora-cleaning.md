## Organisation Overview

Meliora Cleaning Products is a Chicago-based, woman-owned B Corp founded in 2013 by Kate Jakubas (COO) and Mike Mayer (President), manufacturing eco-friendly cleaning products — laundry detergents, oxygen brighteners, home cleaning sprays/scrubs, and personal-care soaps — at its own West Side Chicago factory. The company is distinguished by its "All Ingredients Listed, Right on the Label" policy, disclosing 100% of ingredients, and successfully lobbied for California's SB-258, making California the first US state to require ingredient labeling on cleaning products.

## Business Model

Meliora manufactures and sells cleaning and personal-care products direct-to-consumer (with subscription options) and via wholesale accounts, offering both standard packaged products and refill formats — refill tablets and bulk buckets that reduce packaging per repeat purchase. All manufacturing occurs in-house at its Chicago facility, and the company states it pays living wages to its workers. Its policy-advocacy work (California SB-258) extends its impact beyond its own product line into industry-wide ingredient transparency standards.
