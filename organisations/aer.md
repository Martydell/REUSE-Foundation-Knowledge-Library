## Organisation Overview

Aer is an Austrian consumer-goods company selling home-cleaning and body-care products as concentrated, water-soluble powders packed in paper sachets, intended to be mixed with water in a reusable bottle rather than shipped as pre-filled liquid. The company, registered as aer GmbH, was founded in 2019 by Michael Bartenstein and Fabienne Hierzer (per company materials) and manufactures its products in Austria. Aer positions the model as eliminating single-use plastic bottles and the water weight normally shipped in liquid cleaning products. The brand sells direct-to-consumer online across several European markets.

## Business Model

Aer's core model is "powder-in-paper, reuse-the-bottle": customers buy a starter set that includes a reusable glass or plastic bottle plus a supply of powder refill sachets (kitchen cleaner, bathroom cleaner, glass cleaner, laundry, dish soap, body-care lines). Refill sachets are reordered and the same bottle is reused indefinitely, removing the need to ship or discard a new plastic bottle with every purchase. Because the product is shipped dry, in compostable cellulose sachets, the company markets a large reduction in shipping weight and associated carbon emissions compared with conventional liquid refill or pre-filled products. Revenue is direct-to-consumer e-commerce through theaer.com, with region-specific currency/language storefronts indicating sales across multiple European countries beyond Austria. No verified information was found on wholesale/retail stockists, funding rounds, or revenue.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems — powder sachet refills mixed into a reusable bottle by the consumer
- Waste Reduction — compostable cellulose sachet packaging in place of single-use plastic bottles
- Consumer Education — brand messaging built around "less is more" and eliminating unnecessary water transport/plastic

## Products & Services

Refillable home-cleaning powders (kitchen/multi-surface cleaner, bathroom cleaner, glass and mirror cleaner, laundry detergent) and body-care powders, sold as starter sets (bottle + initial sachets) and standalone refill packs/trios.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

Aer is a small, founder-led Austrian brand whose core proposition — replacing shipped liquid product and single-use plastic bottles with a lightweight powder-and-reusable-bottle system — is directly relevant to reuse and waste-reduction goals. Its manufacturing is domestic (Austria) and its "95% less carbon" and compostable-packaging claims are plausible for a powder-format product but are unaudited and self-reported.
