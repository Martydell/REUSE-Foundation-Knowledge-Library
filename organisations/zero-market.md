## Organisation Overview
Zero Market México is a 100% Mexican-owned bulk store business dedicated to promoting responsible, sustainable consumption of natural and ecological products. Aligned with the global Zero Waste movement, it operates physical stores in Mexico City plus bazaar/distributor presence, selling bulk foods and plastic-free personal care alternatives.

## Business Model
Zero Market sells bulk dried foods and package-free personal care products (shampoo, creams, soaps) alongside reusable alternatives to disposable plastics (e.g., bamboo toothbrushes, cloth feminine pads). Customers bring their own containers or purchase reusable ones in-store. Revenue comes from direct retail sales across its Mexico City locations (including Coyoacán and G.A.M. branches), distributor relationships, and bazaar appearances.
