## Organisation Overview

MuvMi was founded in 2016 by Krisada Kritayakirana, Pipat Tangsiripaisan and Supapong Kitiwattanasak, launching its ride-hailing app for electric tuk-tuks in 2018. It operates a fleet of 600+ electric tuk-tuks across roughly 12 Bangkok neighbourhoods, connecting riders to mass-transit hubs.

## Business Model

MuvMi runs an app-based, on-demand ride-sharing service using electric tuk-tuks, with route planning, fare-splitting and passenger-driver matching. It generates revenue from per-ride fares and also offers monthly rental tuk-tuks for businesses. The model is built around shared, electric last-mile transport rather than packaging or product reuse.
