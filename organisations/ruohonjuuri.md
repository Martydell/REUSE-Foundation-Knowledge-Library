## Organisation Overview

Ruohonjuuri is a Finnish retail chain specialising in organic food, natural cosmetics, and health products, tracing its origins to 1982 when its founders began importing recycled paper into Finland. It now operates 16 physical stores, online shops in Finland and Sweden (Happy Food Store), and offers certified natural cosmetics with refill options.

## Business Model

Ruohonjuuri sells organic groceries, wellness products, and natural cosmetics through physical retail stores and e-commerce, supported by a loyalty programme and content marketing (magazine, blog). Its sustainability positioning includes refillable cosmetics and household product lines alongside certified organic and vegan/allergen-friendly ranges, targeting environmentally conscious Finnish and Swedish consumers.

## Circular Economy Activities

## Products & Services

Organic food and groceries, natural cosmetics and skincare, wellness supplements, and eco-friendly household/cleaning products, including refillable cosmetics lines, sold via 16 Finnish stores and online (Finland and Sweden).
