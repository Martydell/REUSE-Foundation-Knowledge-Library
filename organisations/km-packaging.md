## Organisation Overview

KM Packaging Services Ltd. is a UK flexible-packaging manufacturer founded in 1984, based in Oundle, near Peterborough, England. The company supplies lidding films, skin films, pouches, bags, foils and flow-wrap packaging to food manufacturers across sectors including prepared meals, fresh produce, meat/poultry/seafood, bakery, confectionery, dairy and plant-based protein, operating through proprietary brands (KPEEL®, KSEAL®, KSKIN®, KFOIL®, KRESEAL®, KNATURE®) and a compostable "C-Range." The company converted to an Employee Ownership Trust structure to secure long-term independence. Its sustainability focus centres on paper-based (KNature®, >85% paper content, kerbside recyclable), compostable and mono-material/PCR-content packaging rather than reusable/returnable container systems. KM Packaging is a member of WRAP's UK Packaging Pact.

## Business Model

KM Packaging operates as a B2B flexible-packaging manufacturer, converting film and paper substrates into printed, sealed packaging formats (lidding films, skin films, pouches, flow-wrap, foils) sold directly to food and consumer-goods manufacturers across five continents. Its commercial model centres on material substitution and design-for-recyclability — offering customers paper-based barrier packaging (KNature®), compostable bio-plastic packaging (C-Range), mono-material structures and post-consumer-recycled (PCR) content as lower-impact alternatives to conventional multi-layer plastic film — plus supporting services such as print, perforation and a film-selection calculator tool. In 2023 the company transitioned to an Employee Ownership Trust, giving staff a stake in the business and aiming to preserve independence and long-term strategic focus. Revenue comes from wholesale supply contracts with food manufacturers rather than consumer-facing sales. Membership of WRAP's UK Plastics/Packaging Pact reflects a voluntary industry commitment to recyclability and waste-reduction targets rather than a reuse/return-based packaging model.
