## Organisation Overview

Naturals by Watsons is a personal-care product line under A.S. Watson Group's Watsons retail chain, featured in the group's "Refill for a Better Tomorrow" sustainability initiative. Confirmed reporting documents a Naturals by Watsons refill station launched in Sunway Pyramid, Malaysia, rather than Hong Kong specifically.

## Business Model

Customers bring in-store bottles (packaged in 100% post-consumer recycled material) to refill at dedicated in-store stations, reducing new packaging production. Products are marketed as vegan and cruelty-free. This operates within Watsons' broader retail/pharmacy chain footprint across Asia.
