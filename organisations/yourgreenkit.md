## Organisation Overview

YourGreenKit is a Luxembourg reuse initiative offering "packaging-as-a-service": subscribers pay for unlimited annual use of YourGreenBox (reusable food takeaway/delivery containers) and YourGreenCup (reusable hot-drink cups), returning them to collection points or partner restaurants/cafés after use for cleaning and reuse. It is supported by national innovation agency LuxInnovation and listed as a World Economic Forum project.

## Business Model

Users subscribe to annual unlimited-use plans for reusable food boxes and/or coffee cups; after use, containers are dropped at dedicated collection points or handed back to partnering restaurants, cafés or delivery personnel, then professionally cleaned and recirculated to partner food-service outlets for the next customer. The organisation states its materials (natural-fibre-reinforced or recycled polymer compounds) can cut the carbon footprint of single-use PVC boxes by up to 57%, and that end-of-life containers are designed to be upcycled rather than landfilled or incinerated.
