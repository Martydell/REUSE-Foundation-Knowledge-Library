## Organisation Overview
Ocean Lanka, founded in 1996, is Sri Lanka's largest weft-knitted fabric manufacturer, supplying international apparel brands including Nike, GAP, Uniqlo and Marks & Spencer. It was the first Sri Lankan textile/apparel manufacturer to partner with bluesign® on sustainable production.

## Business Model
B2B textile manufacturer (knitting, dyeing, finishing, printing, yarn dyeing) supplying major global fashion and apparel brands. As part of its sustainability practices, finished fabric rolls are delivered to customers on reusable plastic tubes rather than single-use cores, reducing packaging waste in its B2B logistics chain.
