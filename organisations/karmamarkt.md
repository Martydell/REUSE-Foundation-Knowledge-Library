## Organisation Overview

KarmaMarkt is an independently owned, packaging-free retail store in Bruges, Belgium, run by shopkeeper/owner Isabelle. It sells organic and locally sourced bulk goods without packaging, letting customers bring their own containers or purchase reusable ones in-store. Distinctively, the business also incorporates a wellness space offering massages, sound baths, and workshops alongside its zero-waste retail operation, and maintains an online webshop in addition to its physical store.

## Business Model

KarmaMarkt combines conventional bulk/package-free grocery retail (organic and local dry goods, personal care, and household products sold by weight, with customers bringing their own containers or buying reusable ones on-site) with a secondary wellness-services offering (massages, sound baths, workshops) at the same location — an unusual hybrid model blending zero-waste retail with holistic wellness experiences. Revenue comes from both product sales (in-store and via online webshop) and wellness-service bookings.
