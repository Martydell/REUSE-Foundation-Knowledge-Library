## Organisation Overview

Refilled is a Sydney-based startup that installs "The Refiller," a smart drink dispenser pouring over 100 varieties of flavoured still or sparkling drinks on demand into reusable bottles or glasses, targeting offices, co-working spaces, gyms, and cafeterias to eliminate single-use plastic bottle waste.

## Business Model

Refilled sells or leases Refiller dispenser units to corporate clients (offices, co-working spaces), who offer staff on-demand flavoured drinks poured directly into reusable containers instead of stocking single-use bottles or cans. The company has raised AUD $1.3 million in seed funding and reports lower operating costs than vending machines due to the large drink variety stocked per unit.
