## Organisation Overview

RETURNiT is a Seoul-based platform operated by itgreen Co., Ltd. that lets customers order food through major Korean delivery apps (e.g., Coupang Eats, Naver Order) delivered in reusable stainless-steel containers instead of single-use packaging, with containers collected, sanitized, and recirculated.

## Business Model

RETURNiT partners with restaurants and merchants to adopt reusable stainless-steel containers, integrating with major food delivery platforms. The company operates cleaning centers that perform a seven-stage sanitization process on returned containers before redistribution, and also offers B2B corporate delivery solutions. Revenue is generated through merchant partnerships, platform integration, and container logistics/cleaning services.
