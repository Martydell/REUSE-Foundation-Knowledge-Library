## Organisation Overview

Alya Packaging is a privately held Istanbul manufacturer of custom-printed packaging — luxury paper bags, presentation and gift boxes, folding cartons and postal/e-commerce boxes — operating from an 8,000m² production facility and exporting to more than 40 countries. Alongside its core paper-packaging business, the company offers a line of reusable "eco-bags" made from cotton, non-woven polypropylene (PP) and woven PP, marketed as recyclable and reusable alternatives to single-use plastic carrier bags. It serves independent brands, SMEs, agencies and multinational companies, primarily across Europe and Asia, with in-house design and fast-turnaround manufacturing services.

## Business Model

Alya Packaging operates a B2B, made-to-order manufacturing model: customers (retail brands, patisseries, cosmetics and fashion companies, agencies) commission custom-printed packaging, and Alya designs, prototypes and produces it at its own Istanbul facility before exporting internationally. Its reusable eco-bag line — cotton tote bags and laminated non-woven PP bags — is one product category within a broader catalogue dominated by paper bags, rigid/folding boxes and cartons; the company positions these PP and cotton bags as durable, water-resistant, wipe-clean and capable of remaining in circulation "for months or years," in contrast to single-use paper or plastic carriers. The company does not appear to operate any take-back, deposit, or closed-loop collection system for the bags it manufactures — its role ends at the point of sale to the B2B customer, who then distributes the bags to their own end consumers. Revenue is generated through direct manufacturing contracts, with free graphic design and rapid prototyping offered as value-added services to win and retain B2B accounts, and FSC-certified materials available on request for customers with sustainability requirements.
