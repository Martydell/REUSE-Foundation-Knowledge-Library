## Organisation Overview
RaaS ("Reusable as a Service") is an Israeli company helping businesses, event venues and organisations replace single-use plastic tableware with reusable cups and utensils. It provides end-to-end logistics: supply, delivery, on-site return stations, collection, washing and re-circulation.

## Business Model
RaaS supplies reusable cups and utensils to events, conferences and organisations, ships them to the venue, sets up return/collection points with signage, then collects, washes and disinfects the items at a central "Wash Hub" before recirculating them — a pay-per-use reusable-tableware service rather than an outright product sale.
