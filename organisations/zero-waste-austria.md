## Organisation Overview
Zero Waste Austria is a Vienna-based non-profit association founded to amplify the zero-waste movement across Austria by strengthening networks and raising public awareness. It is a member of Zero Waste Europe and works nationally through talks, school workshops, and collaborations with waste management organisations and green start-ups.

## Business Model
As a non-profit association, Zero Waste Austria funds its work through membership, donations, and project/grant funding rather than commercial sales. It delivers school and corporate workshops (e.g., with STRABAG and PwC), trains volunteer workshop leaders, and collaborates with circular-economy start-ups and Zero Waste shops to advance waste-prevention policy and behaviour change at individual, business, and political levels.
