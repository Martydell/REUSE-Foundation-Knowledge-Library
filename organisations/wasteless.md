## Organisation Overview

Wasteless is a retail-technology company providing AI-driven dynamic pricing ("AI Markdowns") and shelf-life management tools (DateCheck+, Rotation Calendar, Grind Logs, Waste Tracking, Insights) to help grocery retailers reduce food waste by automatically adjusting prices as products approach expiry, deployed across 640+ stores including Makro (Poland) and Spanish retailers.

## Business Model

Wasteless licenses a SaaS platform to grocery retailers that integrates with in-store systems to dynamically reprice near-expiry food items, aiming to sell more inventory before spoilage. It reports claimed outcomes of up to 50% food-waste reduction, 20% revenue increase, and 9.1% margin improvement for retail clients, monetising via software/subscription fees to retail chains rather than product sales.
