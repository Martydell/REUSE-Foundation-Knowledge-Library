## Organisation Overview
Founded in December 2018 by Rocío Patrón and Jeannet Maggi, Terra Refill is a zero-waste bulk store offering 600+ products — flours, seeds, legumes, condiments, nuts, cleaning and personal-care items — encouraging customers to reuse their own containers, and has expanded to Guadalajara and Chihuahua.

## Business Model
B2C bulk refill retail: customers refill their own containers with bulk dry goods and household/personal-care products in-store or via online order, building a "zero waste community" alongside an educational mission about sustainable grocery shopping.
