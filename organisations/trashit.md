## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2018, TrashIt is a Pakistan-based youth-led social enterprise that collects organic waste via its "Trash on Wheelz" service and converts it into organic compost, operating facilities in Karachi, Hyderabad, Bahawalpur and Gharo, alongside an online eco-store selling reusable and zero-waste lifestyle products.

## Business Model
Dual-revenue social enterprise: B2B/B2C organic waste collection and composting (sold to farms and gardens, with an EU-funded Agri Waste Recovery Hub in development) plus a direct-to-consumer "eco-friendly switch" store selling reusable household products.

## Circular Economy Activities
- Waste Reduction
- Material Recovery
- Consumer Education

## Products & Services
Organic waste collection ("Trash on Wheelz"), compost/fertiliser products, and an online store for reusable/eco-friendly household goods.

## Customers
Home gardeners, small farms, commercial agriculture buyers, and eco-conscious retail consumers.

## Partnerships
WWF Pakistan (Sukkur field trials); EU-funded Agri Waste Recovery Hub in District Thatta.

## Measurable Impact
Composting capacity of ~20 tonnes/month across four sites; WWF Pakistan field trials reported up to a 23% increase in cotton output using TrashIt compost.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment
A credible, growing composting/organic-waste enterprise with independently-cited agricultural impact data and EU funding; its retail "eco store" adds a smaller reuse-products angle. ★★★ Medium
