## Organisation Overview
Guppyfriend GmbH, founded in 2016 in Berlin by Oliver Spies and Alexander Nolte, is a German-developed, patented reusable washing bag designed to trap microplastic fibres shed by synthetic clothing during machine washing, preventing them from entering wastewater and, ultimately, oceans and waterways. Independent testing by the Fraunhofer Institute UMSICHT found that clothes washed inside the bag shed on average 86% fewer microfibres. The product is manufactured in Europe — filter fabric sourced from Italy, other materials from Portugal, with sewing carried out in Portugal and Poland — and is sold direct-to-consumer online and through outdoor/sustainable retail partners.

## Business Model
Guppyfriend sells its washing bag and companion products (microplastic-filtering cloths, brushes, detergent, and sink/bucket water filters, plus professional solutions for commercial laundries and sports facilities) primarily direct-to-consumer via its own e-commerce site, supplemented by third-party retail and outdoor-brand partners. Revenue is generated through one-time product sales rather than a subscription or take-back service; the product's value proposition is entirely a consumer-education and pollution-prevention one — extending garment life through gentler washing while intercepting microfibre pollution at its source. The company has cultivated partnerships with sustainability-focused brands (notably Patagonia) and professional sports clubs (several German Bundesliga football clubs) who install Guppyfriend filtration in team laundry facilities and publicize captured-microplastic totals as an awareness campaign.

- Independent Fraunhofer Institute UMSICHT testing found 86% fewer microfibres shed from clothing washed inside the bag (dated study; specific publication year not confirmed in sources reviewed)

## Technology
Patented fine-mesh fabric bag construction designed to trap shed microfibres inside the bag rather than releasing them into washing-machine wastewater; separate inline water filters for sinks/buckets and commercial laundry settings.

## REUSE Foundation Assessment
Guppyfriend is a narrowly scoped but genuinely impactful reuse/waste-reduction product: it is a durable, reusable item (not single-use) that directly targets a well-documented pollution pathway (textile microfibre shedding), backed by independent scientific testing (Fraunhofer) rather than marketing claims alone. Its relevance to the REUSE Foundation's mandate is real but narrower than full reuse-system operators (no packaging return loop, deposit or refill mechanism) — it is best categorized as a consumer waste-reduction product with credible third-party validation.
