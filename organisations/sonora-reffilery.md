## Organisation Overview
Sonora Refillery is a zero-waste shop in Oceanside, California offering plastic-free bath, body and home products from small businesses, including customisable-scent oils and bulk spices. Customers bring their own containers or purchase glass bottles in-store to refill.

## Business Model
B2C retail refillery: bulk liquids, oils and spices sold by refill, sourced partly from organic farms and small producers; encourages BYOC to minimise packaging waste, positioning itself as a low-toxin, sustainable lifestyle shop rather than an online-only retailer.
