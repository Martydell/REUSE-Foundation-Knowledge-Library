## Organisation Overview

KidV (Kennisinstituut Duurzaam Verpakken) is a Dutch knowledge institute focused on helping organisations make informed, realistic decisions about sustainable packaging, including reusable-packaging collaboration and standards. In November 2025, the organisation rebranded to "Verpact," continuing the same mission under a new name — a notable status change not reflected in the seed tracker, which still lists it as "KidV."

## Business Model

KidV/Verpact operates as a non-profit knowledge institute rather than a commercial business: it provides tools (e.g. a "Recyclecheck" tool, a disposal guide called "Weggooiwijzer"), sector- and theme-specific research dossiers, a knowledge base, consultancy services, and webinars/educational content to help Dutch organisations navigate sustainable and reusable packaging decisions. It published updated Recyclecheck tool versions as recently as September 2025, shortly before its November 2025 rebrand.
