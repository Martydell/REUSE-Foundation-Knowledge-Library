## Organisation Overview

A Singapore/Malaysia-focused online retailer primarily known for bags, wallets and accessories, which also stocks a dedicated reusable-cup category — including KeepCup, Oasis, and Sttoke brand reusable coffee cups and insulated travel tumblers.

E-commerce retailer selling third-party branded reusable drinkware (KeepCup, Oasis Eco Cup, Sttoke ceramic cup) and other lifestyle accessories (bags, wallets) direct to consumers in Singapore and Malaysia; revenue from product resale margins rather than manufacturing its own reusable products.
