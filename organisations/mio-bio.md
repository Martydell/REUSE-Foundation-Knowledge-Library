## Organisation Overview

MioBio is a Bucharest-based retailer of organic and natural cosmetics, founded in 2009 by Ioana Paun and Alma Rusu. It operates both a physical boutique in central Bucharest and an e-commerce platform, curating over 50 organic personal-care brands from Romania and internationally. Note: the seed data's country attribution of "Denmark" is incorrect — the .ro domain and all sourced content confirm the company is Romanian, based and operating in Bucharest.

## Business Model

MioBio is a curated multi-brand retailer rather than a manufacturer, selling third-party and Romanian-made organic/natural cosmetics (skincare, makeup, hair care, body care, soaps, natural perfumes) through a boutique storefront and an online shop with in-store pickup and shipping options. Its sustainability angle is centred on packaging: the company states it packages orders in recycled and recyclable materials, and reuses good-condition boxes received from its own suppliers ("second journey" boxes) rather than buying new shipping materials. This is a modest, packaging-focused waste-reduction practice layered onto an otherwise conventional specialty-retail business model, rather than a dedicated refill or reuse-systems venture. The company positions itself as a "boutique chic" niche retailer emphasizing natural ingredients and small/independent cosmetic brands.
