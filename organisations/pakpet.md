## Organisation Overview
Pakpet is a Karachi-based manufacturer of PET plastic bottles, supplying brands such as Dalda, Soya Supreme and SC Johnson. Available sources describe a conventional B2B bottle-manufacturing business (minimum order 10,000 units) rather than a documented reuse/refill system.

## Business Model
Pakpet manufactures PET bottles at scale for consumer goods brands (food oils, household chemicals) on a wholesale B2B basis; no independently verifiable "reusable/refillable" product line, deposit-return scheme, or refill-station network was found.
