## Organisation Overview
Launched in April 2018 by Danielle Champagne, Mayur Singh and Agatha Lee, The Green Collective began as a platform bringing together sustainable brands and has grown into Singapore's largest eco-friendly collective and first B-Corp-certified platform for eco-friendly goods in Asia.

## Business Model
B2C multi-brand retail platform: aggregates 35+ eco-friendly brands (reusable cutlery, collapsible cups, cotton mesh bags, period cloth pads, etc.) under one storefront, providing smaller sustainable businesses collective retail reach and consumers a curated one-stop shop.
