## Organisation Overview

KazakhUpack is a Kazakhstani packaging company based in Shymkent, operating since 2003 (20+ years), specialising in polymer pails, buckets, and containers for the safe storage and transport of food, chemical, and paint/varnish products. The seed tracker's description of "reusable containers and bags" is only partially supported — verified sources describe the company primarily as a durable-container manufacturer/trader rather than a documented reuse-loop operator.

## Business Model

KazakhUpack manufactures and sells durable polymer buckets/pails and containers, marketed as reliable, functional, and sustainable packaging for B2B customers in the food, chemical, and paint/varnish industries. The durability of its containers gives them inherent reuse potential (customers can repurpose sturdy buckets after original use), but no documented company-run collection, return, or reuse-loop system was found — this differs from a formal deposit-return or reuse-as-a-service model.
