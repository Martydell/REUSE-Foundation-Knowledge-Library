## Organisation Overview

Biocírculo is a Bogotá-based mechanical recycling company that processes post-consumer PET, HDPE, PP and PS plastic waste into pelletised recycled raw material for packaging and plastic-product manufacturers, partially or wholly displacing virgin plastic. It is B Corp-certified (since 2022) and supplies recycled resin used in packaging, including by refill/reuse-oriented brands.

## Business Model

Biocírculo collects and mechanically recycles plastic waste (PET, HDPE, PP, PS) at its Bogotá facility, converting it into pelletised recycled raw material sold B2B to packaging and plastics manufacturers as a substitute for virgin resin, alongside related products such as posts, strips and pallets made from recovered caps and labels. Revenue comes from sale of recycled material rather than consumer-facing services.
