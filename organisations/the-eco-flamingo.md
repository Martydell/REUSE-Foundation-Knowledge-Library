## Organisation Overview
Co-founded in 2020 by Bethany Barbouti and Jackie MacCartie, Eco & The Flamingo is Chicago's first zero-waste general store, offering eco-friendly products, refills and a market café, with a second location added in Evanston in 2022.

## Business Model
B2C zero-waste general store and café: sells refillable household/personal-care products, sustainably-packaged farm-stand goods, bulk coffee/tea and deli items with refillable containers; also runs a Sustainability Center hosting workshops on waste reduction, bike repair and mending.
