## Organisation Overview

Home Ec. Market is a zero-waste refillery based in Presque Isle, Maine, selling household, personal-care, and family products described as toxin-free and plastic-free. It positions itself around local sourcing and reducing single-use packaging, operating both an e-commerce storefront and an active social presence (Instagram, Facebook). It appears to be a small independent retailer serving a regional/rural Maine market plus online customers, rather than a large national chain.

## Business Model

Home Ec. Market operates a refillery/retail model: customers can purchase household cleaning products, personal-care items, and related goods either package-free (bring-your-own-container refills, typical of the zero-waste refillery format) or in minimal/reusable packaging, alongside branded merchandise such as a recycled-cotton tote bag. The business maintains a dedicated "recycling" information page on its site, suggesting it also serves as a local collection or education point for hard-to-recycle materials. Revenue is generated through direct retail sales via its website and (likely) a physical storefront in Presque Isle, supplemented by social media-driven community engagement (e.g., seasonal sustainability campaigns promoted via Facebook). No franchise, wholesale, or B2B service model was identified.
