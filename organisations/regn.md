## Organisation Overview

Regn is a UK e-commerce wholesaler supplying retailers with sustainable, largely plastic-free products such as reusable sanitary items, bamboo toothbrushes, natural skincare, and household cleaning goods, positioning itself as a "go-to wholesale partner for eco products."

## Business Model

Regn runs a B2B wholesale platform requiring a minimum order of £150, offering competitive wholesale pricing, free UK postage on qualifying orders, and dispatch from domestic warehouses. Retailers register for account access to browse and order from the full catalogue, priced in GBP or EUR to also serve the Irish market. Regn states it supplies 1,000+ retailers.
