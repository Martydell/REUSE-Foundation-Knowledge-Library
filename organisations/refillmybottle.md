## Organisation Overview

RefillMyBottle is a community-driven global map/app launched in Indonesia in 2015, helping travellers and locals find nearby free or low-cost water refill stations to reduce single-use plastic bottle consumption; it has since expanded to Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, and other countries via local ambassadors.

## Business Model

RefillMyBottle operates a free public web map and mobile app listing refill stations, crowdsourced and maintained with support from local "Refill Ambassadors" in each country/region. It is funded through partnerships, sponsorships, and grants rather than user fees, with businesses and public fountains added to the map at no cost to encourage broader adoption of refill habits.
