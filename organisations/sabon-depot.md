## Organisation Overview

Sabon Depot JDG is a Philippine soap and cleaning-product retailer based in Bulacan, operating a "Green Formula" product line sold through a franchise and dealership network. Customers can access refillable soap and cleaning products at multiple franchised outlets across the greater Manila area and Baguio.

## Business Model

Sabon Depot JDG operates primarily as a franchise and dealership business: it recruits franchisees and dealers to open branded retail outlets selling its soap and household cleaning products, offering franchisee discounts and "no questions asked" easy-return policies to support network growth. Refill-based soap retail is delivered at the level of individual franchised branches rather than as a single centrally-operated store.
