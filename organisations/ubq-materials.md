## Organisation Overview
UBQ Materials is an Israeli cleantech company that converts unsorted household waste — organics, mixed plastics, paper, cardboard — into UBQ, a bio-based thermoplastic composite sold as pellets. The material substitutes for virgin plastic, wood, or concrete in manufacturing.

## Business Model
UBQ sells its thermoplastic pellets (UBQ material) as a drop-in feedstock to manufacturers across automotive, construction, packaging, and consumer goods industries. Waste is sourced from municipal collection, processed through a closed-loop, water-free conversion facility, and sold on B2B contracts. A second production plant is under construction in Bergen op Zoom, Netherlands.
