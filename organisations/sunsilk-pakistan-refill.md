## Organisation Overview
Sunsilk is a Unilever hair-care brand; as part of Unilever's global "Reuse. Refill. Rethink." programme, refill stations offering Sunsilk shampoo have been deployed at select retail locations (confirmed at a Carrefour in Karachi, Pakistan), alongside similar Unilever refill kiosks in other markets like the Philippines.

## Business Model
B2C in-store refill dispensers at partner retail locations: shoppers bring an empty bottle to a refill kiosk and receive a discount, reducing new packaging purchases; part of Unilever's broader multi-country refill/reuse strategy across several of its personal-care brands.
