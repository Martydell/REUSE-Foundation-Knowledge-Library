## Organisation Overview

ZeroWaste Switzerland is a non-profit association founded in 2015 to guide long-term waste reduction across the Swiss population. It raises awareness of waste-related issues, promotes behaviour change, and coordinates volunteer "Ambassador" networks across Swiss regions.

## Business Model

As a membership-based non-profit, ZeroWaste Switzerland is funded by member organisations (municipalities, companies, and NGOs) and supporters rather than commercial sales. It runs public events, maintains a national directory of zero-waste addresses (bulk stores, repair, second-hand, rental/loan services), and coordinates local volunteer "Ambassador" groups across Swiss regions to spread zero-waste practices. It is a member of the Zero Waste Europe network.
