## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2011 by Eliza, Kelly and Wuen, Touch The Toes was Singapore's (and reportedly Asia's) first sustainably-produced yoga gear retailer, selling eco-friendly mats, props and apparel made from organic cotton, recycled and upcycled materials, and bamboo blends; it added a community studio/workshop space in 2017.

## Business Model
B2C specialty retail: curates and sells sustainably-made yoga apparel, mats and props through retail outlets (e.g. The Green Collective @Funan, Isetan Scotts) and online, supplemented by studio workshops and green-living community events.
