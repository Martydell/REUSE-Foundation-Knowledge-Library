## Organisation Overview

RefillNZ is a grassroots, volunteer-led non-profit campaign founded in Wellington in October 2018 by Jill Ford, inspired by her work with City to Sea (founders of the UK Refill movement), that maps free tap-water refill sites nationwide via stickers and an app to cut single-use plastic bottle consumption.

## Business Model

RefillNZ operates entirely through volunteer effort as a small non-profit (registered as Refill Nation Limited). Cafés, businesses, and council venues join for free as "Refill stations," displaying a window sticker and appearing on the RefillNZ app/map so the public can find free tap water on the go; funding comes from grants, donations, and award recognition rather than commercial revenue.

## Products & Services

Free RefillNZ app/map of tap-water refill stations nationwide; window-sticker sign-up program for participating businesses and councils.

## Customers

General public across New Zealand seeking free water refills; cafés, businesses, and council venues as station hosts.

## Partnerships

Inspired by and modelled on City to Sea's UK Refill campaign; recipient of Keep NZ Beautiful Community Initiative Award (2019) and finalist, Sustainable Biz Network Awards.

## Measurable Impact

Reports more than 1,100 Refill sites listed on its map (per company website, not independently audited).
