## Organisation Overview

Recup UK is a British company supplying reusable polypropylene cups (pint, half-pint, wine) for events, stadiums, and venues, offering purchase, rental, deposit-scheme, or levy-based models to reduce single-use cup waste. Self-described as operating since 2016 with 25+ years of team experience in plastics and recycling.

## Business Model

Recup UK sells and rents branded and generic reusable polypropylene cups directly to event organisers, venues, and brands. Customers choose between purchasing cups outright, renting them for one-off events, or running a deposit/levy scheme where attendees pay a small returnable deposit per cup. Cups are UK-manufactured, shatterproof, reusable 200+ times, and recyclable at end of life; the company also handles washing/logistics for repeat use at recurring venues.
