## Organisation Overview

Minimal Waste Grocery is an Irish zero-waste delivery service, founded autumn 2019, supplying plastic-free pantry staples and household goods sourced from small Irish producers, organic where possible.

## Business Model

Delivers plastic-free pantry staples, produce, body/hair/skin/dental care, and household cleaning products to Irish consumers; holds full organic certification via the Irish Organic Association.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Waste Reduction
- Refill Systems

## Products & Services

Pantry staples (grains, legumes, nuts, seeds, spices, pasta, tea, coffee), dried fruit, organic produce, personal care, household cleaning products.
