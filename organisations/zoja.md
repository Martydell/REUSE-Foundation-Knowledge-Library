## Organisation Overview

Zoja Market is a boutique organic grocery store in the Palermo area of Buenos Aires, selling dried goods, pantry staples, and organic products, with a retail presence promoted primarily through Instagram and an online storefront.

## Business Model

Zoja Market operates a physical store at Avenida Santa Fe 4872, Buenos Aires, selling organic and bulk dried goods (grains, legumes, nuts) alongside a small online ordering channel. It markets primarily through Instagram (@zojamarket) rather than paid advertising, reflecting a small independent retail operation rather than a scaled or franchised business.
