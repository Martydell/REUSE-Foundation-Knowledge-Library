## Organisation Overview

Simula ("beginning" in Filipino) is a Metro Manila sustainable lifestyle store selling zero-waste and local products. It hosts a physical "Refillery" for household cleaners and personal care items, and carries over 70 partner local and micro/small enterprise brands.

## Business Model

Simula operates a bricks-and-mortar retail store plus online shop selling both packaged sustainable goods and refillable products (shampoo, dishwashing liquid, household cleaners) that it produces itself. Revenue comes from direct retail sales; it supports small Filipino producers by acting as a curated distribution channel and consumer education hub for low-waste living.

## Products & Services

In-store refill station for household cleaners, shampoo and dishwashing liquid, plus curated zero-waste retail products from 70+ local brands.

## Customers

Environmentally conscious individual consumers in Metro Manila and online shoppers across the Philippines.

## Partnerships
