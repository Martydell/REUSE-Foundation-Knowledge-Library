## Organisation Overview

Cosmogen is a French cosmetics-applicator and packaging specialist based in Paris, founded roughly 35 years ago (circa 1990-1991), specialising in refillable and reusable applicator systems for colour cosmetics — including its patented "Powder Pack" (an airtight, refillable compact with paper refills) and "Tense Cushion" dual-sided applicator for concealer, blush, and highlighter. The company reported record 2025 turnover (+16% growth), with the US accounting for 20% of sales, driven by product launches with major beauty brands including L'Oréal, Dior, and Estée Lauder.

## Business Model

Cosmogen operates as a B2B applicator and packaging manufacturer/supplier to major cosmetics brands rather than selling directly to consumers: its patented refillable systems (Powder Pack, Tense Cushion, and others) are licensed or supplied to beauty companies who incorporate them into their own branded products. The refill mechanism — reusable outer compact/applicator body with replaceable inner refills (e.g. paper-based Powder Pack refills) — reduces packaging waste per repurchase for the end consumer, while Cosmogen's own revenue comes from B2B supply contracts with brands such as L'Oréal, Dior, and Estée Lauder.
