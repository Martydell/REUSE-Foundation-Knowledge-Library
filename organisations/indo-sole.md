## Organisation Overview

"Indo Sole" (indosole.com.sg) is the Singapore-market storefront of Indosole, a footwear brand founded by American entrepreneur Kyle Parsons after discovering tire-sole sandal craftsmanship in Bali in 2004; the brand commercially launched in 2009. Indosole makes flip-flops, slides and sandals with soles made largely from upcycled waste tires (roughly 40% recycled tire content, blended with natural rubber and other materials), manufactured by artisans in Bali and sold globally — reportedly in nearly 400 stores across 27 countries, including this dedicated Singapore online/retail channel.

## Business Model

Indosole's model is industrial material reuse applied to consumer footwear manufacturing: discarded vehicle tires, which would otherwise go to landfill or incineration in Indonesia, are collected and mechanically reprocessed into sandal soles, mixed with natural rubber, recycled EVA foam, and other materials, with straps made from a low-impact "enviro fiber." The finished sandals are sold direct-to-consumer online and through retail partners worldwide. The Singapore storefront (indosole.com.sg) operates as a localized e-commerce/retail channel for this same global product line rather than as an independently designed or manufactured product. The company states a long-term goal of diverting 1 million tires from Indonesian landfills, and pairs its supply chain with fair employment for Indonesian artisans; it holds B Corporation certification.
