## Organisation Overview

Day by day is described as France's first bulk-grocery ("vrac") retail network, founded in 2011 by Didier Onraita and David Sutrat under the company My Retail Box, with the first store opening in Meudon in May 2013. It operates as a franchise model selling 750+ products without packaging, letting customers buy exactly the quantity they need using reusable or returnable containers. The network has grown to more than 70 stores across France, with locations also in Belgium and Luxembourg, and in 2023 acquired fellow bulk-retail brand Mademoiselle Vrac.

## Business Model

Day by day operates as a franchised bulk-grocery chain: individual franchisees run day by day-branded stores stocking 750+ products (dry goods, personal care, cleaning products, etc.) sold by weight/volume from dispensers, with customers bringing or purchasing reusable containers to fill. The franchisor (My Retail Box) provides the brand, supply chain, and store-format systems to franchisees, who operate individual locations. The company has pursued growth both organically (new franchise openings) and via acquisition (Mademoiselle Vrac, February 2023), and reportedly raised funding in 2024 to support the franchisor's continued expansion while aiming to "keep the start-up spirit."
