## Organisation Overview

Public Goods is a US e-commerce brand founded in 2017 by Morgan Hirsh and Michael Ferchak, selling sustainable personal-care, household, grocery, and supplement products under a minimalist, unbranded aesthetic. It operates on a low-cost annual membership model and emphasizes eco-friendly packaging such as sugarcane-based bottles.

## Business Model

Public Goods sells direct-to-consumer via a $59/year membership that unlocks access to its full product catalog at member pricing. Many household cleaning and personal-care products (e.g., shampoo) are designed to be refillable, with the company using recycled shipping materials and planting a tree for every order.
