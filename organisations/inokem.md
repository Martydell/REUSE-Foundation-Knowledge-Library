## Organisation Overview

Inokem is a Portuguese brand producing ultra-concentrated, biodegradable household cleaning and disinfecting products. Its central proposition is concentration: because its detergents are sold in a highly concentrated form, one Inokem package is marketed as replacing up to 100 packages of conventional ready-to-use detergent. The company sells both direct-to-consumer concentrate bottles and offers in-store refill of empty Inokem packaging at partner retail points, including notable retail alliances with Lidl Portugal and Auchan. Inokem has received Portuguese sustainability recognition, including wins in the National Sustainability Award's Circular Economy category.

## Business Model

Inokem's core model is concentration-driven waste reduction paired with a refill network: rather than shipping and selling mostly-water, ready-to-use cleaning products, Inokem sells small-format ultra-concentrate bottles that customers dilute themselves, and encourages customers to return/refill the same bottle at partner retail locations rather than buying new packaging each time. Revenue streams include direct consumer sales (online and retail), a refill service through partner retailers (confirmed alliances include Lidl Portugal and Auchan), reseller/franchise-style partnerships, and a separate B2B/institutional sales division. The company also runs an "Inokoins" loyalty/rewards mechanism tied to sustainable purchasing behavior. The claimed 90% reduction in plastic use and carbon footprint stems from the combination of concentration (fewer total packages needed) and the refill option (reusing the same container repeatedly).
