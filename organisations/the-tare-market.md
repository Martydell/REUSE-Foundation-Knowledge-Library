## Organisation Overview

Minneapolis-based zero-waste retailer billed as Minnesota's first such store, operating two locations offering 135+ bulk food items and 700+ package-free household/personal-care "swaps," with local delivery and curbside pickup.

## Business Model

Two-location bricks-and-mortar retail plus e-commerce/delivery; uses a standard tare-weight bulk system (customers bring or borrow containers, weighed empty then full, charged only for contents) for foods and household goods; same-day/next-day local delivery adds convenience-based revenue beyond in-store sales.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems (tare-weight bulk system)
- Reusable Packaging (customer-supplied or borrowed jars)
- Consumer Education
- Waste Reduction

## Products & Services

135+ bulk food items, 700+ zero-waste household/personal-care products (e.g., shampoo bars, compostable floss), local delivery and curbside pickup.
