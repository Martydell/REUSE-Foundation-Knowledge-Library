## Organisation Overview

Indigo River & Co. is a small, independently owned low-waste retail shop in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, selling handmade soaps, personal care items, and refillable household and cleaning products. Reported to be among the first (or one of very few) low-waste refill shops in the state, it operates both a physical storefront with an in-store refill station and an online store. The business is run by co-owners Rachel and Tim, with Tim producing the shop's in-house soap line.

## Business Model

Indigo River & Co. operates as a direct-to-consumer specialty retailer combining handmade goods with a refill model: customers can bring their own containers or purchase reusable ones in-store, then refill household cleaning products, personal care items and similar goods at the shop's refill station rather than buying new single-use packaged versions each time. Product lines include in-house handmade bar soaps, shower steamers, bath bombs, lip balm and sugar scrubs, alongside sourced low-waste goods like Swedish dishcloths, dryer balls, and toothpaste tablets (e.g., Huppy tablets). Revenue comes from in-store and online retail sales, with a wholesale channel also offered to other retailers. As a small independent shop, its scale is local/regional (Cedar Rapids and online shipping) rather than national or international.
