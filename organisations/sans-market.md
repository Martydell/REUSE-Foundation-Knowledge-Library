## Organisation Overview
Sans Market is a low-waste refillery and grocery store in downtown St. Petersburg, founded by Eniko Olah in 2018 to combat single-use plastic in the community. Customers refill bottles and containers with bulk household and personal-care products rather than buying new packaged goods.

## Business Model
B2C in-store refill model: customers bring their own jar/container, buy a reusable glass/aluminium container in-store, or take a free prewashed upcycled container; staff show how to tare the container before filling from a bulk bar of non-toxic products (detergent, soap, shampoo, deodorant, toothpaste, etc.).
