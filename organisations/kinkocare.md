## Organisation Overview

Kinkō (referred to in seed data as "KinkoCare") is a US natural-deodorant brand built around its proprietary "MICROBIOMIX" technology — a patent-pending blend of prebiotics and postbiotics designed to balance the underarm microbiome and prevent odour naturally rather than mask it. Products are aluminium-free, non-toxic, biodegradable, 100% vegan, and PETA-approved. The brand has been featured in publications including Allure, Yahoo Lifestyle, and BeautyStat, and offers a subscription purchasing model.

## Business Model

Kinkō sells its Balancing Deodorant (scented and unscented variants) direct-to-consumer, with a subscription option offering free shipping for repeat purchases. The core product differentiation is its microbiome-focused formulation technology rather than a documented packaging-reuse or refill mechanism — no evidence was found in sources accessed of a refillable container system, take-back programme, or similar reuse infrastructure, despite the seed tracker's "Eco-Friendly Household Products" classification implying a sustainability-packaging angle.
