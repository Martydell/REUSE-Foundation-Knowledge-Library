## Organisation Overview

Replenish is a curated general store at 208 Main St S, Newmarket, Ontario, selling sustainable, refillable, and consciously made goods across skincare, bath and body, home, kitchen, and lifestyle categories, with a strong focus on Canadian and Ontario-made products.

## Business Model

Replenish operates a storefront-plus-online retail model selling near-100% Canadian-made products, including a refillery component of Canadian-made natural products designed to reduce plastic waste. It offers free local delivery over CAD $60, free shipping over CAD $100, and plastic-free order packaging, generating revenue through curated product retail and refills.
