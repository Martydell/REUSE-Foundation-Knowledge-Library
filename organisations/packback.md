## Organisation Overview
PackBack is a Dutch reuse-technology company aiming to eliminate single-use food and beverage packaging waste. It supplies connected hardware and software — digital IDs, SmartBins, and a dashboard — to make returnable packaging simple and scalable for foodservice and retail operators.

## Business Model
PackBack sells/licenses a returnable-packaging technology stack: QR-coded reusable packaging, automated SmartBin return machines that let guests return items without staff assistance, and PackPulse, a dashboard giving operators real-time tracking and impact reporting. A consumer rewards layer (PackPerks) incentivizes returns via cashback or perks.
