## Organisation Overview

Kita Refill is the trading name of Cleancycle Enterprise, an unpackaged bulk refill store based in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia. The brand was rebranded from its original name "Bring YOUR Own Bottle" (BYOB) to "Kita Refill" — "Kita" meaning "we" in Malay — reflecting a collective, community-oriented sustainability positioning. Customers reuse their own containers to refill floor/kitchen cleaners, laundry detergents, specialty cleaning solutions, gardening products, and personal-care items at affordable prices.

## Business Model

Kita Refill operates a bring-your-own-container bulk refill model: customers bring reusable containers to refill household cleaning, laundry, gardening, and personal-care products, reducing single-use packaging while accessing competitively-priced goods. The company serves both individual households/bulk consumers and corporate clients, including SMEs and multinational corporations, via what are described as "corporate engagement programs" — suggesting a B2B sustainability-partnership dimension alongside its direct-to-consumer retail model.
