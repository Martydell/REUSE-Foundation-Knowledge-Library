## Organisation Overview

Yope is a Polish natural personal-care and home-cleaning brand founded in 2013 by Karolina and Paweł Kosowicz, exporting to over 40 markets with an annual turnover of roughly €20 million. Since 2021/2022 it has partnered with Polish refill-technology startup Swapp to offer coffee-machine-style refill stations for hand soap, shampoo, shower gel and dish soap in retail locations, letting customers refill reusable Yope/Swapp bottles at a discount versus buying new packaged product.

## Business Model

Yope's core business is manufacturing and selling packaged natural soaps, gels, lotions and home-care products through retail and export channels. Its refill activity is delivered via a partnership with Swapp, whose automated refill kiosks are installed in partner retail locations (e.g., Carrefour Bio Warsaw, Carrefour Wrocław, Auchan Piaseczno, Super-Pharm stores); customers buy a reusable Yope/Swapp bottle once, then return to refill it, paying around 15% less than for new packaged product since packaging cost is removed.

## Products & Services

Natural soaps, shower gels, shampoos, hand creams and home-cleaning products sold in conventional retail packaging, plus in-store refill of hand soap, shampoo, shower gel and dish soap via Swapp refill stations using reusable bottles.

## Customers

Retail consumers in Poland and 40+ export markets (conventional product line); refill-station customers at partner retail locations in Poland (Warsaw, Wrocław, Kraków, Piaseczno).

## Partnerships

Swapp (refill-station technology partner); retail hosts including Carrefour Polska (Carrefour Bio Warsaw, Carrefour Wrocław), Auchan (Piaseczno) and Super-Pharm (Targówek, Kraków).

## Measurable Impact

Swapp/Yope refill stations reported to have "eliminated several thousand disposable packages from the market" (Swapp company communications, not independently verified).

## REUSE Foundation Assessment

Yope is primarily a conventional packaged-goods cosmetics brand, but its refill-station partnership with Swapp is a concrete, multi-retailer refill deployment in Poland with real consumer discount incentives. Relevance is good as an example of an established consumer brand integrating third-party refill infrastructure, though refill remains a small part of its overall business.
