## Organisation Overview

Jentli (branded "JENTLI ZERO WASTE") is a small independent zero-waste refillery based in downtown Elgin, Illinois, describing itself as the Fox Valley region's dedicated refill shop. It offers a bring-your-own-container (BYOC) refill station for household and personal-care products alongside a curated selection of pre-packaged eco-friendly goods (e.g., safety razors, utensil sets, deodorant). The shop sells both in-store and online with nationwide shipping via its own website and third-party marketplaces such as Marketspread. Public information about the business is limited to its website, product listings, and directory/marketplace listings.

## Business Model

Jentli operates as a brick-and-mortar refillery combined with e-commerce. Customers can bring their own containers to refill items such as dish soap (sold in solid "block" form), shampoo/dry shampoo, and other household or personal-care staples, reducing single-use plastic bottle consumption. The store also retails durable, reusable alternatives to disposable items — safety razors, reusable utensil sets, and similar "swap" products — which customers purchase once and reuse repeatedly rather than purchasing single-use equivalents. Revenue comes from a mix of in-person retail/refill transactions at the Elgin storefront and online orders shipped nationwide. As a small independent retailer, Jentli does not appear to operate a formal B2B or franchise model; it functions as a local consumer-facing zero-waste shop typical of the broader independent refillery movement in the US.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Refill Systems — in-store BYOC (bring-your-own-container) refill station for household/personal-care liquids and solids
- Waste Reduction — sale of reusable alternatives (safety razors, reusable utensils) to displace single-use plastic products
