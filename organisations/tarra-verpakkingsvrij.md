## Organisation Overview
Founded in 2017 by former plastic-arts teacher Nora Lorré, inspired by Bea Johnson's packaging-free movement, Tarra is a packaging-free store in Dendermonde selling dry foods (nuts, pasta, rice, flour, herbs, teas, oils) and zero-waste care products via BYOC refill.

## Business Model
B2C bulk refill retail: customers bring jars, bottles and bags to fill with dry goods and household/personal-care products; the shop also connects with a local farmers' collective (Buurderij Dendermonde, also founded by Lorré) for combined packaging-free and local-produce shopping.

Bulk dry foods, oils, herbs and teas; zero-waste care products (washable pads, shampoo bars, natural cleaners, plastic-free toilet paper).
