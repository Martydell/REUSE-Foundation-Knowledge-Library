## Organisation Overview
NULL Zero Waste Factory operates refill dispensing stations for liquids (cleaning and personal-care products) across shopping malls in Malaysia's Klang Valley, launched in 2019. It encourages customers to bring their own containers, and reuse glass bottles where provided, avoiding new packaging.

## Business Model
Mall-based physical refill stations plus dealer distribution, generating revenue from per-fill liquid sales. A "Return & Reward" program gives customers incentives (e.g., complimentary hand sanitiser) for returning used bottles, reinforcing a closed-loop container model alongside the "Six Rs" (rethink, reduce, refuse, reuse, recycle, rot) ethos.
