## Organisation Overview
Event Cup is an e-commerce brand offering customisable reusable plastic and cardboard cups (10–60cl), pitchers and cup holders for weddings, birthdays, sports events, festivals and corporate functions. It operates as part of Imprim' & Com', a French printing/customisation business based in Orvault, France, with production described as "made and printed in Europe (France & Portugal)." The site offers online cup design tools, no minimum order quantity, and references a B Corp certification and corporate social responsibility ("Démarche R.S.E.") approach.

## Business Model
Event Cup's model is direct-to-consumer and B2B e-commerce: customers (individuals, companies, associations, sports clubs) design and order customised reusable cups online, printed to order in France or Portugal, for one-off or recurring events. Unlike dedicated event-cup logistics providers, the site's public content does not describe a full closed-loop rental/wash/collection service; a "Rental" section is referenced on the site but not described in detail on the pages fetched. The core revenue model therefore appears to be product sales (cup manufacturing and customisation) rather than a subscription or per-use logistics service, though some rental option may exist. Sister/affiliated brands mentioned include Mariage Perso and Naoglass, suggesting Imprim' & Com' operates a portfolio of related personalised-product e-commerce sites.
