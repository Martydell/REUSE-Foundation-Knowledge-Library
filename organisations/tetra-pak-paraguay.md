## Organisation Overview
Tetra Pak Paraguay is the local operation of the global beverage-carton manufacturer, running a carton-recycling facility processing an estimated 1,500 tons per year, positioned by the company as promoting a broader packaging reuse/recycling value chain in Paraguay.

## Business Model
Multinational packaging manufacturer's regional recycling operation: collects and recycles used beverage cartons (paper/plastic/aluminium composite) into raw materials for secondary products, supporting Tetra Pak's global sustainability commitments rather than operating a direct-to-consumer reuse system.
