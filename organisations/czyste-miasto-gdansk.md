## Organisation Overview

Czyste Miasto Gdańsk ("Clean City of Gdańsk," abbreviated CMG) is a public-facing brand and communications/education programme operated by the Department of Municipal Economy of Gdańsk City Hall, in Poland. Rather than being an independent company, CMG is the city's umbrella identity for municipal waste-management services, resident communication (collection schedules, sorting rules, a mobile app) and — under a "Circular Economy" and "Less Waste Idea" strand — public education encouraging residents to reduce consumption, repair goods, share items, and buy second-hand. It also runs or hosts several EU-funded circular-economy projects (e.g., WeeeWaste, Circular WEEEP, FoodLoops, LIFE Pom GOZilla.PL).

## Business Model

CMG is not a commercial business; it is a municipal government function funded through the City of Gdańsk (and, for specific initiatives, EU project grants) rather than through sales or service fees to residents. Its "Circular Economy" and "Less Waste Idea" content areas function as public-education campaigns: residents are directed toward practical actions (using reusable shopping bags and lunch boxes, drinking tap water, refusing single-use items, composting) and toward city-curated resources such as a map of local repair shops ("craftsmen"), a map of electro-waste collection containers, and "Gdańsk's Sorterownie" (sorting/reuse points). CMG also administers standard municipal waste services — collection schedules, sorting rules, an online waste-fee payment portal, and a resident mobile app — and participates in EU-funded circular-economy and food-waste projects as a public partner rather than a commercial operator.

## Circular Economy Activities

- Consumer Education (Less Waste Idea campaign built on a "6R" framework: Refuse, Reduce, Reuse, Repair, Recycle, Rot)
- Repair (curated "map of local repair shops" for shoes, clothing, leather goods, watches/clocks)
- Sharing (stated goal to "revive the culture of repairing and sharing" and increase use of second-hand items)
- Waste Reduction (core mission of the Less Waste Idea programme)
- Material Recovery (electro-waste collection container network; municipal sorting infrastructure "Sorterownie")

No evidence was found of CMG directly operating reusable packaging, deposit-return, or rental/logistics infrastructure itself — its role is coordination, education and signposting to third-party repair/reuse actors rather than delivering reuse services directly.
