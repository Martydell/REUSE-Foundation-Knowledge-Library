## Organisation Overview

My Green Pod is an independent UK media and marketplace platform founded in 2013 by Katie Hill and Jarvis Smith to promote ethical, sustainable living. It curates and sells vetted eco-friendly brands, including refillable beauty products, through its online marketplace and editorial content.

## Business Model

My Green Pod combines content publishing (articles, guides) with an e-commerce marketplace that lets vetted ethical and sustainable brands sell and ship directly to consumers. The marketplace was launched via crowdfunding to make ethical products more accessible and affordable. For every purchase, the company plants a tree via charity partner TreeSisters.
