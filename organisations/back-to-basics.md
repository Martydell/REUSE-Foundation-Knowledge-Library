## Organisation Overview

Back to Basics Ecostore was founded in 2019 in Quezon City by five environmental advocates concerned about the wasteful packaging generated by conventional supermarket shopping. Operating as a women-led social enterprise, BtB Ecostore runs a refillery grocery store offering refill stations for household essentials alongside eco-friendly products, including goods sourced from indigenous communities and women-led, community-based enterprises. The store won the Quezon City Green Awards 2024 for Excellence in Climate Action.

## Business Model

BtB Ecostore operates a physical refillery/zero-waste grocery store at its Quezon City location, generating revenue through in-store retail of refillable household and personal-care essentials plus eco-friendly packaged goods, with an explicit sourcing preference for products from indigenous communities and women-led, community-based enterprises. Customers bring or purchase reusable containers and refill items such as cleaning and personal-care liquids at in-store refill stations, reducing single-use packaging compared to buying pre-packaged equivalents. As a social enterprise, BtB Ecostore combines a commercial retail model with an explicit social mission — supporting small, community-based and women-led producers — and positions itself within the Philippines' broader "zero waste made easy" and plastic-reduction movement, referencing affiliation with the Break Free From Plastic network in its social messaging.
