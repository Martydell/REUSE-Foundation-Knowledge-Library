## Organisation Overview

Biobox Sàrl, based in Bouveret (Port-Valais), Switzerland, operates biobox.ch, a monthly subscription box service delivering 5-6 full-size natural/organic beauty, skincare and wellness products to subscribers in Switzerland and Liechtenstein. This is materially different from the seed-data description ("refillable container solutions for household products"): no evidence was found that Biobox Sàrl manufactures, sells, or supplies reusable/refillable containers, or that it operates in the household-products refill space at all. It appears to be a beauty-product discovery/subscription retailer, not a circular-economy or reuse company.

## Business Model

Biobox operates a direct-to-consumer subscription commerce model: customers pay a recurring fee and receive a curated monthly box containing several full-size, natural or organic beauty and wellness products (makeup, skincare, nutricosmetics, accessories), shipped by post around the 15th of each month with free delivery within Switzerland and Liechtenstein, and a satisfaction-guarantee return policy. No refill, reuse, deposit-return, or packaging take-back mechanism was identified in any source reviewed — the product itself (a curated box of retail beauty items) does not appear to be reuse-oriented.
