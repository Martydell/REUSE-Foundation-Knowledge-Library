## Organisation Overview

A Turkish clean-beauty/skincare brand founded in 2020, offering paraben/alcohol/dye-free skincare (serums, cleansers, toners, moisturisers, sunscreens) sold in over 30 countries, with "Refill Eco Packs" introduced as a sustainability-focused packaging option.

## Business Model

D2C and international-distribution skincare brand selling dermatologically-tested, vegan, cruelty-free formulations; generates revenue primarily through standard retail cosmetic sales, supplemented by Refill Eco Pack offerings that let customers replace product without a new full container, reducing packaging waste per repurchase.

Skincare products (serums, cleansers, toners, moisturisers, sunscreens) sold in standard and "Refill Eco Pack" refill formats.
