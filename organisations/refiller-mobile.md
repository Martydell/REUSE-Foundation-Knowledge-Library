## Organisation Overview

Refiller Mobile is Malaysia's first mobile zero-waste store, a refill van founded by former media producer Oh Sok Peng that travels to Klang Valley neighbourhoods so residents can refill household cleaning products, dried food, and personal-care items in their own containers.

## Business Model

Refiller Mobile sets up temporarily (about 15 minutes to establish, roughly four hours per stop) in neighbourhoods that invite the van, where residents bring empty containers to refill with dishwashing liquid, shampoo bars, toothpaste, dried food, and other low-waste products, priced by weight/volume (e.g., from RM4/kg). It also offers door-to-door delivery, particularly for elderly or homebound customers, for an added handling fee.
