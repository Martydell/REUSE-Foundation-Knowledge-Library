## Organisation Overview
Fill Refill Co is a small, independent, family-run UK manufacturer based at its own factory in Finedon, Northamptonshire. It produces refillable household cleaning, laundry, hand/body and hair-care products, sold in closed-loop returnable glass bottles and bulk returnable containers under its "Washtainer" system. The company was incorporated in November 2018 (Companies House no. 11683937) and launched its closed-loop rinse-and-return model in 2020. It holds B Corp certification, is a 1% for the Planet member, and is Leaping Bunny (cruelty-free) certified.

## Business Model
Fill Refill Co manufactures its own product range in-house and sells via direct-to-consumer channels alongside independent refill shops and hospitality/food-service accounts (e.g. Silo London). Its core circular mechanic is a rinse-and-return glass bottle system: customers return used glass bottles, which the company washes and refills (the "Washtainer" system) rather than using single-use packaging, alongside bulk returnable containers for B2B customers. It also partners with Milk & More for bottle-return and wash logistics, leveraging existing UK doorstep-delivery infrastructure rather than building fully separate reverse-logistics capacity from scratch. Revenue comes from product sales (DTC and B2B/bulk), with the closed-loop system positioned as a brand differentiator and cost-saver over time versus one-way packaging.
