## Organisation Overview
Tosca has led the reusable transport packaging and pooling industry for over 60 years, supplying durable plastic pallets, crates, beverage trays, dollies and e-commerce totes to supply chains across North America, Europe (including the UK) and Australia/New Zealand.

## Business Model
Managed-pooling model: Tosca supplies reusable plastic containers/pallets to manufacturers and retailers, retrieves them after use, sanitizes and inspects each unit, then redeploys the assets back into the supply chain — monetising via pooling/rental fees rather than one-off sales.
