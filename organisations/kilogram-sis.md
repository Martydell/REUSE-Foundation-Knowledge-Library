## Organisation Overview

Kilogram S.I.S. describes itself as the first "bio e-shop sustainable and zero waste" in Luxembourg — an online organic grocery store delivering products (beauty/personal care, beverages, bakery, fresh produce, dairy, wine, home/garden items, and more) in returnable containers that customers send back for refill cycles, with 24-48 hour delivery. The company claims to avoid up to 90% of packaging waste compared to conventional grocery delivery.

## Business Model

Kilogram S.I.S. operates an online grocery delivery model built around returnable containers: customers order organic, local, and additive-free groceries across a broad range of categories, receive them in reusable containers, and return the containers for cleaning and refilling on subsequent orders — a deposit/return-style logistics loop applied to e-commerce grocery delivery rather than a physical bulk store. The company also serves wholesale suppliers as a professional customer category, suggesting a B2B dimension alongside its consumer-facing e-shop.
